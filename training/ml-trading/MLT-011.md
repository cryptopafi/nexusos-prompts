# MLT-011 — Identify Statistical Arbitrage Opportunities

## Problema
Statistical arbitrage exploits temporary mispricings between related securities but requires rigorous statistical testing.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Historical price data for multiple securities
- Python with statsmodels

### Steps
1. Identify a universe of related securities (same sector, ETF components)
2. Test for cointegration between pairs using Engle-Granger or Johansen test
3. Calculate the spread and its statistical properties
4. Determine if the spread is mean-reverting (half-life < trading horizon)
5. Define entry and exit signals based on z-score of the spread
6. Backtest the strategy with transaction costs
7. Assess risk with VaR and stress testing

### Verification
- At least one cointegrated pair is identified
- Backtest shows positive risk-adjusted returns after costs
