# MLT-025 — Evaluate Results of a Pair Trade

## Problema
After executing pair trades, systematic evaluation determines whether the strategy is working as designed.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Completed pair trade records
- Historical spread data

### Steps
1. Collect trade log with entry/exit prices, dates, and P&L
2. Calculate per-trade metrics: return, holding period, max adverse excursion
3. Aggregate metrics: win rate, average win/loss ratio, Sharpe ratio
4. Analyze spread behavior during trades (did it mean-revert as expected?)
5. Identify losing trades and diagnose the cause
6. Assess whether the pair's cointegration relationship still holds
7. Decide whether to continue, adjust, or terminate the strategy

### Verification
- Trade evaluation report is complete with all metrics
- Clear recommendation on strategy continuation
