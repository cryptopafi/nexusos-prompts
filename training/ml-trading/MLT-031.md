# MLT-031 — Implement Q-Learning for Trading Strategy

## Problema
Q-learning enables learning optimal trading actions directly from market interaction without a model of market dynamics.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Understanding of Q-learning
- Historical trading data

### Steps
1. Define state-action space for trading (states x {buy, sell, hold})
2. Initialize Q-table with zeros or small random values
3. Implement epsilon-greedy exploration strategy
4. Implement Q-learning update: Q(s,a) += alpha * (r + gamma*max(Q(s',a')) - Q(s,a))
5. Train over multiple episodes of historical data
6. Decay epsilon over time to shift from exploration to exploitation
7. Evaluate the learned policy on unseen data

### Verification
- Q-values converge over training episodes
- Learned policy outperforms random trading on test data
