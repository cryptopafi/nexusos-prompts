# VC-042 — Measure Impact of AI-Built Apps on Productivity

## Problema
Without measuring impact, developers cannot justify continued investment in AI-built automation tools.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- AI-built app deployed (VC-039)
- Baseline metrics recorded

### Steps
1. Define baseline metrics before deploying the AI app
2. Track time spent on the task before and after automation
3. Measure error rates before and after
4. Calculate ROI (time saved vs development time invested)
5. Survey users for qualitative feedback
6. Create a report summarizing impact metrics

### Verification
- Impact report with quantified time savings
- ROI calculation is positive or improvement areas identified
