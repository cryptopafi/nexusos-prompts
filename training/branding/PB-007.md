# PB-007 — Create Consumer-Focused Content vs Product-Focused Content

## Problema
Brands default to talking about themselves (features, specs) when audiences engage more with content that solves their problems or reflects their identity.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Brand House document (PB-003)
- Content calendar or brief

### Steps
1. Audit your last 10 pieces of content: classify each as product-focused (about you) or consumer-focused (about them).
2. Calculate the ratio. Target is 70% consumer-focused, 30% product-focused.
3. For each product-focused piece, rewrite the headline from the consumer's perspective (what they gain, not what you offer).
4. Create a consumer-focused content framework: pain points, aspirations, lifestyle, education, entertainment.
5. Map each framework category to your brand pillars to ensure relevance.
6. Build 10 consumer-focused content ideas using the framework.
7. Produce and publish 3 pieces, then measure engagement vs. product-focused benchmarks.

### Verification
- Content audit completed with clear ratio
- At least 10 consumer-focused ideas documented
- 3 pieces published with engagement tracked
