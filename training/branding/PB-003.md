# PB-003 — Create a Brand House Document

## Problema
Without a single-page brand architecture document, teams interpret brand meaning inconsistently across campaigns and touchpoints.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Completed brand positioning work (PB-002)
- Brand mission/vision statements if they exist

### Steps
1. Define the brand foundation: mission, vision, and core values (bottom of the house).
2. Articulate the brand promise: the single most important commitment to customers (middle floor).
3. List 3-4 brand pillars: the proof points that support the promise (columns).
4. Write the brand personality: 3-5 adjectives that describe how the brand speaks and behaves.
5. Define the brand tagline or essence statement (roof of the house).
6. Add the target audience summary from your segmentation work.
7. Format everything into a single-page visual Brand House template.
8. Review with cross-functional stakeholders for alignment.

### Verification
- All six Brand House elements are completed
- Document fits on one page
- At least two stakeholders have reviewed and approved
