# PB-014 — Build a Creative Pitch Deck

## Problema
Pitch decks that dump data without a narrative arc fail to persuade. A structured creative pitch sells the idea before the budget.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Campaign concept (PB-013)
- Budget and timeline estimates

### Steps
1. Open with the insight: what consumer truth or market tension makes this campaign necessary? (1 slide)
2. Present the brand challenge: what needs to change in perception or behavior? (1 slide)
3. Reveal the creative concept: the big idea in one sentence, supported by a key visual. (1-2 slides)
4. Show concept executions: 3-4 mockups across channels (digital, social, OOH, print). (3-4 slides)
5. Present the media strategy: where and when the audience will see this. (1-2 slides)
6. Include the production plan and timeline. (1 slide)
7. Close with expected outcomes: KPIs, measurement plan, and budget summary. (1-2 slides)
8. Rehearse the pitch in under 10 minutes. Every slide must earn its place.

### Verification
- Deck is 10-15 slides maximum
- Narrative arc flows: insight to concept to execution to results
- Pitch rehearsed under 10 minutes
