# PB-006 — Name a Brand (Qualities of Effective Names)

## Problema
Choosing a brand name by gut feel leads to names that are hard to remember, impossible to trademark, or meaningless to the target audience.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Brand positioning and personality defined (PB-003)
- Domain and trademark search tools

### Steps
1. Generate 30+ name candidates using techniques: word mashups, metaphors, acronyms, foreign words, invented words.
2. Filter names against the 7 qualities: memorable, meaningful, likable, transferable, adaptable, protectable, and available.
3. Check domain availability for top 10 candidates (.com priority).
4. Run preliminary trademark search on USPTO/EUIPO for top 5 candidates.
5. Test top 3 names with 5-10 people from the target audience for recall and association.
6. Select the final name and document the rationale.

### Verification
- At least 30 candidates generated
- Final name passes all 7 quality filters
- Domain and trademark availability confirmed
