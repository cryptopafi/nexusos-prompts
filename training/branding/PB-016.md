# PB-016 — Map a Customer Journey

## Problema
Without a visual customer journey map, teams optimize isolated touchpoints while missing the end-to-end experience that shapes brand perception.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Customer persona or segment profile (PB-001)
- Touchpoint data from analytics and customer service

### Steps
1. Define the journey scope: which customer action from first awareness to post-purchase advocacy?
2. List all touchpoints the customer encounters at each stage: awareness, consideration, purchase, retention, advocacy.
3. For each touchpoint, document: channel, customer action, customer emotion, brand interaction, and pain points.
4. Map the emotional curve: where does the customer feel delighted, neutral, or frustrated?
5. Identify moments of truth: the 2-3 touchpoints that disproportionately impact overall satisfaction.
6. Document quick wins: pain points that can be fixed with minimal effort.
7. Create a visual journey map (linear or circular) that can be shared across teams.

### Verification
- All 5 journey stages mapped with touchpoints
- Emotional curve documented
- Moments of truth identified with improvement actions
