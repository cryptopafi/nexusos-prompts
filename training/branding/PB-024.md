# PB-024 — Conduct Ethical Review of Customer Data Usage

## Problema
Brands that use customer data without transparent ethical review risk regulatory penalties and irreversible trust damage.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Data inventory or data map
- Privacy policy and consent mechanisms

### Steps
1. Create a data inventory: what customer data do you collect, where is it stored, who has access, and how long is it retained?
2. For each data type, document the stated purpose and verify it matches actual usage.
3. Check consent mechanisms: is consent informed, specific, and freely given? Can customers easily withdraw?
4. Evaluate data sharing with third parties: who receives data, for what purpose, and under what agreements?
5. Apply the 'newspaper test': would you be comfortable if this data practice was reported on the front page?
6. Identify practices that are legal but ethically questionable: dark patterns, excessive tracking, opaque algorithms.
7. Create an action plan to fix any issues found and schedule annual ethical reviews.

### Verification
- Complete data inventory documented
- All consent mechanisms reviewed
- Action plan for any ethical issues with owners and deadlines
