# PB-009 — Produce a Branded Film Brief

## Problema
Branded content video projects fail when the creative brief is vague, leading to expensive productions that miss the brand message.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Brand House and brand story (PB-003, PB-008)
- Budget range and distribution plan

### Steps
1. Define the film objective: awareness, consideration, or conversion (pick one primary).
2. Write the core message in one sentence (what the viewer should feel/think/do after watching).
3. Describe the target audience using segment profiles (PB-001).
4. Choose the content format: documentary, narrative short, testimonial, or animation.
5. Write the creative concept in 2-3 sentences: the hook, the story, and the resolution.
6. Specify brand integration rules: logo placement, product visibility, tone of voice boundaries.
7. Define technical requirements: duration, aspect ratios, platform specs (YouTube, Instagram, TikTok).
8. List deliverables: hero cut, social cuts, behind-the-scenes, stills.
9. Set the production timeline: pre-production, shoot, post, delivery dates.
10. Include budget allocation: talent, crew, location, post-production, distribution.

### Verification
- Brief covers all 10 elements
- Core message is one sentence
- Budget and timeline are realistic and documented
