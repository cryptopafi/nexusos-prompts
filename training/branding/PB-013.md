# PB-013 — Convey USP in Advertising Concepts

## Problema
Ads that try to communicate multiple messages end up communicating none. A clear USP-driven concept cuts through noise and drives recall.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Brand benefit ladder (PB-004)
- Creative concepts from brainstorming (PB-012)

### Steps
1. Extract the single most important USP from your benefit ladder (PB-004).
2. Write the USP as a consumer-facing promise in one sentence.
3. Create 3 different advertising concepts that each dramatize the USP in a unique way.
4. For each concept, write: headline, visual description, body copy (2-3 sentences), and call to action.
5. Apply the 'cover test': cover the logo. Could this ad be from any competitor? If yes, strengthen the USP expression.
6. Test the strongest concept with 5 target consumers: can they state the USP after seeing the ad once?

### Verification
- USP stated in one clear sentence
- 3 concept executions created
- Cover test passed: ad is distinctly yours
