# PB-023 — Audit Outsourced CX for Brand Alignment

## Problema
Outsourced customer service and support teams often deliver experiences that contradict the brand promise because they lack brand training and quality standards.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Brand guidelines (PB-019)
- Outsourced partner contracts and SLAs

### Steps
1. Review current outsourced partner SLAs: do they include brand experience standards, not just efficiency metrics?
2. Mystery-shop the outsourced experience: 5 interactions scored against brand tone, helpfulness, and resolution quality.
3. Compare outsourced CX scores to in-house CX scores on the same dimensions.
4. Identify the gaps: where does the outsourced experience deviate from brand standards?
5. Create a brand training module for outsourced teams: brand story, tone of voice, do/don't examples.
6. Add brand experience metrics to the SLA: tone compliance, first-contact resolution, customer sentiment.
7. Schedule monthly quality audits with a rotating sample of interactions.

### Verification
- 5 mystery-shop interactions scored
- Gap analysis documented
- Brand training module delivered to partner
