# PB-022 — Use Value Proposition Canvas for CX Alignment

## Problema
Products and services drift from customer needs when teams lack a structured way to validate that what they offer matches what customers actually value.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Customer segment profile (PB-001)
- Product/service feature list

### Steps
1. Fill the Customer Profile side: customer jobs (tasks they need to do), pains (frustrations), and gains (desired outcomes).
2. Fill the Value Map side: products/services you offer, pain relievers, and gain creators.
3. Draw explicit lines connecting each pain reliever to a specific customer pain.
4. Draw explicit lines connecting each gain creator to a specific customer gain.
5. Identify mismatches: pain relievers with no corresponding pain, or customer pains with no reliever.
6. Prioritize the biggest mismatches for product or service improvement.
7. Validate the canvas with 5 customer interviews.

### Verification
- Both sides of the canvas fully populated
- All connections explicitly drawn
- Mismatches identified with priority actions
