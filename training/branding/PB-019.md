# PB-019 — Create Brand Guidelines That Drive CX Consistency

## Problema
Brand guidelines that sit in a PDF and are never referenced fail to drive consistency. Living guidelines must be accessible, practical, and enforced.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Brand House (PB-003)
- Visual identity assets
- Tone of voice examples

### Steps
1. Define the guideline structure: brand story, visual identity, tone of voice, photography, digital, print, social, partnerships.
2. For each section, include: the rule, the rationale, a do example, and a don't example.
3. Create visual identity specs: logo usage, color codes (HEX/RGB/CMYK), typography, spacing, minimum sizes.
4. Write tone of voice guidelines with 5 real examples of on-brand vs. off-brand copy.
5. Include channel-specific guidance: social media, email, website, customer service scripts.
6. Host guidelines in an accessible format: shared drive, Notion page, or brand portal (not a static PDF).
7. Assign a brand guardian responsible for guideline updates and compliance checks.

### Verification
- All major sections completed with do/don't examples
- Guidelines hosted in a live, accessible format
- Brand guardian assigned
