# PB-021 — Map Employee Experience to Improve CX

## Problema
Poor employee experience directly degrades customer experience. Disengaged employees cannot deliver the brand promise consistently.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Brand values (PB-003)
- Employee touchpoint access (HR, internal comms)

### Steps
1. Map the employee journey: recruitment, onboarding, daily work, development, exit.
2. For each stage, document: what the employee experiences, how they feel, and where friction exists.
3. Identify the connection points where employee experience directly impacts customer experience (e.g., support agents, sales reps).
4. Score each connection point: is the employee equipped, motivated, and empowered to deliver the brand promise? (1-5 each)
5. Identify the top 3 employee experience improvements that would most impact CX.
6. Create action plans with HR and operations owners.
7. Establish an employee feedback loop (quarterly pulse survey) linked to CX metrics.

### Verification
- Employee journey mapped across all 5 stages
- EX-to-CX connection points scored
- Top 3 improvements have action plans and owners
