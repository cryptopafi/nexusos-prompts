# PB-018 — Identify and Fix Branding Strategy Orphans

## Problema
Strategy orphans are brand activities, campaigns, or touchpoints that exist without a clear connection to the brand strategy, wasting resources and diluting brand equity.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Brand House document (PB-003)
- List of all active brand activities and campaigns

### Steps
1. Compile a complete list of all active brand activities: campaigns, sponsorships, content series, partnerships, events.
2. For each activity, trace the connection back to a specific brand pillar or strategic objective.
3. Flag any activity that cannot be clearly connected: these are strategy orphans.
4. Classify orphans: valuable (keep and connect), redundant (eliminate), or legacy (sunset gracefully).
5. For valuable orphans, reframe the brief to explicitly connect to a brand pillar.
6. For redundant orphans, calculate the resource savings from elimination.
7. Update the brand activity registry and establish a review gate for new activities.

### Verification
- All active activities catalogued
- Orphans identified and classified
- Resource savings calculated for eliminated orphans
