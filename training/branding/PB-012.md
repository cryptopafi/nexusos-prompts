# PB-012 — Brainstorm Creative Advertising Concepts (Word Association)

## Problema
Creative brainstorming sessions produce safe, predictable ideas because teams lack structured divergent thinking techniques.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Brand brief or campaign objective
- Team of 2-5 people or solo with timer

### Steps
1. Write the core brand message or campaign objective on a whiteboard.
2. Set a 3-minute timer. Each person writes 20 words they associate with the message (no filtering).
3. Collect all words and group them into unexpected clusters (force connections between unrelated words).
4. For each cluster, generate 3 'What if...' concept statements.
5. Select the 5 most surprising or provocative concepts.
6. For each concept, sketch a rough visual and write a one-line headline.
7. Test concepts against brand guidelines: does it fit the personality while being surprising?
8. Shortlist 2-3 concepts for development.

### Verification
- At least 60 association words generated
- 5+ unexpected concepts documented
- 2-3 shortlisted concepts pass brand fit check
