# PB-004 — Build a Benefit Ladder (Feature-to-Emotion)

## Problema
Marketing teams default to listing features instead of connecting product attributes to the emotional benefits that actually drive purchase decisions.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Product spec sheet or feature list
- Customer insight data

### Steps
1. List all product/service features in a column (the base of the ladder).
2. For each feature, ask 'So what?' to identify the functional benefit it delivers.
3. For each functional benefit, ask 'Why does that matter?' to find the emotional benefit.
4. For each emotional benefit, ask 'What does that say about me?' to reach the self-expressive benefit (top of ladder).
5. Select the 2-3 strongest ladders where your brand has genuine differentiation.
6. Test the top-of-ladder insights against customer interviews or survey data.
7. Document the final benefit ladders as a reference for creative briefs.

### Verification
- At least 3 complete ladders from feature to emotion
- Top benefits validated against real customer language
- Document shared with creative team
