# PB-005 — Evaluate Brands Using the Brand Asset Valuator

## Problema
Companies lack a structured framework to diagnose brand health and identify whether their brand is growing, leading, eroding, or declining.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Brand tracking data or consumer survey results
- Competitor benchmark data

### Steps
1. Measure brand Differentiation: how distinct is the brand from competitors? (survey or expert rating 1-10).
2. Measure brand Relevance: how appropriate is the brand for the target audience? (1-10).
3. Calculate Brand Strength = Differentiation x Relevance.
4. Measure brand Esteem: how well-regarded is the brand? (1-10).
5. Measure brand Knowledge: how well do consumers understand what the brand stands for? (1-10).
6. Calculate Brand Stature = Esteem x Knowledge.
7. Plot on the BAV Power Grid (Strength on Y-axis, Stature on X-axis) to identify brand quadrant.
8. Diagnose the brand state: New/Niche (high diff, low knowledge), Leader (high both), Eroding (low strength, high stature), or Declining (low both).

### Verification
- All four pillars scored with data source cited
- Brand plotted on BAV Power Grid
- Diagnosis includes specific action recommendations
