# PB-010 — Build Community Through Brand Entertainment

## Problema
Brands that only push promotional content lose audience attention. Entertainment-first content builds organic community and long-term brand affinity.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Brand personality defined (PB-003)
- Content publishing capability

### Steps
1. Identify 3 entertainment formats your audience already consumes (podcasts, short films, games, quizzes, memes).
2. Map each format to a brand pillar to ensure relevance without being promotional.
3. Create a pilot piece of brand entertainment content (minimal production value is fine).
4. Distribute through organic channels and measure engagement rate vs. promotional content.
5. Build a community feedback loop: comments, polls, user submissions.
6. Scale the best-performing format into a recurring series (weekly or biweekly).
7. Track community growth metrics: repeat viewers, shares, user-generated responses.

### Verification
- At least one entertainment pilot published
- Engagement rate compared to promotional baseline
- Community feedback mechanism active
