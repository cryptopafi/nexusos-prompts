# PB-011 — Create a Visual Brand Story Through Photography

## Problema
Brands rely on generic stock photography that fails to communicate brand personality or create visual distinctiveness.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Brand personality and visual identity (PB-003)
- Camera or smartphone with good camera

### Steps
1. Define 3-5 visual themes that reflect your brand personality (e.g., warmth, precision, adventure, simplicity).
2. Create a mood board with 15-20 reference images that capture the desired aesthetic.
3. Define photography guidelines: color palette, lighting style, composition rules, subject matter.
4. Shoot or commission 10 photos following the guidelines.
5. Edit photos to match brand color grading and style consistency.
6. Test photos in context: website headers, social posts, ads. Do they feel distinctly yours?
7. Document the visual style guide for ongoing photography.

### Verification
- Mood board created with 15+ reference images
- 10 on-brand photos produced
- Visual style guide documented
