# PB-008 — Write a Brand Story Using Universal Narratives

## Problema
Brand stories that lack narrative structure feel like corporate copy. Universal story archetypes create emotional resonance and memorability.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Brand House document (PB-003)
- Knowledge of brand origin and customer transformation

### Steps
1. Choose a universal narrative archetype: The Quest, Rags to Riches, Overcoming the Monster, Voyage and Return, or Rebirth.
2. Identify the hero of your story (typically the customer, not the brand).
3. Define the conflict or challenge the hero faces.
4. Position your brand as the guide or tool that helps the hero overcome the challenge.
5. Describe the transformation: what life looks like after the hero succeeds.
6. Write the story in 250-500 words using conversational, emotional language.
7. Test the story with 3 people: can they retell it from memory after one reading?

### Verification
- Story follows a clear narrative arc
- Customer is the hero, brand is the guide
- At least 2 of 3 test readers can retell the core story
