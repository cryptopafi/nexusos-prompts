# PB-017 — Audit Brand Touchpoints Across the Customer Journey

## Problema
Brand consistency breaks down when individual teams own touchpoints without a systematic audit framework to catch misalignment.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Customer journey map (PB-016)
- Brand guidelines (PB-019)

### Steps
1. Export the full touchpoint list from the customer journey map.
2. For each touchpoint, score brand consistency on: visual identity (1-5), tone of voice (1-5), message alignment (1-5), and experience quality (1-5).
3. Calculate an average Brand Consistency Score per touchpoint and per journey stage.
4. Flag any touchpoint scoring below 3 in any dimension as a priority fix.
5. Identify touchpoint owners and check if they have access to current brand guidelines.
6. Create an action plan: what needs to change, who owns it, and by when.
7. Schedule quarterly re-audits to track improvement.

### Verification
- All touchpoints scored on 4 dimensions
- Priority fixes identified with owners
- Quarterly audit cadence established
