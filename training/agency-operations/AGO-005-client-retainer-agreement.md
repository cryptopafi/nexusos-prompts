# Client Retainer Agreement Structure

## Obiectiv
Construiește un acord de retainer complet care protejează cash flow-ul agenției, definește cu precizie ce e livrat și ce nu, include mecanisme de escalare/de-escalare a serviciilor, și stimulează commitment pe termen lung prin discount structures — menținând gross margin >55% pe retainere și reducând churn-ul contractual la sub 3% lunar.

## Pași

### Pas 1: Stabilește structura retainer-ului și modelul de pricing
- **Definire livrare lunară**: activități fixe per lună (ex: 8 articole SEO + 1 raport + 2 strategy calls + X ore de suport ad-hoc), NU promite rezultate garantate în contract
- **Model ore incluse vs deliverable-based**:
  - **Deliverable-based (recomandat)**: "Primești X articole, Y backlinks, Z rapoarte." Client știe exact ce primește. Marjă mai predictibilă dacă ai SOP-uri.
  - **Hours-based**: "Inclus X ore/lună." Flexibil dar periculos — clientul poate cere orice în orele incluse. Folosit pentru consultanță, nu pentru execuție.
  - **Hybrid (best practice)**: deliverables fixe + X ore ad-hoc incluse (ex: 5 ore/lună pentru cereri extra). Orele neutilizate NU se reportează (sau maxim 50% în luna următoare cu cap de 2 luni).
- **Tier structure** (3 pachete standard):
  - **Starter** ($2,000-$3,500/mo): 1 serviciu principal, deliverables basic, raport lunar, 1 call/lună, 3 ore ad-hoc. Ideal: clienți noi, test-drive.
  - **Growth** ($4,000-$7,500/mo): 1-2 servicii, deliverables extended, raport lunar + weekly updates, 2 calls/lună, 5 ore ad-hoc, dedicated account manager. Ideal: clienți validați cu buget de creștere.
  - **Scale** ($8,000-$15,000/mo): multi-channel, deliverables premium, dashboard live, weekly call, 10 ore ad-hoc, senior strategist, quarterly strategy session. Ideal: clienți enterprise sau high-growth.
- **Setup fee**: prima lună dedicată audit + strategy + setup = $1,500-$5,000 one-time (non-negotiable — acoperă COGS de setup care nu e recurent)
- **Minimum commitment**: 3 luni (entry-level), 6 luni (standard), 12 luni cu 10-15% discount (premium). Nu accepta month-to-month fără premium de 15-20% pe preț.
- Calculează prețul pornind de la COGS + target margin: `Preț = (ore estimate × cost intern/oră + overhead allocation) / (1 - target_margin)`. Target margin: 55-65%.

### Pas 2: Redactează secțiunile core ale acordului de retainer

**Secțiunea 1 — Services Included**
```
During each calendar month of the Term, Agency shall provide:

DELIVERABLES:
• [X] SEO-optimized blog articles (800-1,200 words each), published to
  Client's CMS
• [X] high-authority backlinks (minimum DA30) acquired through white-hat
  outreach
• Monthly technical SEO audit and implementation of critical fixes
• [X] social media posts (designed + copywritten + scheduled)
• Monthly performance report with insights and recommendations (PDF +
  live dashboard access)

SUPPORT:
• [X] hours of ad-hoc support per month for requests within the service
  scope (unused hours do not roll over)
• [X] strategy call(s) per month ([X] minutes each) via Zoom

EXCLUDED (require Change Order per Section 7):
• Community management (responding to comments, DMs, reviews)
• Paid advertising management (Google Ads, Meta Ads)
• Video production or editing
• Website design or development changes beyond minor text/image updates
• Content in languages other than [English/Romanian]
```

**Secțiunea 2 — Deliverables Schedule**
```
| Deliverable              | Frequency | Delivery By                    |
|--------------------------|-----------|--------------------------------|
| Blog articles            | Monthly   | 15th of each month             |
| Backlink report          | Monthly   | Last business day of month     |
| Social media content     | Monthly   | 5th of each month (for approval)|
| Performance report       | Monthly   | 3rd business day of next month |
| Strategy call            | Monthly   | 3rd week of each month         |

Reports delivered via: [Looker Studio live dashboard + PDF summary email]
```

**Secțiunea 3 — Fees and Payment**
```
Monthly Retainer Fee: $[X,XXX] + applicable taxes
Setup Fee (one-time): $[X,XXX], due at signing

Payment Terms:
• Monthly fee invoiced on the 1st of each month, due within [5] business
  days of invoice date
• Payment method: [Stripe auto-charge / ACH / wire transfer]
• Preferred: automatic payment via Stripe (Client provides payment method
  at onboarding)

Late Payment Policy:
• 7 days past due: automated reminder email
• 15 days past due: phone call from account manager + written notice
  that deliverables will be paused
• 20 days past due: all work paused until payment is current
• 30 days past due: 2% monthly interest applied to outstanding balance;
  Agency may terminate per Section 6
• Paused work due to non-payment may require up to [5] business days
  to resume

Note: Ad spend, third-party software licenses, and stock photo costs
are NOT included in the retainer fee and are billed separately to Client
or paid directly by Client.
```

**Secțiunea 4 — Term, Renewal, and Commitment Discounts**
```
Initial Term: [X] months from Effective Date
Renewal: Auto-renews for successive [1-month / 3-month] periods unless
  either party provides [30] days written notice before the end of the
  current period.

Commitment Discounts (applied to monthly fee):
• 3-month commitment: standard rate ($[X]/mo)
• 6-month commitment: 5% discount ($[X]/mo)
• 12-month commitment: 10% discount ($[X]/mo)
• 24-month commitment: 15% discount ($[X]/mo) [enterprise clients only]

Annual Rate Adjustment: Agency reserves the right to adjust the monthly
fee annually (at the anniversary of the Effective Date) with 60 days
written notice. Adjustments reflect market conditions, expanded
capabilities, and team expertise. Clients on active annual commitments
are protected from mid-term increases.
```

**Secțiunea 5 — Client Responsibilities**
```
Client shall:
• Provide admin access to all required platforms within [5] business days
  of signing (GA4, GSC, CMS, social accounts, ad platforms)
• Designate one (1) Point of Contact (POC) authorized to approve
  deliverables and make binding decisions
• Respond to approval requests within [3] business days. Deliverables
  not actioned within [5] business days are deemed approved.
• Maintain active hosting, domain, and necessary software subscriptions
• Communicate changes to business strategy, promotions, or events that
  may impact marketing activities with minimum [7] days notice

Impact of Client Delays: Delays in providing access, approvals, or
requested information may impact deliverable timelines. Agency will
notify Client of any impact in writing within 2 business days.
```

**Secțiunea 6 — Termination**
```
Termination by Client:
• [30] days written notice to Agency
• All fees for the current period are due in full
• Client receives all work completed through termination date

Early Termination (before end of initial commitment period):
• Client pays an early termination fee equal to [50]% of remaining
  monthly fees for the balance of the initial term
• Example: 12-month commitment at $5,000/mo, terminated at month 4 →
  early termination fee = 50% × 8 months × $5,000 = $20,000

Termination by Agency:
• [15] days written notice if Client payment is overdue by 30+ days
• [30] days notice for termination without cause
• Agency will provide transition support and all Client data/assets

Offboarding: Upon termination, Agency shall provide within [10] business
days: all login credentials transferred back to Client, final
performance report, export of all Client data from Agency systems,
copies of all creative assets produced during the engagement.
```

**Secțiunea 7 — Change Orders and Scope Modifications**
```
Requests for services beyond Section 1 are managed via Change Orders (CO).
Process: (See AGO-004 for full CO workflow)
• Client submits request in writing
• Agency provides CO with scope, cost, and timeline within 2 business days
• No additional work begins without written CO approval

Standing Change Order option: For Clients who regularly need ad-hoc
services, a monthly "flex budget" of $[X] can be added to the retainer
for pre-approved out-of-scope work up to [X] hours/month.
```

### Pas 3: Adaugă clauze de protecție avansate
- **Non-solicitation clause**: "Client shall not directly or indirectly solicit, recruit, or hire any Agency employee or contractor during the Term and for twelve (12) months following termination. Violation shall require payment of a placement fee equal to 50% of the individual's annual compensation."
- **Limitation of liability**: "Agency's total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid by Client in the three (3) months immediately preceding the claim. Agency shall not be liable for indirect, incidental, consequential, or punitive damages."
- **Force majeure**: "Neither party shall be liable for delays or failures due to circumstances beyond reasonable control, including but not limited to: natural disasters, pandemics, government actions, internet/platform outages, or algorithm changes by third-party platforms (Google, Meta, etc.)."
- **Dispute resolution**: "Any dispute arising from this Agreement shall first be addressed through good-faith mediation. If mediation fails within 30 days, disputes shall be resolved through binding arbitration in [City, State/Country] under [applicable arbitration rules]."
- **Platform algorithm disclaimer**: "Agency does not guarantee specific rankings, traffic levels, or conversion rates. Results are influenced by factors outside Agency's control including search engine algorithm changes, platform policy updates, competitive landscape changes, and market conditions. Agency commits to applying industry best practices and transparent reporting of actual results."
- **Data processing**: dacă operezi în EU/GDPR: "Agency processes Client data as a Data Processor under GDPR. A Data Processing Agreement (DPA) is attached as Appendix A."

### Pas 4: Implementează procesul de semnare și activare
- Trimite acordul prin PandaDoc sau Proposify — nu email cu PDF nesemnat
- Include:
  - Cover page cu logo agency + client (profesional din prima secundă)
  - Executive summary: 1 paragraf cu ce e inclus și prețul total (decision maker citește doar asta)
  - Full agreement cu toate secțiunile
  - Appendix A: Onboarding Questionnaire link
  - Appendix B: DPA (dacă GDPR applies)
- La semnare: automatics trigger —
  1. Invoice for setup fee + month 1 generated in QuickBooks/Stripe
  2. Welcome email triggered (AGO-003)
  3. CRM status: "Closed Won"
  4. Slack notification: #new-clients channel
- La plata confirmată: client entry in project management + Kickoff Call scheduled
- **Payment setup**: prefer auto-charge via Stripe la semnare. Scripting: "To keep things seamless, we'll set up automatic monthly billing through our secure payment portal. You'll receive a receipt each month."

### Pas 5: Gestionează reînnoirile proactiv (nu pasiv)
- **Calendar trigger**: 60 zile înainte de expirare → CRM reminder la account manager
- **La 45 zile**: trimite Renewal Package email cu:
  - Retrospectivă de rezultate (top 5 achievements din perioada curentă cu cifre)
  - ROI estimation: "Investiția voastră de $X a generat Y leads / Z revenue / W trafic" (cuantifică valoarea)
  - Propunere pentru perioada următoare: same package renewed SAU upgrade recomandat cu justificare
  - Commitment discount reminder: "Dacă semnezi 12 luni acum, blochezi prețul curent și primești 10% discount"
- **Upsell natural**: reînnoirea este cel mai bun moment de upgrade. "Pe baza rezultatelor din Q1-Q2, recomandăm adăugarea [serviciu] care ar amplifica [metric]. Pachetul Growth la $[X]/mo include asta."
- **Dacă clientul menționează plecare**: nu oferi discount imediat. Organizează un "Retention Call" (AGO-014) — află cauza reală, oferă soluție specifică.
- **Reînnoire tracking**: target renewal rate 85-90%. Sub 80% = problemă sistemică (calitate, comunicare, sau pricing).

### Pas 6: Creează tier-uri de retainer cu upgrade path clar
- **Service catalog** (internal + client-facing):
  | Tier | Monthly Fee | Services | Ad-hoc Hours | Calls | Reporting |
  |------|------------|----------|-------------|-------|-----------|
  | Starter | $2,000-$3,500 | 1 service | 3h | 1/mo | Monthly PDF |
  | Growth | $4,000-$7,500 | 1-2 services | 5h | 2/mo | Monthly + weekly updates |
  | Scale | $8,000-$15,000 | Multi-channel | 10h | Weekly | Live dashboard + monthly |
  | Enterprise | $15,000+ | Full service | 20h | Weekly | Custom cadence |
- **Upgrade triggers**: QBR results positive → recommend tier upgrade. CO frequency high → integrate into higher tier. Client mentions growth goals → propose Scale tier.
- Păstrează 2-3 versiuni de template per tier: Starter template, Growth template, Scale template. Nu recrea de la zero per client.
- **De-escalation option**: dacă un client vrea să reducă bugetul dar nu să plece, oferă tier downgrade cu scope redus. Păstrezi clientul, păstrezi marja. Mai bine $2,500/mo decât $0/mo.

### Pas 7: Documentează și standardizează
- Menține toate template-urile în PandaDoc/Proposify cu versioning (v2.1, v2.2)
- La fiecare dispută sau situație neacoperită: actualizează template-ul imediat (nu "la 6 luni")
- Training echipă: fiecare account manager trebuie să cunoască fiecare clauză — nu doar sales
- **Annual legal review**: $500-$1,500 investiție anuală la avocat local pentru actualizare template pe baza legislației curente. ROI: previne o singură dispută care costă $5K-$50K+.
- **Benchmark tracking**: gross margin per tier | churn rate per tier | ARPC per tier | most common CO requests per tier → informează ajustări de scope și preț

## Verificare
- [ ] Template retainer cu toate secțiunile redactat, versionat, aprobat de avocat
- [ ] 3-4 tiere de servicii definite cu pricing, scope, și upgrade path
- [ ] Durată minimă și discount pentru commitment lung documentate
- [ ] Late payment policy inclusă cu escalation steps clari
- [ ] Client responsibilities enumerate explicit (accesuri, timpi de aprobare, POC)
- [ ] Early termination fee calculat și inclus
- [ ] IP, confidențialitate, non-solicitation incluse
- [ ] Semnare electronică configurată cu automations (invoice + onboarding trigger)
- [ ] Reînnoire proactivă implementată (reminder la 60 zile, package la 45 zile)
- [ ] Auto-charge Stripe/GoCardless configurat ca default payment method

## Instrumente
- PandaDoc ($35/mo) / Proposify ($49/mo) — creare, semnare, tracking retainer agreements
- Stripe ($0 monthly + 2.9% per transaction) / GoCardless (0.3% per transaction) — auto-charge lunar
- QuickBooks ($30/mo) / Xero ($15/mo) — invoicing integrat cu retainer schedule
- HubSpot / Pipedrive CRM — renewal reminder automations, deal tracking
- Google Drive — arhivare acorduri semnate, versioned templates
- Harvest ($12/user/mo) — time tracking per client per retainer tier (validare margin)

## Note
- **Death trap — Month-to-month without premium**: clientul care refuză orice commitment face cherry-picking. Ori acceptă 3-month minimum, ori plătește 15-20% premium pe month-to-month. Altfel churn-ul explodează.
- Debit automat (Stripe/GoCardless) elimină 90% din problemele de colectare. Clienții care refuză auto-charge au 3× mai mare rată de late payment.
- Nu fi negociabil pe plata upfront/setup fee și pe durata minimă — protejează cash flow și angajamentul clientului
- Clientul care refuză orice angajament minim e adesea un client cu risc ridicat de churn — evaluează dacă merită acceptat
- Revizuiește prețul retainer-ului anual — inflația, creșterea competenței echipei, și tooling nou justifică 5-15% annual increase
- **Benchmark**: agențiile cu retainere medii de $5K+ au churn lunar de 2-3%. Cele sub $2K au churn de 7-10%. Preț mai mare = client mai serios = mai puțin churn.
