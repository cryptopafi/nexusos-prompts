# Agency Team Hiring and Delegation SOP

## Obiectiv
Construiește un sistem de hiring, onboarding și delegare care permite agenției să crească de la solo-founder la echipă de 10-25+ persoane cu calitate consistentă, fără burn-out al fondatorului, fără dependency pe o singură persoană — folosind team structure blueprints per revenue milestone, scorecard-uri obiective, SOP-uri de delegare și un model hibrid angajați + freelanceri optimizat per etapă.

## Pași

### Pas 1: Definește team structure per revenue milestone
- **$0-$10K MRR (Solo + 1)**:
  - Fondator: vânzări + strategie + client management (60% din timp)
  - **Hire #1: Execution Specialist** — cel mai mare bottleneck de timp al fondatorului. Tipic: copywriter, designer, sau specialist SEO/PPC. Part-time sau freelancer cu ore garantate (15-25h/săptămână).
  - Cost: $1,500-$3,000/mo (freelancer) sau $2,500-$4,000/mo (part-time employee)
  - **Nu angaja generalist** — angajează pe skill-ul care îți mănâncă cel mai mult timp de execuție
  - Structure: flat, direct communication, fondator reviews everything

- **$10K-$50K MRR (Core Team: 3-6 people)**:
  - Fondator: vânzări + strategie + leadership
  - **Hire #2: Account Manager / Client Success** ($3,500-$5,500/mo) — gestionează comunicarea zilnică, raportarea, onboarding. Eliberează fondatorul de client management.
  - **Hire #3: Second Execution Specialist** ($2,500-$4,500/mo) — complementar cu primul (dacă #1 e SEO, #2 e PPC sau content)
  - **Hire #4: Junior / VA** ($1,200-$2,500/mo) — admin, scheduling, data entry, report pulling, invoicing
  - **Optional**: Sales Development Rep dacă pipeline-ul crește ($3,000-$4,500/mo base + commission)
  - Structure: fondator manages everyone directly, weekly team sync (30 min)

- **$50K-$100K MRR (Departments: 7-15 people)**:
  - Fondator: CEO — strategy, major sales, culture
  - **Hire #5: Delivery/Operations Manager** ($5,000-$8,000/mo) — manages delivery team, QA, capacity planning. **Cel mai important hire de la $50K MRR** — fără el, fondatorul rămâne bottleneck.
  - **3-5 Execution Specialists** across services ($3,000-$5,000/mo each)
  - **1-2 Account Managers** ($4,000-$6,000/mo each)
  - **1 Sales person** ($4,000-$6,000/mo base + 10-15% commission on new deals)
  - **1 Part-time Bookkeeper / Finance** ($1,500-$2,500/mo)
  - Structure: 2-3 departments (Sales, Delivery, Ops). Weekly department meetings + bi-weekly all-hands.

- **$100K+ MRR (Leadership Team: 15-25+ people)**:
  - Fondator: CEO — vision, partnerships, major accounts
  - **COO / Director of Operations** ($8,000-$12,000/mo) — runs daily operations
  - **Head of Sales** ($6,000-$10,000/mo + commission) — manages sales team
  - **Head of Delivery** ($6,000-$9,000/mo) — manages all delivery teams
  - **Department leads** for each service vertical
  - **HR / People Ops** ($4,000-$6,000/mo) — hiring, culture, training
  - Structure: leadership team meets weekly. CEO not in daily operations.

### Pas 2: Construiește Job Description cu Scorecard obiectiv
- **Job Description structure** (no fluff, clear expectations):
  ```
  TITLE: [Role Title]
  DEPARTMENT: [Delivery / Sales / Operations]
  REPORTS TO: [Direct manager]
  LOCATION: [Remote / Hybrid / Office] — [Timezone requirements]

  MISSION (1 sentence):
  "[Role] ensures [outcome] by [primary activity]."

  OUTCOMES (what success looks like — measurable):
  • At 30 days: [specific achievement]
  • At 60 days: [specific achievement]
  • At 90 days: [specific achievement]

  RESPONSIBILITIES (5-7, prioritized):
  1. [Most important task — takes 30%+ of time]
  2. [Second most important]
  ...

  NOT RESPONSIBLE FOR (3-4 items — prevents scope creep on the role):
  • [Task that seems related but isn't theirs]

  REQUIREMENTS (must-have — max 5):
  • [X years experience in Y]
  • [Specific tool proficiency]
  • [Language / timezone]

  NICE-TO-HAVE (max 3):
  • [Bonus skill]

  COMPENSATION:
  • [Range] + [Benefits/perks]
  • [Commission structure if applicable]
  ```

- **Scorecard per rol** (separate from JD — internal document):
  ```
  90-DAY SCORECARD: [Role]

  COMPETENCY SCORES (1-5):
  □ Quality of work: _/5
  □ Speed of execution: _/5
  □ Communication: _/5
  □ Proactivity: _/5
  □ Culture fit: _/5

  OUTCOME SCORES (achieved / not achieved):
  □ [Outcome 1 from JD — measurable]
  □ [Outcome 2]
  □ [Outcome 3]

  OVERALL: Pass (avg ≥3.5 + all outcomes achieved) / Probation (2.5-3.5) / Fail (<2.5)
  ```
- Nu angaja fără scorecard definit — fără el, evaluarea la 90 zile e subiectivă și conflictuală

### Pas 3: Procesul de selecție în 4 etape (rigorous dar humane)
**Etapa 1 — Application Filter (async, 5 min per candidate)**:
- Întrebare obligatorie în application: "Describe a specific result you achieved in a similar role. Include numbers." Cei care nu răspund specific = eliminați automat.
- Screening criteria: relevant experience? timezone compatible? compensation aligned? Portfolio/examples available?
- Pass rate: 20-30% → max 15 candidați la etapa 2

**Etapa 2 — Paid Test Task (async, 2-4 ore, plătit $75-$150)**:
- Task reprezentativ din rolul real, nu generic. Exemple:
  - Copywriter: "Rewrite this landing page section for [industry]. Include headline, 3 bullet points, CTA." (2h)
  - PPC specialist: "Audit this Google Ads account (view-only access). Provide top 5 optimization recommendations with expected impact." (3h)
  - Account Manager: "Write a client email responding to this scenario: [unhappy client scenario with specific context]." (1.5h)
  - Designer: "Create 3 social media post designs for [brand] using these guidelines." (3h)
- **Plătește ÎNTOTDEAUNA** — elimini candidații neseriosi, arăți respect, și testezi calitatea reală. $100 per test task × 10 candidates = $1,000 investiție pentru un hire care valorează $50K+/an.
- Evaluează pe scorecard-ul de competență, nu pe "am feeling"

**Etapa 3 — Interview (45-60 min, structured)**:
- (10 min) Background + motivație: "Tell me about the last time you handled [relevant scenario]."
- (15 min) Situational questions (STAR format — Situation, Task, Action, Result):
  - "Describe a time when a client was unhappy with your work. What did you do?"
  - "How do you prioritize when you have 5 tasks due today?"
  - "Tell me about a mistake you made at work and what you learned."
- (10 min) Role-specific deep dive: based on test task results, probe decisions
- (10 min) Culture and values discussion: agency values, work style, communication preferences
- (5 min) Their questions to you
- **Red flags**: can't give specific examples, badmouths previous employer, doesn't ask any questions, compensation-first mentality without interest in role

**Etapa 4 — Reference Check + Trial Period**:
- Check 2 references (previous manager or client, not friends)
- Questions: "Would you hire this person again? What's their biggest strength? What should I watch out for?"
- Offer: include 30-day trial period (for contractors) or 90-day probation (for employees) — explicit in contract
- Start date: align with capacity need (don't hire in advance without revenue to support)

### Pas 4: Onboarding angajat (30-60-90 day framework)
- **Pre-Day 1** (before they start):
  - Send welcome email: first day agenda, what to prepare, team intro doc
  - Create accounts: email, Slack, ClickUp, Google Drive, relevant tools
  - Assign buddy/mentor from existing team (not their direct manager)
  - Prepare first week task list (clear, achievable, confidence-building)

- **Day 1-5 (Foundation)**:
  - Day 1: welcome call (whole team), agency overview (mission, values, clients, processes), tool access verification, first task assignment (small, achievable within 2h)
  - Day 2-3: shadow on 2-3 active projects, read all relevant SOPs, attend client calls as observer
  - Day 4-5: first deliverable with guidance. Manager reviews together, provides feedback in real-time.

- **Day 6-30 (Ramp-up)**:
  - Increasing autonomy: by week 2, execute tasks with review. By week 3, execute with spot-check only.
  - Weekly 1:1 with manager (30 min): what's going well, what's confusing, any blockers
  - Day 30 check-in: formal review against scorecard. Score competencies + outcomes. Discuss gaps.
  - Decision point: continue (on track) / extend probation with specific improvement plan / part ways

- **Day 31-90 (Independence)**:
  - By day 60: should handle client work independently with minimal review
  - By day 90: should meet all outcome targets on scorecard
  - Day 90 final review: formal assessment. Pass = confirmed hire, salary review if applicable. Fail = documented reason + termination.
  - Feedback cadence: bi-weekly 1:1 during months 2-3, then monthly ongoing

### Pas 5: Delegarea corectă — sistemul, nu speranța
- **Formula delegării eficiente**: Task + Context + Standard + Deadline + Verificare
  - ❌ "Fă raportul pentru ClientX" (lipsa totală)
  - ✅ "Creează raportul lunar pentru ClientX (template aici: [link]). Include datele din GA4 și GSC, comparație MoM, 3 insights, și plan next month. Calitate QA: second reviewer înainte de trimitere. Deadline: miercuri 14:00. Trimite-mi draft pentru review final la marți 18:00."
- **Delegation Ladder** (4 niveluri — progresezi echipa pe fiecare):
  1. **Do together**: tu faci, ei observă (prima dată cu task-ul)
  2. **They do, you watch**: ei fac, tu observi și corectezi în timp real
  3. **They do, you review**: ei fac autonom, tu verifici output-ul
  4. **They do, they own**: ei fac și verifică (tu primești doar escalări)
  - Primele 3 repetări ale unui task nou: nivel 1-2-3. De la repetarea 4: nivel 4.
- **SOP requirement**: fiecare task delegat trebuie să aibă SOP (chiar dacă e 5 bullet points). Dacă nu ai SOP, dedici 15 minute sa scrii unul ÎNAINTE de a delega. Nu delega cu "te descurci tu".
- **Nu re-faci munca altcuiva fără feedback** — dacă modifici output-ul cuiva fără să-i explici de ce, nu construiești capacitate, construiești dependență.

### Pas 6: Configurează comunicarea și meeting cadence
- **Daily async standup** (Slack, 2 min per person):
  - What I did yesterday (3 bullets max)
  - What I'm doing today (3 bullets max)
  - Any blockers (if none, say "none")
- **Weekly team sync** (30 min, video call):
  - Pipeline update (sales): new leads, proposals out, deals closing
  - Delivery update: client status, deadlines this week, capacity
  - Wins + challenges (rotate: each person shares 1 win, 1 challenge)
  - Action items with owners + deadlines
- **Monthly 1:1s** (30 min, manager + direct report):
  - Performance: what's going well, what needs improvement
  - Development: what skills do you want to build?
  - Satisfaction: 1-10 how happy are you? What would make it higher?
  - Career: where do you see yourself in 6-12 months?
- **Quarterly all-hands** (60 min):
  - Agency performance: revenue, growth, wins, client highlights
  - Strategy: what's coming next quarter
  - Culture: recognition, values reinforcement
  - Open Q&A
- **Tools**: Slack (async daily), Zoom (weekly sync), ClickUp (task management), Notion (knowledge base)

### Pas 7: Scalează cu model hibrid angajați + freelanceri
- **Core team (employees/long-term contractors)** — roles that need:
  - Continuity (account management, strategy)
  - Institutional knowledge (senior specialists who know clients deeply)
  - Culture carrier (someone who embodies agency values)
  - Cost: higher base, but lower management overhead and higher quality consistency
- **Flex team (freelancers/contractors)** — roles that need:
  - Skill diversity (specific expertise for specific projects)
  - Scalability (ramp up/down with client volume)
  - Cost efficiency (no benefits, no idle time)
  - Cost: $25-$75/hr for offshore, $50-$150/hr for onshore, $100-$250/hr for specialists
- **Sourcing**:
  - Upwork (verify: 95%+ success rate, $10K+ earnings, specific portfolio items relevant to your niche)
  - Toptal (vetted senior talent, $100-$200/hr, best for strategy/architecture)
  - LinkedIn (search for specific titles in your region/timezone)
  - Agency communities (Facebook groups, Slack communities) — tap into existing networks
  - Referrals from existing team (best source — $250-$500 referral bonus)
- **Freelancer management**:
  - NDA + freelancer agreement signed before ANY access to client data
  - Access: minimal required (task-specific, revoked after project)
  - Briefing: always written (not verbal). Use ClickUp task with brief, assets, deadline, quality standard
  - Payment: milestone-based for projects, bi-weekly for ongoing work. Pay on time, always. Reputation matters.
  - QA: always review freelancer work before sending to client. They represent your agency.
- **Optimal ratio**: 60-70% core team / 30-40% flex team. This gives stability + scalability.

### Pas 8: Construiește cultura de la prima angajare
- **Definește 3-5 core values** (nu platitudini — valori acționabile):
  - Exemplu: "Radical transparency" = we share bad news with clients immediately, not at the monthly report
  - Exemplu: "Own the outcome" = if a deliverable fails, the person responsible proposes the fix, not the manager
  - Exemplu: "Teach, don't redo" = if someone's work needs improvement, we teach them how, we don't silently redo it
- **Values in practice**:
  - Include in interview process (ask: "Give me an example when you demonstrated [value]")
  - Include in onboarding (day 1: values walkthrough with examples)
  - Include in 1:1s (ask: "How did you practice [value] this month?")
  - Include in reviews (scorecard has "Culture fit" dimension)
- **Recognition**: public praise in Slack #wins channel. Monthly "team MVP" nominated by peers. Small rewards ($50-$100 gift card, extra half-day off).
- **The founder's behavior IS the culture** — how you handle mistakes, how you communicate with clients, how you give feedback defines everything. If you work 80-hour weeks, your team will burn out trying to match you or disengage because they can't.

## Note
- **Prima angajare va dura 2-3x mai mult decât crezi** — planifică 3-4 săptămâni de la JD la start. Nu angaja în panică.
- **"Hire slow, fire fast"**: dacă nu performează la 60 de zile cu feedback clar și suport, nu va performa la 120. Fii direct și fă schimbarea.
- **"Nu am timp să pregătesc pe cineva"** este exact motivul pentru care ești blocat în execuție. Invest 20 ore acum, save 200 ore în următoarele 6 luni.
- **Death trap — Hiring ahead of revenue**: nu angaja FTE dacă nu ai MRR care acoperă salariul lor × 3 luni în cash reserve. Folosește freelanceri ca buffer.
- **Death trap — Founder as single point of failure**: dacă te lovește un autobuz și agenția se oprește → nu ai delegat suficient. Test: poți lipsi 2 săptămâni fără ca business-ul să sufere semnificativ?
- **Compensation benchmark (digital agency, 2025-2026)**:
  - Junior specialist: $2,500-$4,000/mo
  - Mid specialist: $4,000-$6,000/mo
  - Senior specialist: $6,000-$9,000/mo
  - Account manager: $4,000-$6,500/mo
  - Ops/Delivery manager: $5,500-$8,500/mo
  - Director level: $8,000-$12,000/mo
  - (Ranges for US/EU market; adjust 30-50% lower for offshore)
- Cultura se construiește de la prima angajare — cum comunici, cum dai feedback, cum tratezi erorile definește cultura pe termen lung
