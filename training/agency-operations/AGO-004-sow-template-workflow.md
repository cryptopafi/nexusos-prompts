# SOW (Statement of Work) Template and Workflow

## Obiectiv
Creează și implementează un template de Statement of Work (SOW) cu clauze specifice per tip de serviciu, workflow de creare-semnare-arhivare automatizat, și sistem de Change Order management — eliminând ambiguitatea care duce la scope creep, dispute de plată și servicii necontractate livrate gratuit. Un SOW solid previne 80% din dispute și protejează marja agenției.

## Pași

### Pas 1: Înțelege structura unui SOW de agenție profesional (9 secțiuni obligatorii)
Un SOW complet pentru agenții digitale trebuie să acopere:
1. **Scope of Services** — ce faci exact, cu granularitate maximă
2. **Deliverables** — output-urile concrete (nu activități, ci livrabile tangibile)
3. **Timeline & Milestones** — date exacte, nu "approximate"
4. **Client Responsibilities** — ce furnizează clientul, când, cu ce consecințe dacă întârzie
5. **Pricing & Payment Schedule** — sume, eșalonare, penalități, metode
6. **Change Order Process** — cum se gestionează cereri extra
7. **Exclusions** — ce NU e inclus (la fel de important ca scope-ul)
8. **Termination & Exit** — condiții de ieșire pentru ambele părți
9. **Intellectual Property & Confidentiality** — cine deține ce, la ce condiții

SOW-ul este fie un document standalone (proiecte), fie un addendum la un Master Service Agreement (MSA) pentru retainere pe termen lung.

### Pas 2: Construiește template-ul SOW master cu clausele exacte

```
STATEMENT OF WORK [SOW-{YYYY}-{NNN}]

Date: [Date]
Client: [Legal Entity Name], [Address]
Agency: [Your Legal Entity], [Address]
Effective Period: [Start Date] through [End Date / "ongoing per Section 8"]

─────────────────────────────────────────────

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES

Agency shall provide the following services during the Term:

[OPTION A — Retainer Example]
• Monthly SEO management including: technical audit (quarterly), on-page
  optimization for up to [X] pages/month, content creation of [X] blog
  articles (800-1,200 words each), link building ([X] backlinks/month
  from DA30+ domains), monthly performance report with insights
• Google Ads management for up to [X] campaigns across Search and Shopping
  networks, with weekly bid optimization and monthly creative refresh
• Monthly strategy call (45 minutes) with account manager

[OPTION B — Project Example]
• Design and development of [X]-page website on [WordPress/Webflow/Shopify]
• Responsive design (desktop, tablet, mobile)
• Basic on-page SEO setup (meta titles, descriptions, schema markup)
• Integration with [GA4, GSC, Facebook Pixel]
• [X] rounds of design revisions per page
• 1-hour post-launch training session via Zoom

2. EXCLUSIONS (OUT OF SCOPE)

The following services are explicitly NOT included in this SOW and will
require a separate Change Order if requested:
• [Community management (responding to comments, DMs, reviews)]
• [Content translation into languages other than English]
• [Print design materials]
• [Video production or editing]
• [Additional pages beyond the [X] specified above]
• [Third-party software licenses or ad spend (billed separately)]
• [Photography or stock image licensing]

3. DELIVERABLES

| # | Deliverable              | Format        | Delivery Date | Owner   |
|---|--------------------------|---------------|---------------|---------|
| 1 | [Technical SEO Audit]    | [PDF Report]  | [Date]        | Agency  |
| 2 | [Blog Content x4]       | [CMS Draft]   | [Monthly, 15th]| Agency |
| 3 | [Monthly Report]         | [PDF + Looker]| [1st business day]| Agency|
| 4 | [Strategy Call]          | [Zoom]        | [Monthly, 3rd week]| Both|

4. TIMELINE & MILESTONES

• Project Kickoff: [Date] (contingent on receipt of all Client assets
  per Section 5)
• Milestone 1 — [Description]: [Date]
• Milestone 2 — [Description]: [Date]
• Final Delivery: [Date]

NOTE: Timeline assumes receipt of all Client Responsibilities (Section 5)
by [Date]. Delays in Client deliverables automatically extend the timeline
by an equivalent number of business days. Agency will notify Client in
writing of any timeline impact within 2 business days of the delay.

5. CLIENT RESPONSIBILITIES

Client shall provide the following by the dates specified:
• Admin access to Google Analytics 4, Google Search Console, Google Ads
  account, Meta Business Manager — by [Date]
• Brand assets (logo in vector format, brand guidelines, font files) —
  by [Date]
• Designated Point of Contact (POC) available for questions within
  [2] business days
• Approval or actionable feedback on deliverables within [3] business
  days of submission. After [5] business days with no response,
  deliverables are deemed approved.
• Ad spend budget funded directly to advertising platforms (not through
  Agency) — monthly, by [Date]

IMPORTANT: Failure to provide the above by specified dates may result in
delays to the project timeline and/or reduced deliverable quality. Agency
is not responsible for missed KPIs resulting from Client delays.

6. PRICING & PAYMENT

Total Fee: $[Amount] [per month / one-time] + applicable taxes
Setup Fee (if applicable): $[Amount], due at signing

Payment Schedule:
[RETAINER] $[Amount] due on the 1st of each month, payable within
  [5] business days of invoice date
[PROJECT] 50% ($[X]) due at signing | 25% ($[X]) due at Milestone 2 |
  25% ($[X]) due at Final Delivery
[PERFORMANCE] $[X] management fee/month + [X]% of managed ad spend,
  invoiced monthly in arrears

Payment Method: [Wire transfer / Stripe / ACH / Credit card]
Currency: [USD / EUR]

Late Payment: Invoices unpaid after [10] calendar days from due date
incur a late fee of [2%] per month on the outstanding balance. Agency
reserves the right to pause all work after [15] days of non-payment
until account is brought current. Resumption of work following a payment
pause may require up to [5] business days.

7. CHANGE ORDER PROCESS

Any request for services, deliverables, or modifications not described
in Section 1 shall be managed through a formal Change Order (CO).

Process:
(a) Client submits request in writing (email acceptable)
(b) Agency provides CO document within [2] business days including:
    description of change, impact on timeline, additional cost
(c) No work begins on Change Order items until CO is approved in
    writing by Client
(d) Approved COs become binding addenda to this SOW

Change Orders are numbered sequentially (CO-001, CO-002, etc.) and
archived with this SOW.

8. TERM & TERMINATION

[RETAINER] Initial term: [X] months from Effective Date. Auto-renews
  for successive [1-month / 3-month] periods unless either party
  provides [30] days written notice before the end of the current period.
[PROJECT] This SOW terminates upon completion of all deliverables and
  receipt of final payment.

Termination by Client: [30] days written notice. Client pays for all
  services rendered through termination date plus any approved COs.
Termination by Agency: [15] days written notice if payment is overdue
  by [15]+ days, or if Client materially breaches obligations under
  Section 5.
Early Termination Fee: If Client terminates before completion of the
  initial [X]-month term, a fee equal to [50]% of remaining monthly
  fees for the balance of the term is due.

9. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY & CONFIDENTIALITY

• All original work product created by Agency under this SOW transfers
  to Client upon receipt of full payment. Prior to full payment, Agency
  retains all rights.
• Agency retains the right to use anonymized results, screenshots, and
  case study data in portfolio and marketing materials unless Client
  opts out in writing.
• Both parties agree to keep confidential all proprietary business
  information shared during the engagement.
• Non-solicitation: Client shall not directly solicit or hire Agency
  personnel during the Term and for [12] months following termination.
  Violation incurs a placement fee of [50]% of the employee's annual
  compensation.

AGREED AND ACCEPTED:

Client: _________________________ Date: _________
Name:  _________________________  Title: _________

Agency: _________________________ Date: _________
Name:  _________________________  Title: _________
```

### Pas 3: Creează variații SOW per tip de serviciu (3 templates minimum)
- **SOW — SEO Retainer**: scope = activități lunare fixe (audit tehnic trimestrial, X articole/lună, X backlinks/lună din DA30+, optimizare on-page X pagini/lună, raport lunar + strategy call). Deliverables = raport lunar PDF + Looker Studio live + ranking tracker share. Timeline = rolling lunar cu review trimestrial QBR. Exclusions: content translation, PPC, social media, web development. Pricing: $2,500-$8,000/mo típic, $2,000 setup fee.
- **SOW — Web Design Project**: scope = nr. pagini, nr. revizii incluse per page, tehnologie (WordPress/Webflow/Shopify), responsive breakpoints. Deliverables = wireframes → design mockup → development → staging review → go-live → 1h training + 30-day support. Timeline = 4-8 săptămâni cu milestones per fază. Exclusions: copywriting (unless specified), photography, hosting, domain, ongoing maintenance, additional pages. Pricing: $5,000-$25,000 project, 50/25/25 payment.
- **SOW — PPC Management**: scope = setup inițial (account audit, campaign structure, keyword research, ad copy, conversion tracking) + management lunar (weekly optimization, bid management, A/B testing, negative keyword management, monthly report). Ad spend managed by client directly. Deliverables = raport săptămânal + raport lunar + dashboard live + monthly strategy call. Exclusions: landing page design, CRO, organic social, SEO. Pricing: $1,500-$5,000/mo management fee + 15-20% of ad spend (tiered: 20% under $20K spend, 15% $20-50K, 12% $50K+). Setup fee: $1,500-$3,000.

### Pas 4: Implementează workflow de creare, review și semnare SOW
- **Step 1 — Discovery call completed**: sales team completes deal summary in CRM (services, price agreed, timeline)
- **Step 2 — SOW draft (same day or next business day)**: account manager creates SOW from template in Proposify/PandaDoc. Populate: client details, scope per services sold, pricing, exclusions, dates. Internal QA: second person reviews scope and pricing before sending.
- **Step 3 — Send for client review**: via PandaDoc/Proposify (not email with PDF attachment). Include cover note: "Attached is the SOW summarizing what we discussed. Please review and let me know if anything needs clarification. Once signed, I'll send the first invoice and we'll schedule your Kickoff Call."
- **Step 4 — Follow-up cadence**: Day 0 = sent. Day 2 = follow-up if not opened ("Just checking you received the SOW"). Day 5 = follow-up if opened but not signed ("Any questions I can clarify?"). Day 10 = final follow-up ("I want to hold your start date of [X]. Can we finalize this week?"). Day 15 = if still unsigned, flag as stale deal — address objection or close lost.
- **Step 5 — Signed + paid**: nu începi lucrul până SOW-ul nu e semnat ȘI prima plată nu e confirmată. Nicio excepție, nicio promisiune verbală, niciun "trimite factura mai târziu". Regula #1 a cash flow sănătos.
- **Step 6 — Archive**: SOW semnat automat salvat în Google Drive `/Clients/[Name]/07-Admin/` + CRM deal record + trigger onboarding sequence (AGO-003)

### Pas 5: Gestionează Change Order-urile cu proces formal
- **Când apare cerere out-of-scope**: nu spui "nu", nu faci gratis, spui: "Cu plăcere — pregătesc un Change Order cu costul și timeline-ul în 24 ore."
- **Change Order template** (1 pagină):
  ```
  CHANGE ORDER [CO-{NNN}]
  Reference SOW: [SOW-{YYYY}-{NNN}]
  Client: [Name]
  Date: [Date]

  1. REQUESTED CHANGE
  [Exact description of additional work requested]

  2. IMPACT ON SCOPE
  [What is being added to the original SOW]

  3. IMPACT ON TIMELINE
  [Does this affect existing deliverable dates? If yes, new dates.]

  4. ADDITIONAL COST
  $[Amount] + applicable taxes
  Payment: [Due upon approval / Added to next monthly invoice]

  5. APPROVAL
  This Change Order becomes effective upon written approval (email
  or electronic signature acceptable).

  Client Approved: ______________ Date: __________
  Agency Acknowledged: __________ Date: __________
  ```
- Trimite CO prin Proposify/PandaDoc (same tool as SOW — profesionalism consistent)
- Nu există CO verbal — orice aprobare verbală trebuie urmată de confirmare scrisă (email counts, dar e-sign e preferabil)
- Numerotează CO-urile secvențial per client. Ține un log: CO# | Date | Description | Amount | Status (Approved/Pending/Declined)
- **Pricing COs**: orice task sub 2 ore → minimum $250 CO. 2-5 ore → $500-$1,500. 5+ ore → custom quote. Nu faci niciodată "doar 30 minute" fără CO — 30 min × 10 clienți = 5 ore/lună pierdute = $2,000-$3,000 revenue pierdut.

### Pas 6: Implementează SOW amendment process pentru retainere pe termen lung
- La fiecare reînnoire sau QBR: evaluează dacă scope-ul curent reflectă realitatea
- **SOW Amendment** (diferit de CO — e o modificare permanentă, nu punctuală):
  - "Effective [Date], Section 1 of SOW [Number] is amended as follows: [New scope]. Monthly fee adjusted from $[X] to $[Y]."
  - Semnat de ambele părți, atașat SOW-ului original
- Trigger amendment: client a cerut CO-uri frecvente pe același subiect (>3 CO-uri similare = integrezi în scope cu preț ajustat), schimbare de strategie, sau price increase anual
- **Annual price increase clause** (include în SOW Section 6): "Agency reserves the right to adjust monthly fees annually, with 60 days written notice. Adjustments reflect changes in scope, market conditions, and team expertise."

### Pas 7: Review periodic și îmbunătățire template SOW
- La fiecare 6 luni sau după o dispută semnificativă: revizuiește template-ul
- Întreabă-te: unde a apărut confuzia? ce a fost interpretat diferit? ce lipsea din contract?
- Actualizează secțiunea EXCLUSIONS pe baza celor mai frecvente cereri out-of-scope (ultimele 6 luni)
- Consultă un avocat local pentru validarea clauzelor de terminare, IP și limitare de răspundere — o singură dată pentru template-ul master ($500-$1,500 investiție, ROI infinit)
- Partajează versiunea actualizată cu echipa internă + training de 30 min pe modificări
- Versionează template-urile: SOW-Template-v2.1, v2.2 etc. Nu modifica template live fără documentare.

## Note
- SOW NU este un contract legal complet — dacă lucrezi cu clienți enterprise sau sume >$25K, adaugă un Master Service Agreement (MSA) separat. SOW-ul devine addendum la MSA.
- Secțiunea "EXCLUSIONS" este la fel de importantă ca scope-ul inclus — dacă nu e scris explicit ce NU e inclus, clientul va presupune că e inclus
- Nu negocia plata upfront — 50% upfront este standard în agenții și protejează cash flow-ul. Clienții care refuză 50% upfront sunt red flag pentru cash flow problems
- Un SOW bun previne 80% din disputele cu clienții. Restul de 20% le previne Change Order process-ul.
- **Timp mediu SOW creation**: cu template, 30-45 minute per SOW. Fără template, 2-3 ore. Template-ul se amortizează la al doilea client.
- **Signature to payment benchmark**: SOW semnat → invoice trimis în aceeași zi → plată confirmată în max 5 zile. Dacă durează mai mult, tighten payment terms.
