# PL-003 — Determine HIPAA Applicability

## Problema
Incorrectly assuming HIPAA does or does not apply leads to either costly unnecessary compliance or dangerous non-compliance.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- PL-001 completed or basic healthcare industry knowledge

### Steps
1. Determine if the entity is a Covered Entity: health plan, healthcare clearinghouse, or healthcare provider transmitting health info electronically
2. Identify Business Associates that handle PHI on behalf of Covered Entities
3. Define what constitutes Protected Health Information (PHI) vs de-identified data
4. Assess if the data qualifies as PHI (individually identifiable + relates to health/payment/care)
5. Evaluate the 18 HIPAA identifiers for de-identification safe harbor method
6. Determine which HIPAA rules apply: Privacy, Security, Breach Notification, Enforcement

### Verification
- Covered Entity or Business Associate status definitively determined
- PHI correctly identified and distinguished from de-identified data
