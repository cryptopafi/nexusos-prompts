# PL-011 — Evaluate FTC Section 5 Enforcement Exposure

## Problema
The FTC's broad Section 5 authority fills gaps where no sector-specific privacy law applies, catching many organizations off guard.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- PL-001 completed

### Steps
1. Assess if the organization's privacy practices could be deemed unfair or deceptive under FTC Act Section 5
2. Review privacy policies for accuracy (deceptive if practices contradict stated policies)
3. Evaluate data security practices against FTC's established expectations from consent decrees
4. Assess children's data practices against COPPA requirements
5. Review FTC enforcement history in the organization's industry sector
6. Identify potential unfairness claims: substantial injury, not outweighed by benefits, not reasonably avoidable
7. Develop compliance recommendations based on FTC guidance and precedent

### Verification
- Privacy policy accuracy verified against actual data practices
- Key FTC enforcement precedents in the sector identified and addressed
