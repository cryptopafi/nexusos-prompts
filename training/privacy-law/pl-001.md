# PL-001 — Conduct Privacy Law Applicability Assessment

## Problema
Organizations often misidentify which privacy laws apply to their data practices, leading to compliance gaps or unnecessary overhead.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Inventory of data types collected and processed by the organization

### Steps
1. Catalog all personal data collected: categories, sources, and purposes
2. Identify data subjects' jurisdictions (federal, state, international)
3. Map applicable federal laws: HIPAA, GLBA, FERPA, COPPA, ECPA
4. Map applicable state laws: CCPA/CPRA, state breach notification laws
5. Assess GDPR applicability (EU data subjects, establishment in EU, or monitoring behavior)
6. Determine sector-specific regulations based on industry (healthcare, financial, education)
7. Create applicability matrix: data type x jurisdiction x applicable law

### Verification
- Applicability matrix covers all data types and jurisdictions
- At least federal and state levels assessed for US operations
