# PL-009 — Develop a Data Breach Notification Response Plan

## Problema
Organizations without pre-built breach response plans consistently miss notification deadlines and make compliance errors under pressure.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- PL-001 completed
- PL-008 recommended

### Steps
1. Define what constitutes a breach under applicable laws (HIPAA, state laws, GDPR)
2. Establish breach detection and escalation procedures
3. Form incident response team with defined roles (privacy officer, legal, IT, communications, leadership)
4. Create notification timeline tracker: HIPAA (60 days), state laws (varies), GDPR (72 hours)
5. Draft template notification letters for individuals, regulators, and media
6. Define risk assessment methodology for determining notification obligations
7. Establish documentation and evidence preservation procedures
8. Plan post-breach remediation and prevention measures

### Verification
- Response plan covers all applicable breach notification laws
- Template notifications meet content requirements for each applicable law
