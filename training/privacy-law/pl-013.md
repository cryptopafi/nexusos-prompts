# PL-013 — Perform Privacy Risk Assessment for an Organization

## Problema
Privacy risk assessments prevent costly breaches and regulatory actions by identifying vulnerabilities before they are exploited.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- PL-001 completed
- PL-002 completed

### Steps
1. Define assessment scope: systems, data types, processing activities
2. Create comprehensive data flow maps showing collection, use, storage, sharing, and destruction
3. Identify privacy threats: unauthorized access, excessive collection, purpose creep, retention beyond necessity
4. Assess likelihood and impact for each identified threat
5. Evaluate existing controls and their effectiveness
6. Calculate residual risk after existing controls
7. Prioritize risks and develop mitigation recommendations
8. Document risk assessment with executive summary for leadership

### Verification
- Data flow maps cover all in-scope processing activities
- Risk matrix populated with likelihood and impact for all identified threats
