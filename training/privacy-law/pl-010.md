# PL-010 — Conduct Post-Breach Compliance Response

## Problema
The actions taken after discovering a breach determine regulatory penalties, litigation exposure, and reputational damage.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- PL-009 completed

### Steps
1. Contain the breach: isolate affected systems, revoke compromised credentials
2. Conduct forensic investigation to determine scope, cause, and affected individuals
3. Perform four-factor risk assessment (HIPAA): nature of PHI, unauthorized person, actual access/use, mitigation extent
4. Determine notification obligations based on risk assessment results
5. Notify affected individuals within required timelines with all required content
6. Notify regulators: HHS (HIPAA), state AGs, supervisory authorities (GDPR)
7. Notify media if breach affects 500+ individuals in a state (HIPAA)
8. Implement corrective actions and document lessons learned

### Verification
- All notifications sent within required timelines
- Four-factor risk assessment documented with supporting evidence
