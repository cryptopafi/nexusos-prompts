# PL-002 — Apply Fair Information Practice Principles (FIPPs)

## Problema
FIPPs are the foundation of all modern privacy law, and failing to apply them creates systemic compliance failures across regulations.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- PL-001 completed

### Steps
1. Apply Notice/Awareness: document and communicate data collection practices
2. Apply Choice/Consent: implement opt-in or opt-out mechanisms appropriate to data sensitivity
3. Apply Access/Participation: enable individuals to view and correct their data
4. Apply Integrity/Security: implement safeguards proportional to data sensitivity
5. Apply Enforcement/Redress: establish accountability mechanisms and complaint processes
6. Map each FIPP to specific organizational policies and technical controls
7. Identify gaps where FIPPs are not fully implemented

### Verification
- All five FIPPs addressed with specific organizational implementations
- Gap analysis identifies priority remediation items
