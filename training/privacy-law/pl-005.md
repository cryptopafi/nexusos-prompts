# PL-005 — Manage HIPAA Patient Access to Records

## Problema
Access request mishandling is the most common HIPAA complaint to HHS and results in frequent enforcement actions.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- PL-003 completed

### Steps
1. Establish process to receive and log access requests
2. Verify identity of the individual requesting access
3. Respond within 30 days (one 30-day extension permitted with written notice)
4. Provide access in the format requested if readily producible
5. Limit fees to reasonable cost-based amounts (labor for copying, supplies, postage)
6. Document and communicate any permissible denials with appeal rights
7. Handle requests for electronic copies of electronic health records

### Verification
- Response timeline meets 30-day requirement
- Fee schedule complies with HHS guidance on reasonable cost-based fees
