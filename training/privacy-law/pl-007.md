# PL-007 — Evaluate HIPAA Permitted Uses and Disclosures

## Problema
Incorrectly classifying a disclosure as permitted vs requiring authorization results in either HIPAA violations or delayed care.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- PL-003 completed
- PL-006 completed

### Steps
1. Classify the proposed use/disclosure: treatment, payment, or healthcare operations (no authorization needed)
2. Evaluate if the disclosure falls under a permitted category: public health, law enforcement, judicial, research, etc.
3. Determine if individual authorization is required (marketing, sale of PHI, psychotherapy notes)
4. Assess if the disclosure qualifies for the facility directory or notification exceptions
5. Apply minimum necessary standard to permitted disclosures
6. Document the legal basis for each disclosure decision
7. Maintain accounting of disclosures as required

### Verification
- Every disclosure classified with specific regulatory citation
- Authorization requirements correctly identified
