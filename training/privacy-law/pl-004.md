# PL-004 — Implement HIPAA Privacy Notice Requirements

## Problema
Non-compliant privacy notices expose covered entities to enforcement actions and erode patient trust.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- PL-003 completed

### Steps
1. Draft Notice of Privacy Practices (NPP) covering all required elements
2. Include description of uses and disclosures for treatment, payment, and healthcare operations
3. Document individual rights: access, amendment, accounting of disclosures, restrictions, confidential communications
4. Include complaint process and contact information for privacy officer and HHS
5. Distribute NPP at first service encounter and make available on request
6. Post NPP prominently in facility and on website
7. Obtain written acknowledgment of receipt from patients

### Verification
- NPP contains all elements required by 45 CFR 164.520
- Distribution and acknowledgment processes documented
