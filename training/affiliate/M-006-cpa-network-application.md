# M-006 — CPA Network Application and Acceptance

**Domeniu**: affiliate | **Sub-domeniu**: cpa-network-application
**Versiune**: 2.0 | **Data**: 2026-03-20
**Autor**: Genie Slim Core
**Status**: ACTIVE
**Regula asociata**: TRAIN-S-001
**Forge Score**: estimated H/3.5

---

## Obiectiv

Procedura completa de aplicare si acceptare in retelele CPA de top (MaxBounty, ClickDealer, Mobidea, Advidi, Perform[cb]), de la pregatirea CV-ului de afiliat pana la stabilirea relatiei cu affiliate manager-ul. Retelele CPA resping 60-80% din aplicanti — aceasta procedura maximizeaza rata de acceptare prin pregatire strategica. Accesul la retele CPA este prerequisite-ul #1 pentru orice campanie profitabila pe SMS/push.

**SMSads Context**: 3SNET este singurul network activ. AvaTrade $250 CPA (MaxBounty) are EC/1K SMS de 3-5x superior fata de MarathonBet (3SNET). Fara cont MaxBounty/ClickDealer/Mobidea, SMSads pierde revenue cuantificabil pe fiecare campanie. SMSADS-P4 identifica retelele prioritare — M-006 executa aplicarea si acceptarea.

**Ce se intampla fara procedura:**
- Aplicatie respinsa la MaxBounty (60-80% rejection rate) → acces pierdut la oferte premium
- Aplicatie cu "various traffic methods" → rejection instant (red flag #1 pentru networks)
- Lipsa website profesional → rejection automat (verificat manual de network staff)
- Pierderea call-ului cu AM (procedura comuna la retele serioase) → rejection
- Dependenta exclusiva de un singur network → risc operational major

---

## Pasi

### Pas 1: RESEARCH RETELE CPA TARGET (TIERED APPROACH)

Clasifica retelele pe 3 tiers si aplica strategic:

**Tier 1 — Top Networks (aplica cu pregatire maxima)**:
| Network | Specializare | Payout | Procesul de Aplicare |
|---------|-------------|--------|---------------------|
| MaxBounty | CPA general, finance, health | NET15 | Formular + call telefonic obligatoriu |
| Perform[cb] | Enterprise, finance US | NET30 | Formular + verificare detaliata |
| Advidi | Gambling/dating EU | NET30 | Formular + AM interview |
| CrakRevenue | Dating, adult | NET30 | Formular + site review |

**Tier 2 — Mid Networks (aplica dupa 30 zile stats)**:
| Network | Specializare | Payout |
|---------|-------------|--------|
| ClickDealer | Mobile, CPI, nutra | NET30 |
| Mobidea | Mobile, WAP billing | Weekly |
| 3SNET | Gambling LATAM/CIS | NET30 |
| GlobalWide Media | General CPA | NET30 |

**Tier 3 — Entry Networks (aplica imediat, build track record)**:
| Network | Specializare | Payout |
|---------|-------------|--------|
| CPAGrip | Incent, content locking | NET30 |
| OGAds | Mobile, content locking | Weekly |
| AdWork Media | General, entry-friendly | NET30 |
| Monetizer | SmartLink auto | Daily |

**Strategie**: aplica la 2-3 Tier 3 IMEDIAT → build $500+ track record in 30 zile → aplica la Tier 1 cu dovezi.

### Pas 2: PREGATIRE "CV AFFILIATE" (DOCUMENT INTERN)

Construieste un document intern cu tot ce vei include in aplicatii:

**Sectiuni obligatorii:**
- **Traffic sources** (FII SPECIFIC): "SMS carrier traffic via VOX partnerships in Bolivia (2.4M reach) and Romania (20M reach, launching April 2026). Push notifications via PropellerAds and RichAds. Native ads via MGID."
- **Daily volume**: "5,000-15,000 SMS/day current, scaling to 50,000/day Q2 2026. Push: 10,000 impressions/day."
- **Verticals**: "Sports betting, online casino, forex/CFD trading, mobile app installs"
- **Experience**: conversii documentate (chiar modeste), revenue screenshots (anonimizate), platforme folosite
- **Budget**: "$500-2,000/month on paid traffic" (retelele vor sa stie ca ai buget real)
- **Tracking**: "Self-hosted Binom tracker with S2S postback integration"

**Daca esti nou (zero stats)**:
- Subliniaza: cunostinte tehnice (tracking setup, landing page building), buget alocat, plan de actiune concret
- Mentioneaza training/cursuri completate
- Prezinta plan 90 zile cu volume estimate

### Pas 3: SETUP WEBSITE PROFESIONAL (OBLIGATORIU)

Retelele CPA verifica website-ul in procesul de review. Minim necesar:

- **Domeniu propriu**: smsads.net sau similar (nu subdomain gratuit)
- **5-10 articole de calitate** in nisa ta (reviews, how-to, comparisons — reutilizeaza din M-113)
- **About page**: informatii reale — cine esti, experienta, misiunea site-ului
- **Privacy Policy**: genereaza pe privacypolicygenerator.info (gratuit)
- **Contact page**: email real + optional formular
- **Design profesional**: tema curata WordPress (GeneratePress/Astra gratuit), zero erori vizuale
- **SSL activ**: HTTPS obligatoriu (Let's Encrypt gratuit)
- **Minimum 30 zile online**: unele retele verifica varsta domeniului

**Eroare frecventa**: Website cu "Lorem ipsum" sau pagini goale. Review-erii networkurilor deschid site-ul si verifica — un site incomplet = rejection instant.

### Pas 4: APLICARE SIMULTANA LA 5-7 RETELE (DIVERSIFICAT PE TIERS)

Aplica simultan:
- 2 retele Tier 3 (acceptare rapida, build stats)
- 3-4 retele Tier 2 (sanse moderate)
- 1-2 retele Tier 1 (cel mai pregatit — MaxBounty prioritate)

**Timing**: aplica dimineata (ora locala a network-ului — US EST pentru MaxBounty). Aplicatiile trimise dimineata au 15% rata de raspuns mai buna (procesate in aceeasi zi vs next-day).

**Nu te limita la una**: rejection la o retea nu afecteaza alte retele. Retelele NU comunica intre ele despre aplicanti.

### Pas 5: COMPLETARE FORMULAR DE APLICARE (DETALII CRITICE)

Reguli pe formular:
- **Traffic sources**: FII SPECIFIC — "SMS carrier traffic, push notifications via PropellerAds, native ads via MGID" (nu "various methods" sau "I'll figure it out" — aceste raspunsuri = rejection automat)
- **Website URL**: site-ul pregatit in Pas 3 (obligatoriu, nu lasa gol)
- **Monthly revenue**: fii onest dar optimist. Daca esti nou: "$0 currently, plan to start with $500/month budget"
- **Experience**: mentioneaza orice relevant — chiar si training/cursuri
- **Verticals**: specifica 2-3 verticale concrete
- **How did you hear about us**: "STM Forum recommendation" sau "Industry research" (nu "Google search" — suna neprofesional)

**Campuri obligatorii**: completeaza TOATE. Aplicatii incomplete → rejection automat.

### Pas 6: PREGATIRE PENTRU CALL-UL TELEFONIC (MAXBOUNTY STANDARD)

MaxBounty si alte retele tier-1 suna aplicantii. Pregatire:

**Intrebari frecvente si raspunsuri pregatite:**

| Intrebare | Raspuns Model |
|-----------|--------------|
| "What traffic sources do you use?" | "Primarily SMS carrier traffic through direct operator partnerships. Also push notifications via PropellerAds and native ads through MGID." |
| "How much do you plan to spend daily?" | "$50-100 initially, scaling to $500/day as we find winning campaigns." |
| "What verticals interest you?" | "Sports betting and forex/CFD trading for LATAM markets — Bolivia and Peru primarily." |
| "Do you have experience?" | "I've been running SMS campaigns for [X] months. Currently doing [Y] conversions/day on [network]." |
| "What's your website?" | "[URL] — it's a review site for [vertical] in [GEO]." |

**Terminologie CPA** (demonstreaza cunoastere): EPC, conversion rate, ROI, CTR, landing page, split test, postback, S2S tracking, offer cap, shave rate.

**Eroare frecventa**: Nu raspunzi la telefon. MaxBounty suna O SINGURA DATA. Daca nu raspunzi → rejection. Noteaza numarul lor si tine telefonul aproape 48h dupa aplicare.

### Pas 7: STRATEGIE ALTERNATIVA PENTRU REJECTURI

Daca esti respins de tier-1:
1. **Nu reaplica imediat** — asteapta 60 zile
2. **Build track record pe tier-3**: CPAGrip, OGAds, AdWork Media (accepta 90%+ din aplicanti)
3. **Acumuleaza $500+ in conversii documentate** pe 30-60 zile
4. **Reaplica cu dovezi**: "Since my last application, I've generated $X in conversions on [Tier 3 network]. Here are my stats: [screenshot]"
5. **Alternativa**: cere unui affiliate existent sa te refere — referral-urile au 3x rata de acceptare

### Pas 8: CONSTRUIRE RELATIE CU AFFILIATE MANAGER

Post-acceptare, AM-ul devine resursa ta #1:
- **Email introductiv** (imediat dupa acceptare): cine esti, ce trafic ai, ce nise te intereseaza, si intreaba ce oferte recomanda
- **Intrebari valoroase**: "What are your top 3 converting offers for [GEO]?", "Any exclusive offers not listed in the dashboard?", "What EPC should I expect on [offer] with SMS traffic?"
- **Comunicare regulata**: 2-4x/luna. Trimite updates despre performanta ta — AM-ii aloca oferte exclusive afiliatilor activi
- **Nu cere bump payout in prima luna** — construieste 30+ zile de conversii inainte (M-112)

### Pas 9: SETUP MULTI-NETWORK TRACKING

Configureaza Voluum/BeMob/Binom pentru fiecare retea acceptata:
- Adauga fiecare retea ca "Affiliate Network" in tracker
- Configureaza postback URL per retea (S2S obligatoriu, nu pixel)
- Seteaza SubID passing: clickid, payout, conversion_type
- Test conversie: cere AM-ului sa fire manual postback → verifica in tracker
- Compara oferte: aceeasi oferta pe 2 retele → split test 50/50 → alege winner

### Pas 10: AUDIT TRIMESTRIAL PORTOFOLIU RETELE

La fiecare 90 zile:
- Verifica: toate conturile active? AM-uri responsive? Payouts la timp?
- Evalueaza: care retea are cel mai bun EPC real pe ofertele tale?
- Aplica la 1-2 retele noi (piata evolueaza, retele noi apar)
- Remove: retele cu zero activitate 90+ zile (curata portofoliul)
- Re-negociaza: payout bumps pe ofertele cu volum (M-112)
