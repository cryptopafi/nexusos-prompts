# LC-001 — Conduct a Coaching Conversation Using Intentional Change Theory

## Problema
Most coaching conversations fail because they lack a structured framework connecting the coachee's vision to sustained behavioral change.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Understanding of Intentional Change Theory (ICT) five discoveries

### Steps
1. Open the conversation by exploring the coachee's Ideal Self (personal vision and aspirations)
2. Facilitate honest assessment of the Real Self (current strengths and gaps)
3. Guide development of a Learning Agenda (specific change goals with experiments)
4. Identify opportunities to practice new behaviors in safe settings
5. Establish resonant relationships that support the change process
6. Schedule follow-up to review experiments and iterate

### Verification
- Conversation covers all five ICT discoveries
- Coachee leaves with at least one concrete experiment to try
