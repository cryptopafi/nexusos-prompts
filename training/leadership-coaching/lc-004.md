# LC-004 — Coach for the Ideal Self

## Problema
Without a compelling personal vision, coachees default to compliance-driven change that does not sustain.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- LC-001 completed
- LC-002 completed

### Steps
1. Guide the coachee through a personal vision exercise (10-year vision, values exploration)
2. Explore core identity: who do you want to be, not just what do you want to achieve
3. Connect vision to deeply held values and sense of purpose
4. Distinguish the Ideal Self from the 'Ought Self' (others' expectations)
5. Help coachee articulate their vision in vivid, present-tense language
6. Identify sources of hope and self-efficacy that make the vision feel attainable
7. Create a visual or written artifact that captures the Ideal Self

### Verification
- Coachee can articulate Ideal Self distinctly from Ought Self
- Vision statement is personally meaningful and energizing to the coachee
