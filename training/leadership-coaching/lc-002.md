# LC-002 — Balance Positive vs. Negative Emotional Attractors in Coaching

## Problema
Coaching that focuses too heavily on gaps and deficiencies triggers defensive responses that block genuine behavioral change.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- LC-001 completed

### Steps
1. Assess the current PEA/NEA balance in your coaching approach
2. Open sessions with strengths-based inquiry (Positive Emotional Attractor activation)
3. Use compassion, hope, and mindfulness to sustain PEA state
4. Introduce gaps and development areas only after PEA foundation is established
5. Monitor coachee's emotional state and adjust PEA/NEA balance in real-time
6. Target 3:1 or higher positive-to-negative ratio in coaching interactions
7. Close sessions on a PEA note with vision and possibility

### Verification
- Session maintains positive-to-negative ratio of at least 3:1
- Coachee reports feeling energized rather than drained after session
