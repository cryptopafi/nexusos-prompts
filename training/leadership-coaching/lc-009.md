# LC-009 — Identify and Leverage Coachable Moments

## Problema
Formal coaching sessions miss the majority of development opportunities that occur in everyday work interactions.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- LC-001 completed
- LC-003 completed

### Steps
1. Recognize triggers for coachable moments: success, failure, transition, conflict, feedback
2. Assess readiness: is the person open to reflection right now
3. Create a brief pause from action to enable reflection (even 2 minutes)
4. Ask one powerful question rather than giving advice
5. Listen to the response without fixing or judging
6. Help the person identify their own insight or next step
7. Follow up later to reinforce the learning

### Verification
- At least 3 coachable moments identified and leveraged per week
- Questions asked rather than advice given in the majority of moments
