# LC-008 — Design a Peer Coaching Program for Organizations

## Problema
Professional coaching is expensive and does not scale. Peer coaching programs extend coaching culture across an organization affordably.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- LC-001 through LC-003 completed

### Steps
1. Define program objectives: skill development, engagement, culture change, leadership pipeline
2. Design peer coaching pairing methodology (cross-functional, cross-level, skill-based)
3. Develop peer coaching training curriculum (listening, questioning, PEA/NEA, confidentiality)
4. Create session structure and conversation guides for peer coaches
5. Establish program cadence: session frequency, duration, total program length
6. Build measurement framework: participation, satisfaction, behavioral change, business impact
7. Design facilitator support: supervision sessions, troubleshooting, skill refreshers
8. Plan pilot, evaluate, and scale sequence

### Verification
- Training curriculum covers core coaching competencies
- Measurement framework includes leading and lagging indicators
