# LC-006 — Assess Coachee's Real Self (Strengths and Gaps)

## Problema
Inaccurate self-assessment prevents effective learning agendas and wastes coaching time on non-priority areas.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- LC-001 completed
- LC-004 completed

### Steps
1. Administer or review 360-degree feedback or multi-rater assessment
2. Facilitate balanced self-assessment: strengths first, then gaps
3. Compare self-perception with others' perceptions to identify blind spots
4. Identify confirmed strengths (self and others agree) and hidden strengths (others see, self doesn't)
5. Identify overestimated skills (self sees, others don't) and agreed development areas
6. Prioritize gaps that matter most for the Ideal Self vision
7. Frame gaps as learning opportunities, not deficiencies

### Verification
- Real Self assessment includes multi-source feedback, not just self-report
- Strengths and gaps mapped against Ideal Self priorities
