# LC-005 — Apply Cognitive and Emotional Empathy in Coaching

## Problema
Coaches who rely on cognitive empathy alone miss the emotional resonance that drives trust and openness in coaching relationships.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- LC-003 completed

### Steps
1. Practice cognitive empathy: understand the coachee's perspective and mental model
2. Practice emotional empathy: feel and resonate with the coachee's emotional state
3. Calibrate empathic responses to avoid over-identification (empathic distress)
4. Use empathic attunement to build psychological safety
5. Notice when empathy is blocked (judgment, advice-giving impulse, discomfort with emotions)
6. Practice empathic accuracy: check your understanding with the coachee
7. Maintain appropriate boundaries while being emotionally present

### Verification
- Coachee reports feeling understood (explicit check)
- Coach can articulate both cognitive and emotional dimensions of coachee's experience
