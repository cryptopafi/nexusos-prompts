# LC-003 — Apply Active Listening and Noticing Techniques in Coaching

## Problema
Coaches who talk more than they listen miss critical signals and fail to create the psychological safety needed for change.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Basic coaching skills

### Steps
1. Practice full attention: eliminate distractions, maintain appropriate eye contact
2. Use reflective listening: paraphrase content and reflect emotions back
3. Notice non-verbal cues: body language, tone shifts, energy changes
4. Ask open-ended questions that follow the coachee's lead (not your agenda)
5. Use strategic silence to create space for deeper reflection
6. Notice what is NOT being said (topics avoided, deflections, incongruencies)
7. Summarize key themes at natural transition points

### Verification
- Coach talk-time under 30% of session
- At least 3 non-verbal observations noted and explored
