# DE-054 — Implement Data Marts for Business Analytics

## Problema
Enterprise data warehouses are too broad for individual teams; data marts provide focused, team-specific analytical views.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Data warehouse with populated data
- Business requirements from the target team

### Steps
1. Identify a specific business team and their analytical needs
2. Design a data mart schema tailored to the team's KPIs
3. Extract relevant data from the central data warehouse
4. Transform data to match the data mart schema
5. Load data into the data mart
6. Set up refresh schedules to keep the mart current
7. Validate with the business team using sample reports

### Verification
- Data mart contains correct, relevant data
- Business team can run their reports against the mart
