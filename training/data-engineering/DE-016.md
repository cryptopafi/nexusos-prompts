# DE-016 — Design ER Diagrams and Map Entities to Tables

## Problema
Database design starts with understanding entities and their relationships; poor ER design leads to data anomalies.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Understanding of the business domain
- ER diagram tool (draw.io, Lucidchart)

### Steps
1. Identify all entities in the business domain
2. Define attributes for each entity
3. Identify relationships (one-to-one, one-to-many, many-to-many)
4. Draw the ER diagram with proper notation
5. Map entities to database tables
6. Map relationships to foreign keys and junction tables
7. Validate the design against business requirements

### Verification
- ER diagram accurately represents all entities and relationships
- Mapping to tables follows standard conventions
