# BS-035 — Fit Bayesian AR(1) Model

## Problema
Frequentist AR models provide no uncertainty on autoregressive coefficients; Bayesian AR does.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- R
- JAGS
- BS-020 and BS-034 completed

### Steps
1. Write AR(1) model: `y[t] ~ dnorm(mu + phi*(y[t-1] - mu), tau)`.
2. Specify priors: `phi ~ dunif(-1, 1)` (stationarity), `mu ~ dnorm(0, 0.001)`, `tau ~ dgamma(0.01, 0.01)`.
3. Prepare data: time series vector and length.
4. Run MCMC in JAGS with 3 chains.
5. Check convergence and stationarity constraint (|phi| < 1).
6. Compute posterior probability that series is stationary.
7. Compare posterior phi with OLS estimate from `ar()`.

### Verification
- Chain converges with posterior phi within (-1, 1).
- Posterior mean of phi close to frequentist estimate.
