# BS-027 — Perform Posterior Predictive Simulation

## Problema
Posterior summaries describe parameters; posterior prediction describes observable future data outcomes.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- R or Python
- fitted Bayesian model

### Steps
1. For each posterior sample of parameters, simulate a new dataset.
2. Collect predictive samples: `y_pred[s] ~ f(theta[s])` for s = 1..S.
3. Compute predictive mean and interval from the samples.
4. Plot posterior predictive distribution vs observed data.
5. Compute predictive p-value for a test statistic.
6. Use posterior prediction for decision making under uncertainty.

### Verification
- Posterior predictive distribution covers observed data.
- Predictive intervals properly wider than credible intervals.
