# BS-030 — Apply Mixture Models for Density Estimation

## Problema
Parametric distributions cannot capture multi-modal data; mixtures provide flexible density approximation.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- R or Python
- BS-028 or BS-029 completed

### Steps
1. Fit mixture models with K = 2, 3, 4, 5 components.
2. For each K, compute BIC or DIC to assess fit.
3. Select K that minimizes information criterion.
4. Plot fitted density for selected K overlaid on data histogram.
5. Compare with kernel density estimate as a non-parametric benchmark.
6. Assess quality: QQ-plot of data quantiles vs fitted mixture quantiles.

### Verification
- Selected K produces good density fit.
- QQ-plot shows approximate linearity.
