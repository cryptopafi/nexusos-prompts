# BS-010 — Fit Bayesian Models for Exponential Data

## Problema
Exponential data (wait times, lifetimes) requires Gamma conjugate priors for rate inference.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- R or Python
- BS-004 completed

### Steps
1. Model data as Exponential(lambda) with Gamma(a, b) prior on lambda.
2. Observe data: x_1, ..., x_n (positive real values).
3. Derive posterior: Gamma(a + n, b + sum(x)).
4. Compute posterior mean and credible interval for the rate.
5. Transform to inference on the mean lifetime 1/lambda.
6. Compare Bayesian estimate with MLE = n/sum(x).

### Verification
- Posterior correctly updates with exponential sufficient statistics.
- Bayesian and MLE estimates converge with large n.
