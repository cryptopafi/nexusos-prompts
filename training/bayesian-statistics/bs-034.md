# BS-034 — Compute and Interpret ACF and PACF for Time Series

## Problema
Without ACF/PACF analysis, autoregressive model order selection is pure guesswork.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- R installed
- time series dataset

### Steps
1. Load time series data and plot: `plot(ts_data)`.
2. Compute ACF: `acf(ts_data)` and examine decay pattern.
3. Compute PACF: `pacf(ts_data)` and identify cutoff lag.
4. Interpret: slow ACF decay suggests differencing needed; PACF cutoff suggests AR order.
5. AR(p) signature: PACF cuts off after lag p, ACF decays.
6. MA(q) signature: ACF cuts off after lag q, PACF decays.
7. Use results to propose candidate models for Bayesian fitting.

### Verification
- ACF and PACF plots generated with confidence bands.
- Candidate model order identified from PACF cutoff.
