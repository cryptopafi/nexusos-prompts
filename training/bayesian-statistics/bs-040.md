# BS-040 — Build Seasonal NDLM with Fourier Components

## Problema
Time series with seasonal patterns need explicit periodic components in the state-space model.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- R
- dlm package
- BS-038 completed

### Steps
1. Identify seasonal period s from ACF or domain knowledge.
2. Add Fourier harmonics: `F_t` includes sin(2*pi*j*t/s) and cos(2*pi*j*t/s) for j=1..J.
3. Build seasonal DLM: `dlmModTrig(s=12)` for monthly seasonality.
4. Combine with trend: `dlmModPoly(2) + dlmModTrig(s=12)`.
5. Fit with MLE: `dlmMLE(y, parm, build_fn)` to estimate V and W.
6. Run filter and smoother on the fitted model.
7. Decompose: extract trend and seasonal components from smoothed states.

### Verification
- Model captures seasonal pattern in the data.
- Decomposition shows interpretable trend and seasonal components.
