# SE-025 — Design AI-Driven Sales Campaigns

## Problema
Sales campaigns are generic and manual, missing the personalization and efficiency AI can provide.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- AI tool access (ChatGPT, Jasper, or similar)
- Campaign goals defined
- Contact list

### Steps
1. Define campaign objective: new pipeline, reactivation, upsell, or event-driven
2. Use AI to generate 3 email variations targeting different personas or pain points
3. Use AI to write personalized opening lines based on LinkedIn profiles or company news
4. Set up A/B test structure: test subject lines, CTAs, or send times
5. Configure email sequencing tool (Outreach, Apollo, HubSpot) with AI-generated content
6. Launch campaign to a test segment of 100 contacts
7. Analyze results after 5 days: open rate, reply rate, meeting rate; iterate with AI

### Verification
- 3 AI-generated email variants created
- Campaign launched to 100+ contacts
- Results analyzed with metrics
