# SE-034 — Conduct Real-Time Market Analysis with AI

## Problema
Sales teams react to market changes too slowly because they rely on quarterly reports instead of real-time data.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- AI research tools (Perplexity, ChatGPT)
- Industry news sources
- Competitive intelligence tools

### Steps
1. Set up AI-powered alerts for competitor moves, industry news, and regulatory changes
2. Use AI to analyze competitor pricing, positioning, and product launches weekly
3. Create a competitive battle card updated in real-time with AI-sourced intelligence
4. Monitor customer sentiment on social media and review sites using AI tools
5. Generate a weekly market pulse report: key trends, threats, opportunities
6. Share market intelligence with the sales team in a 15-min weekly briefing
7. Adjust sales messaging and positioning based on real-time market shifts

### Verification
- AI alerts configured
- Battle card created
- Weekly market pulse report delivered
