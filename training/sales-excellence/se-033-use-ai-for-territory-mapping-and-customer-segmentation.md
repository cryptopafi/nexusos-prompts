# SE-033 — Use AI for Territory Mapping and Customer Segmentation

## Problema
Territory planning is manual and misses data-driven opportunities for optimal coverage and segmentation.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Customer data export
- AI/analytics tool (Tableau, Power BI, or AI mapping tool)
- Territory boundaries

### Steps
1. Export customer data: location, revenue, industry, growth rate, last purchase date
2. Use AI clustering to segment customers into behavioral groups (e.g., high-growth, at-risk, dormant)
3. Visualize segments on a map to identify geographic clusters and white space
4. Calculate territory potential: total addressable market per segment per area
5. Rebalance territory assignments based on AI-recommended optimal coverage
6. Create segment-specific engagement strategies
7. Present territory plan to management with data-backed rationale

### Verification
- AI segmentation completed
- Territory map visualized
- Rebalanced plan presented
