# SE-035 — Generate AI-Powered Personalized Sales Approaches

## Problema
Reps use generic pitches because crafting personalized approaches for each prospect is too time-consuming.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- AI writing tool
- Prospect data from CRM and LinkedIn
- Product knowledge base

### Steps
1. For each target prospect, gather data: role, company challenges, recent news, LinkedIn activity
2. Feed prospect data into AI with prompt: 'Generate a personalized sales approach for [role] at [company] facing [challenge]'
3. Have AI create: personalized email, call opening script, and LinkedIn message
4. Review and edit AI output for accuracy and tone (never send unreviewed AI content)
5. A/B test AI-personalized vs. template-based outreach on 50 prospects each
6. Measure response rates and meeting conversion rates
7. Build a prompt library of your best-performing AI prompts for the team

### Verification
- AI-personalized outreach for 10+ prospects
- A/B test completed
- Prompt library started
