# SE-024 — Handle B2C Objections and Close

## Problema
B2C reps lose sales at the last moment because they cannot handle price, timing, or trust objections.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- B2C sales experience
- Objection handling basics (SE-006)

### Steps
1. List the top 5 B2C objections: 'too expensive', 'need to think about it', 'saw it cheaper elsewhere', 'not sure it's right', 'bad timing'
2. For 'too expensive': break price into daily cost or compare to cost of not buying
3. For 'need to think about it': ask 'What specifically would you like to think about?' to surface the real objection
4. For 'saw it cheaper': differentiate on value, warranty, service, or experience
5. For 'not sure': offer a trial, guarantee, or social proof (reviews, ratings)
6. Practice the assumptive close: 'Shall I wrap this up for you?' or 'Would you like delivery or pickup?'
7. Role-play all 5 objections with a partner and refine responses

### Verification
- 5 objection responses written
- Role-play completed
- At least one objection handled live
