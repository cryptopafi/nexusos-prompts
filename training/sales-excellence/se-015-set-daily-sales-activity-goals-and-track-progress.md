# SE-015 — Set Daily Sales Activity Goals and Track Progress

## Problema
Reps lack daily structure, leading to inconsistent effort and unpredictable results.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Pipeline targets
- CRM access
- Calendar

### Steps
1. Calculate backward from your monthly quota: deals needed, proposals needed, demos needed, calls needed
2. Divide monthly numbers by 20 working days to get daily minimums
3. Set daily goals: X calls, Y emails, Z demos, W proposals
4. Create a daily scorecard (spreadsheet or CRM dashboard)
5. At start of each day, review yesterday's scorecard and plan today's priorities
6. At end of each day, log actuals vs. goals on the scorecard
7. Weekly: review 5-day trend and adjust if consistently over or under

### Verification
- Daily activity goals calculated from quota
- Scorecard created
- 5 consecutive days tracked
