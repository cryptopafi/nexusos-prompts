# SE-010 — Apply Solution Selling / Consultative Selling in B2B

## Problema
B2B reps pitch products instead of diagnosing business problems, losing to consultative competitors.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- B2B sales experience
- Discovery skills (SE-004)
- Industry knowledge

### Steps
1. Research the prospect's company: annual report, press releases, industry challenges
2. Identify 3 potential business problems your solution addresses
3. Open the conversation with a hypothesis: 'Companies in your space often struggle with X. Is that relevant?'
4. If confirmed, deep-dive into the business impact: cost, time, risk, revenue implications
5. Collaboratively define the desired future state with the buyer
6. Map your solution capabilities to the gap between current and desired state
7. Present a business case showing ROI, not just features

### Verification
- Business problem hypothesis validated
- Business impact quantified
- ROI-based proposal delivered
