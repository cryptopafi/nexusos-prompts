# SE-026 — Integrate AI into CRM Systems for Sales

## Problema
CRM data is underutilized because AI-powered features (lead scoring, next-best-action) are not activated.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- CRM with AI capabilities or integration options
- Admin access to CRM

### Steps
1. Audit your CRM's AI features: lead scoring, deal insights, activity capture, recommendations
2. Enable AI lead scoring and configure scoring criteria (engagement, fit, behavior)
3. Set up AI-powered activity capture to auto-log emails and calls
4. Configure next-best-action recommendations for reps (e.g., 'Call this lead — engagement spike detected')
5. Train the team on interpreting AI scores and recommendations
6. Run a 30-day pilot comparing AI-assisted reps vs. control group
7. Measure impact on conversion rate, response time, and pipeline velocity

### Verification
- AI features enabled in CRM
- Team trained on AI tools
- 30-day pilot results documented
