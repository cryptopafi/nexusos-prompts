# SE-011 — Leverage CRM for Strategic Client Management

## Problema
CRM is used as a data dump instead of a strategic tool for managing and growing client relationships.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- CRM access with populated accounts
- Client portfolio assigned

### Steps
1. Audit your CRM: ensure all key accounts have complete contact info, notes, and activity history
2. Tag each account with relationship health: Green (strong), Yellow (at risk), Red (churning)
3. For Yellow and Red accounts, create a recovery plan with specific touchpoints
4. Set up CRM alerts for accounts with no activity in 30+ days
5. Create a dashboard showing: pipeline value, activity volume, win rate, average deal cycle
6. Schedule a weekly 30-min CRM review to update account health and plan outreach
7. Use CRM data to identify cross-sell and upsell opportunities in Green accounts

### Verification
- All key accounts health-tagged
- Dashboard created with 4+ metrics
- Weekly CRM review scheduled
