# SE-014 — Research and Qualify Cold Leads

## Problema
Reps call cold leads without research, wasting time on unqualified prospects and making poor first impressions.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Cold lead list
- LinkedIn access
- CRM access

### Steps
1. For each lead, research on LinkedIn: role, tenure, company size, recent posts
2. Check company website: products, news, job postings (indicators of growth or pain)
3. Apply BANT qualification: Budget (can they afford it?), Authority (decision maker?), Need (pain exists?), Timeline (urgency?)
4. Score each lead 1-4 on BANT criteria (4 = strong match on all)
5. Prioritize leads scoring 3-4 for immediate outreach
6. For leads scoring 1-2, add to nurture sequence or disqualify
7. Document qualification notes in CRM for each lead

### Verification
- All leads researched (LinkedIn + company)
- BANT scores assigned
- Priority list created
