# SE-031 — Qualify and Score Leads Systematically

## Problema
All leads are treated equally, so reps spend time on low-quality leads while hot ones go cold.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Lead list
- CRM access
- Qualification framework

### Steps
1. Define lead scoring criteria: demographic fit (25%), behavioral engagement (25%), BANT qualification (50%)
2. Assign point values: e.g., decision maker = 20pts, visited pricing page = 15pts, budget confirmed = 25pts
3. Configure scoring in CRM or build a scoring spreadsheet
4. Score all existing leads in pipeline
5. Set score thresholds: Hot (80+), Warm (50-79), Cold (below 50)
6. Route Hot leads to immediate outreach, Warm to nurture, Cold to disqualify or park
7. Review and recalibrate scoring quarterly based on conversion data

### Verification
- Scoring model defined with criteria and points
- All leads scored
- Threshold-based routing implemented
