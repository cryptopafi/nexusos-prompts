# SE-023 — Execute Consultative Selling in B2C

## Problema
B2C reps push products without understanding the customer's personal situation and needs.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- B2C sales environment
- Questioning skills (SE-004)

### Steps
1. Shift mindset from 'selling product' to 'solving customer problem'
2. Open conversations with lifestyle questions: 'What are you trying to achieve with this purchase?'
3. Listen for emotional drivers (fear, aspiration, frustration) behind the stated need
4. Match the customer to the right product/tier based on their actual needs, not the most expensive option
5. Explain your recommendation with a clear 'because': 'I recommend X because you mentioned Y'
6. Offer alternatives to show you're advising, not pushing: 'Another option would be...'
7. Follow up post-purchase to confirm satisfaction and build referral potential

### Verification
- Needs-based recommendation made
- Customer confirmed the recommendation matched their needs
- Follow-up completed
