# SE-020 — Build a B2C Ideal Customer Profile and Buyer Persona

## Problema
B2C sales efforts target everyone, wasting budget on low-conversion segments.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Customer data or market research
- Product/service definition

### Steps
1. Analyze existing customer data: demographics, purchase behavior, lifetime value
2. Identify top 20% of customers by revenue and find common traits
3. Define ICP with demographics (age, income, location) and psychographics (values, lifestyle, pain points)
4. Create 2-3 buyer personas with names, photos, and narrative descriptions
5. For each persona, document: goals, frustrations, preferred channels, purchase triggers
6. Validate personas with 5 customer interviews or survey data
7. Share personas with marketing and align on messaging per persona

### Verification
- ICP document created
- 2-3 personas with full profiles
- Validation data collected
