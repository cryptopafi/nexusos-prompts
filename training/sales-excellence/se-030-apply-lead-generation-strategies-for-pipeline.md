# SE-030 — Apply Lead Generation Strategies for Pipeline

## Problema
Pipeline dries up because reps rely on inbound leads and lack proactive generation strategies.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- ICP defined
- Multi-channel access (email, phone, social, events)

### Steps
1. Map 5 lead generation channels: inbound content, outbound email, cold calling, referrals, events/webinars
2. For each channel, define expected lead volume and conversion rate
3. Create a referral request template and ask 10 existing clients for introductions
4. Set up a LinkedIn content strategy: post 2x/week to attract inbound interest
5. Partner with marketing on a lead magnet (whitepaper, webinar) targeting your ICP
6. Execute a blitz week: one intensive week using all 5 channels simultaneously
7. Measure leads generated per channel and calculate cost-per-lead

### Verification
- 5 channels mapped with targets
- Referral requests sent
- Blitz week completed with metrics
