# SE-022 — Apply Value-Based Selling Techniques

## Problema
Reps compete on price because they fail to articulate and quantify the value their solution delivers.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Product knowledge
- Customer business understanding
- ROI calculation ability

### Steps
1. Identify the 3 primary value drivers your product delivers (cost savings, revenue increase, risk reduction)
2. For each driver, create a quantification formula (e.g., hours saved x hourly rate x 12 months)
3. During discovery, gather the data inputs needed for your value formulas
4. Build a simple value calculator (spreadsheet) the buyer can see and modify
5. Present value in the buyer's language: 'This saves your team 200 hours/year, worth $50K'
6. Anchor price to value: 'For a $50K return, the investment is $10K — a 5x ROI'
7. Document value-selling wins to build a case study library

### Verification
- Value calculator created
- Value presented in at least one deal
- ROI framing used in proposal
