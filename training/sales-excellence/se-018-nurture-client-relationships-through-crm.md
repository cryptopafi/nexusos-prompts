# SE-018 — Nurture Client Relationships Through CRM

## Problema
Existing clients are ignored after the sale, leading to churn and missed expansion revenue.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- CRM with client data
- Post-sale client list

### Steps
1. Segment clients by value tier and last contact date in CRM
2. Create touchpoint cadence: Tier 1 = monthly, Tier 2 = quarterly, Tier 3 = semi-annual
3. Set up automated CRM reminders for each touchpoint cadence
4. Prepare a value-add touchpoint library: industry insights, product tips, referral requests
5. Execute first round of touchpoints: personalized email or call to top 10 clients
6. Log each touchpoint in CRM with notes on client sentiment and opportunities
7. Review client health monthly using CRM activity reports

### Verification
- Client tiers assigned
- Touchpoint cadence set with reminders
- 10 client touchpoints executed
