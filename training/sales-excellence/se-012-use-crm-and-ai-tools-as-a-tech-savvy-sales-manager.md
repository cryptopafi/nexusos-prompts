# SE-012 — Use CRM and AI Tools as a Tech-Savvy Sales Manager

## Problema
Sales managers underutilize CRM analytics and AI tools, making decisions on gut feel instead of data.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- CRM admin access
- AI tool access (Gong, Clari, or similar)
- Team pipeline data

### Steps
1. Review your CRM's built-in analytics: pipeline reports, activity reports, forecast reports
2. Identify 3 key metrics for your team: conversion rate, average deal size, sales cycle length
3. Set up automated weekly reports for these metrics sent to your inbox
4. Integrate an AI tool (Gong/Chorus for call analysis, Clari for forecasting)
5. Use AI insights to identify which deals need manager intervention
6. Create a coaching cadence: review AI-flagged deals in weekly 1:1s with reps
7. Track improvement in team metrics monthly and adjust coaching focus

### Verification
- 3 automated reports configured
- AI tool integrated
- Coaching cadence established
