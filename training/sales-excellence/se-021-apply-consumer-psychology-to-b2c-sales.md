# SE-021 — Apply Consumer Psychology to B2C Sales

## Problema
B2C sales ignore psychological triggers, relying on discounts instead of persuasion.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- B2C buyer personas (SE-020)
- Sales or marketing channel access

### Steps
1. Study Cialdini's 6 principles: Reciprocity, Commitment, Social Proof, Authority, Liking, Scarcity
2. For each principle, brainstorm 2 tactics relevant to your product/audience
3. Implement Social Proof: add testimonials, review counts, or 'X people bought this' to sales materials
4. Implement Scarcity: create limited-time offers or limited-quantity alerts
5. Implement Reciprocity: offer a free resource or sample before asking for the sale
6. A/B test one psychological trigger vs. control in your next campaign
7. Measure conversion lift and document which principles work for your audience

### Verification
- 6 principles mapped to tactics
- 3 principles implemented
- A/B test results documented
