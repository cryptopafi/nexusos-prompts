# SE-013 — Build a Sales Prospecting Plan (Email + Telephone)

## Problema
Reps prospect randomly without a structured multi-channel plan, resulting in low conversion.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- ICP defined (SE-002)
- Email and phone access
- CRM access

### Steps
1. Define your weekly prospecting targets: X new contacts, Y emails, Z calls
2. Create an email sequence: 3 emails over 10 days (intro, value-add, break-up)
3. Write a phone script with opening hook, value prop, and call-to-action
4. Build a daily prospecting block: 1 hour for email, 1 hour for calls
5. Prepare a voicemail script (under 30 seconds) with callback reason
6. Execute one full day of the plan: send emails, make calls, leave voicemails
7. Log all activity in CRM and calculate contact rate and response rate

### Verification
- Email sequence written (3 emails)
- Phone script ready
- One full day executed and logged
