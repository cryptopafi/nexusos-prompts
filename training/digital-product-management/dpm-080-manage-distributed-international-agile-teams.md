# DPM-080 — Manage Distributed/International Agile Teams

## Problema
Distributed teams lose agile benefits because ceremonies and collaboration don't work across time zones.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Distributed team across 2+ time zones
- Video conferencing tools
- Async collaboration tools

### Steps
1. Map team time zones and find overlapping hours (minimum 2-3 hours overlap needed)
2. Schedule ceremonies during overlap hours; if impossible, rotate meeting times fairly
3. Implement async standups: each member posts update in Slack/Teams by their morning
4. Use visual collaboration tools (Miro, FigJam) for remote planning and retros
5. Document everything: decisions, discussions, and context that would be overheard in an office
6. Create 'working agreements' specific to distributed work: response time expectations, camera policies, documentation standards
7. Run quarterly in-person or extended virtual team-building sessions

### Verification
- Overlap hours mapped and ceremonies scheduled
- Async standup running
- Distributed working agreements documented
