# DPM-035 — Formulate Persona/JTBD Hypothesis for Testing

## Problema
Persona and JTBD assumptions go untested, leading to products built for imaginary users.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Personas (DPM-023) or JTBD statements (DPM-025)
- Testing methodology knowledge

### Steps
1. Select the persona or JTBD with the highest uncertainty
2. Identify the riskiest assumption: 'We assume [persona] actually [behavior/need]'
3. Formulate a testable hypothesis: 'If [persona] truly [need], then [observable behavior] when [test condition]'
4. Define the test method: interview (qual), survey (quant), or behavior observation
5. Set sample size and success criteria: 'Validated if X out of Y participants demonstrate [behavior]'
6. Run the test within 2 weeks
7. Analyze results: confirmed, partially confirmed, or invalidated; update persona/JTBD accordingly

### Verification
- Hypothesis formulated with success criteria
- Test executed
- Persona/JTBD updated based on results
