# DPM-034 — Use Day-in-the-Life to Drive Empathy

## Problema
Teams lack empathy for users because they never experience the user's daily reality.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Target user access
- Observation permission
- Note-taking tools

### Steps
1. Identify a user whose daily experience you want to understand
2. Arrange a 2-4 hour shadowing session (in person or remote screen share)
3. Observe without interrupting: watch how they work, what tools they use, where they struggle
4. Note workarounds: what manual steps do they take because the product doesn't support them?
5. Note emotions: where do they express frustration, confusion, or delight?
6. After shadowing, write a 'Day in the Life' narrative from the user's perspective
7. Share the narrative with the team and identify product implications

### Verification
- Shadowing session completed
- Day-in-the-Life narrative written
- Shared with team with product implications
