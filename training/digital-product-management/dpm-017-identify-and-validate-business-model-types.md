# DPM-017 — Identify and Validate Business Model Types

## Problema
PMs default to familiar business models without evaluating alternatives that might be more profitable.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Product concept
- Market research
- Competitive analysis

### Steps
1. Study the major business model types: SaaS, marketplace, e-commerce, advertising, licensing, hardware+subscription
2. For each type, understand the key success metrics and typical margins
3. Evaluate which 2-3 models could apply to your product
4. For each candidate model, map out: revenue source, cost structure, scaling economics
5. Score each model on: market fit, competitive advantage, scalability, profitability potential
6. Select the best model and document the rationale
7. Design a small experiment to validate the chosen model (e.g., pricing test, willingness-to-pay survey)

### Verification
- 3 business model options evaluated
- Best model selected with rationale
- Validation experiment designed
