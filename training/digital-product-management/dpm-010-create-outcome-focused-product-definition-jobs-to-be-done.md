# DPM-010 — Create Outcome-Focused Product Definition (Jobs-To-Be-Done)

## Problema
Products are defined by features instead of the outcomes customers are trying to achieve.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Customer research data
- Product concept

### Steps
1. Identify the main 'job' your customer is trying to get done (functional, emotional, social)
2. Write the job statement: 'When [situation], I want to [motivation], so I can [outcome]'
3. Map the job process: the steps customers take to get the job done today
4. Identify where customers are underserved (steps that are slow, expensive, or error-prone)
5. Define your product's value proposition in terms of the job: 'Our product helps [customer] [job] by [how]'
6. Prioritize features by their impact on job completion success
7. Validate the job statement with 5 customer interviews

### Verification
- JTBD statement written
- Job process mapped
- Validation interviews completed
