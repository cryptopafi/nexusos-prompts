# DPM-067 — Run Scrum

## Problema
Teams claim to do Scrum but skip key ceremonies, losing the framework's benefits.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Scrum knowledge
- Product backlog
- Development team

### Steps
1. Set up the Scrum framework: 2-week sprints, Product Owner, Scrum Master, Development Team
2. Sprint Planning (2 hrs): select stories from the backlog, define sprint goal, estimate work
3. Daily Standup (15 min): each member shares yesterday/today/blockers
4. Ensure the team is working toward the sprint goal, not just completing random stories
5. Sprint Review (1 hr): demo completed work to stakeholders, collect feedback
6. Sprint Retrospective (1 hr): what went well, what didn't, what to improve
7. Maintain and refine the product backlog: groom top items 1-2 sprints ahead

### Verification
- All Scrum ceremonies executed for 2+ sprints
- Sprint goal achieved
- Retrospective improvements implemented
