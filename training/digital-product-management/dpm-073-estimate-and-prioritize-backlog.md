# DPM-073 — Estimate and Prioritize Backlog

## Problema
Backlog items have no estimates, making sprint planning and roadmap commitments unreliable.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Product backlog
- Development team
- Estimation framework knowledge

### Steps
1. Choose an estimation method: story points (relative) or t-shirt sizing (quick)
2. Run an estimation session: present each story, team discusses and estimates together
3. Use planning poker for story points: each member reveals estimate simultaneously to avoid anchoring
4. If estimates diverge widely, discuss: the highest and lowest estimators explain their reasoning
5. After estimation, sort the backlog by priority using value/effort ratio
6. Identify 'iceberg' stories: simple on surface but complex underneath; add a spike or discovery task
7. Validate estimates retrospectively: compare estimated vs. actual effort each sprint

### Verification
- Top 20 backlog items estimated
- Backlog sorted by value/effort
- Estimation accuracy tracked
