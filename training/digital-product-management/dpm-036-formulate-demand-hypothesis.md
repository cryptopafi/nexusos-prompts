# DPM-036 — Formulate Demand Hypothesis

## Problema
Teams assume demand exists without testing whether customers actually want and will pay for the product.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Product concept
- Target market definition
- Pricing model draft

### Steps
1. State the demand hypothesis: 'We believe [segment] will [sign up / pay / pre-order] for [product] at [price]'
2. Define demand signals: email signup rate, landing page conversion, letter of intent, pre-order count
3. Set the minimum viable demand threshold: 'Demand is validated if [X% conversion / Y signups in Z days]'
4. Choose a test method: landing page test, crowdfunding, pre-sale, or concierge
5. Build the test asset (landing page, ad campaign, or email sequence)
6. Run the demand test for a defined period (typically 1-2 weeks)
7. Analyze results against threshold and decide: proceed, iterate on positioning, or pivot

### Verification
- Demand hypothesis stated with threshold
- Test asset built and launched
- Results analyzed with decision
