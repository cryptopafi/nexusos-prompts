# DPM-063 — Build AI Product Portfolio

## Problema
AI initiatives are scattered across teams without a coherent portfolio strategy tied to product goals.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- AI capability inventory
- Product strategy
- Resource allocation data

### Steps
1. Inventory all current and planned AI initiatives across the product
2. Classify each by type: prediction, automation, personalization, generation, optimization
3. Assess each initiative on: user value, data readiness, model maturity, maintenance cost
4. Map initiatives to product strategy goals: which AI projects drive which strategic outcomes?
5. Identify redundancies: are multiple teams solving similar AI problems?
6. Create a portfolio view: invest (scale), maintain (keep running), sunset (deprecate)
7. Set portfolio review cadence: quarterly, with metrics on AI model performance and business impact

### Verification
- AI portfolio inventory completed
- Initiatives classified and assessed
- Portfolio strategy with review cadence established
