# DPM-056 — Design and Run Demand Experiments

## Problema
Product teams guess at demand instead of running structured experiments to measure willingness to buy.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Demand hypothesis (DPM-036)
- Ad platform or landing page tool
- Analytics

### Steps
1. Design 3 demand experiment variants: different value propositions, price points, or audiences
2. Build landing pages or ad creatives for each variant
3. Set up conversion tracking: click-through, signup, pre-order, or payment
4. Allocate equal budget and traffic to each variant
5. Run experiments for minimum 1 week or until statistical significance
6. Analyze results: which variant had highest conversion and lowest CAC?
7. Use winning variant insights to refine product positioning and pricing

### Verification
- 3 variants tested
- Statistical significance reached
- Winning variant identified with metrics
