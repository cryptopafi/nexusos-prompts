# DPM-024 — Map Persona Think-See-Feel-Do Framework

## Problema
Teams understand who their users are but not what drives their behavior at each interaction point.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Personas created (DPM-023)
- Customer journey data

### Steps
1. Select a persona and a key product interaction to map
2. Think: what is the persona thinking at this moment? (goals, questions, concerns)
3. See: what does the persona see? (UI, competitors, social proof, ads)
4. Feel: what emotions are present? (frustration, excitement, confusion, trust)
5. Do: what action does the persona take? (click, abandon, ask for help, compare)
6. Identify gaps between Think/Feel and See/Do (e.g., they feel confused but we show no guidance)
7. Design improvements that align what the persona sees and can do with what they think and feel

### Verification
- Think-See-Feel-Do map completed for 1+ persona
- Gaps identified
- Improvements designed
