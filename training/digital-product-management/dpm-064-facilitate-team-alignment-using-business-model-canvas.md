# DPM-064 — Facilitate Team Alignment Using Business Model Canvas

## Problema
Teams disagree on product direction because they lack a shared understanding of the business model.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Business Model Canvas template
- Cross-functional team (PM, engineering, design, business)
- 2-hour workshop time

### Steps
1. Schedule a 2-hour BMC workshop with all key team members
2. Brief participants: send BMC template and pre-read on the 9 building blocks
3. Facilitate block by block: start with Value Proposition, then Customer Segments
4. For each block, have team members write ideas on sticky notes independently (3 min), then discuss (7 min)
5. Capture disagreements explicitly: where the team doesn't align, document both views
6. After completing all 9 blocks, review the full canvas for coherence
7. Assign owners to resolve open disagreements within 1 week and share the final BMC

### Verification
- BMC workshop completed with full team
- All 9 blocks filled
- Disagreements documented with resolution owners
