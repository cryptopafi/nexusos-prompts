# DPM-027 — Conduct User Research Interview

## Problema
PMs avoid or poorly execute user interviews, building on assumptions instead of evidence.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Interview guide (DPM-026)
- Recruited participants
- Recording tool

### Steps
1. Send a pre-interview brief to participant: purpose, duration, consent for recording
2. Set up recording (Zoom, Otter.ai, or phone); get verbal consent at start
3. Follow the interview guide but be flexible to follow interesting threads
4. Practice active listening: let silences happen, don't fill them with your opinions
5. Take notes on surprising statements, emotional reactions, and workarounds
6. Close with: 'Is there anything I didn't ask that you think is important?'
7. Within 24 hours, write a structured interview summary: key insights, quotes, implications

### Verification
- Interview conducted and recorded
- Summary written within 24 hours
- Key insights extracted
