# DPM-066 — Create Agile Team Charter

## Problema
Agile teams lack shared agreements on how they work, leading to conflict and inconsistent practices.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Agile team members
- 2-hour workshop time
- Collaboration tool

### Steps
1. Facilitate a team charter workshop (2 hours, all team members present)
2. Define team mission: 'We exist to [purpose] for [customer/stakeholder]'
3. Agree on working agreements: core hours, communication norms, meeting rules, definition of done
4. Define roles and responsibilities within the team (avoid assumptions)
5. Set team values: 3-5 principles that guide behavior (e.g., 'We prefer shipping over debating')
6. Agree on conflict resolution process: how disagreements are raised and resolved
7. Post the charter visibly (physical wall or digital wiki) and review quarterly

### Verification
- Team charter document created
- Working agreements defined
- Charter visible and reviewed quarterly
