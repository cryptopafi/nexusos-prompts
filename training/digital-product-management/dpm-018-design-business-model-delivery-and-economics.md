# DPM-018 — Design Business Model Delivery and Economics

## Problema
The business model looks good on paper but fails in delivery because operational economics are not planned.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Chosen business model
- Operational data
- Financial projections

### Steps
1. Map the value delivery chain: how does value get from creation to customer?
2. Identify all cost centers in the delivery chain: development, infrastructure, support, marketing, operations
3. Calculate gross margin: (revenue - cost of goods sold) / revenue
4. Calculate contribution margin per customer: revenue per customer - variable costs per customer
5. Identify the path to breakeven: how many customers or transactions needed?
6. Model three scenarios: conservative, base, optimistic with different growth and cost assumptions
7. Create a financial dashboard tracking actual vs. projected delivery economics monthly

### Verification
- Delivery chain mapped
- Margins calculated
- 3 scenarios modeled with breakeven analysis
