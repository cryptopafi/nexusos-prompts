# DPM-078 — Run Retrospective

## Problema
Teams skip retrospectives or run them poorly, missing the biggest lever for continuous improvement.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Completed sprint or project phase
- Full team attendance
- 1-hour time block

### Steps
1. Choose a retrospective format: Start/Stop/Continue, Mad/Sad/Glad, or Sailboat
2. Set the stage: remind the team of the prime directive ('everyone did their best with what they knew')
3. Gather data: each member writes observations on sticky notes (physical or digital) independently
4. Group observations into themes using affinity mapping
5. Vote on the top 2-3 themes to discuss (dot voting)
6. For each top theme, brainstorm specific, actionable improvements
7. Select 1-2 improvements to implement next sprint; assign owners and track completion

### Verification
- Retro completed with full team
- Top themes identified
- 1-2 improvements committed with owners
