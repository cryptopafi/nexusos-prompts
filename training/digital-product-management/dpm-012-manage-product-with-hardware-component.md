# DPM-012 — Manage Product with Hardware Component

## Problema
PMs experienced only in software struggle with the unique constraints of hardware product development.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Hardware product specifications
- Manufacturing partner access
- Supply chain basics

### Steps
1. Map the hardware product lifecycle: concept, design, prototype, tooling, manufacturing, distribution
2. Identify key differences from software: longer lead times, higher iteration cost, inventory management
3. Create a BOM (Bill of Materials) and understand cost structure per unit
4. Plan the prototype phase: define number of iterations and testing criteria
5. Establish quality metrics: defect rate targets, reliability testing standards
6. Coordinate software and hardware release cycles (hardware freezes earlier)
7. Build a launch plan that accounts for manufacturing lead time and inventory buffers

### Verification
- Hardware lifecycle mapped
- BOM created
- Launch plan with hardware timelines documented
