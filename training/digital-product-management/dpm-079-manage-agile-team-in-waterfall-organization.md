# DPM-079 — Manage Agile Team in Waterfall Organization

## Problema
Agile teams struggle to operate within organizations that follow waterfall processes and mindset.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Agile team
- Waterfall organizational context
- Stakeholder relationships

### Steps
1. Identify the friction points: budget cycles, approval gates, reporting requirements, resource allocation
2. Create a 'translation layer': map agile artifacts to waterfall expectations (e.g., roadmap = project plan)
3. Deliver agile outputs in waterfall packaging: sprint demos = status reports, backlog = requirements doc
4. Negotiate sprint autonomy: within the sprint, the team operates agile; at boundaries, deliver waterfall artifacts
5. Educate stakeholders gradually: show results first, explain methodology second
6. Protect the team from waterfall disruptions: scope changes mid-sprint, ad-hoc status requests
7. Track and celebrate wins: show how agile delivery improves speed, quality, or customer satisfaction

### Verification
- Friction points identified
- Translation layer created
- Stakeholders receiving agile outputs in familiar format
