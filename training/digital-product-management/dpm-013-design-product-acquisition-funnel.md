# DPM-013 — Design Product Acquisition Funnel

## Problema
Products lack a structured acquisition strategy, relying on hope instead of a designed conversion path.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Product live or in beta
- Analytics tool configured
- Marketing channel access

### Steps
1. Define funnel stages: Awareness → Interest → Consideration → Trial → Conversion → Retention
2. For each stage, identify the primary channel and tactic (e.g., Awareness = content marketing, Trial = freemium)
3. Set conversion rate targets between each stage based on benchmarks or goals
4. Instrument analytics to measure drop-off between each funnel stage
5. Identify the biggest drop-off point in the current funnel
6. Design 3 experiments to improve conversion at the biggest drop-off
7. Create a funnel dashboard and review weekly with growth/marketing team

### Verification
- Funnel stages defined with channels
- Analytics instrumented
- 3 experiments designed for biggest drop-off
