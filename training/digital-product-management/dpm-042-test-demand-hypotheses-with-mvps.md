# DPM-042 — Test Demand Hypotheses with MVPs

## Problema
Demand hypotheses remain theoretical because teams don't build the right MVP to test them.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Demand hypothesis (DPM-036)
- MVP design skills
- Analytics tool

### Steps
1. Review your demand hypothesis and identify the cheapest way to test it
2. Choose MVP type: landing page, explainer video, Wizard of Oz, concierge, or single-feature MVP
3. Build the MVP with a clear call-to-action that measures demand (signup, pre-order, deposit)
4. Drive traffic to the MVP using ads, social media, or email to your target segment
5. Set a time limit: run the test for 1-2 weeks with a minimum sample size
6. Measure: conversion rate, cost per conversion, qualitative feedback
7. Compare results to your demand threshold and decide: build, iterate, or kill

### Verification
- MVP built and launched
- Traffic driven to MVP
- Demand threshold compared with decision made
