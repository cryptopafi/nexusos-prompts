# DPM-039 — Set Up and Conduct User Interviews for Persona Validation

## Problema
Personas are based on desk research and assumptions, never validated with real users.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Draft personas (DPM-023)
- Access to target users
- Interview guide template

### Steps
1. Define what you need to validate: persona goals, pain points, behaviors, and preferences
2. Write 8-10 interview questions that test persona assumptions without leading
3. Recruit 5-8 participants per persona segment
4. Conduct 30-minute interviews with recording (get consent)
5. After each interview, score how well the participant matched the persona (1-5 on each attribute)
6. After all interviews, calculate average match scores per persona attribute
7. Update personas: strengthen confirmed attributes, revise or remove invalidated ones

### Verification
- Interviews completed with 5+ participants per persona
- Match scores calculated
- Personas updated
