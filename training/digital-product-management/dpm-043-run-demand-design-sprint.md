# DPM-043 — Run Demand Design Sprint

## Problema
Teams take weeks to test demand when a focused sprint could validate it in days.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Demand hypothesis
- Cross-functional team (PM, marketing, design)
- 3 days blocked

### Steps
1. Day 1 (Frame): define the demand question, target segment, and success metrics
2. Day 1 (Generate): brainstorm 5+ demand test concepts (landing pages, ads, email campaigns)
3. Day 2 (Build): create the demand test asset (landing page + ad copy + tracking)
4. Day 2 (Launch): go live with the test, targeting 500+ impressions
5. Day 3 (Analyze): collect data on conversion, cost, and feedback
6. Day 3 (Decide): compare results to threshold, decide to proceed or iterate
7. Document the sprint: test design, results, decision, and next steps

### Verification
- 3-day demand sprint completed
- Test launched with 500+ impressions
- Decision documented
