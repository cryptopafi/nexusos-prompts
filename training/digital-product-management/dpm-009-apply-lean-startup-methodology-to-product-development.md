# DPM-009 — Apply Lean Startup Methodology to Product Development

## Problema
Product teams spend months building features without validating assumptions, wasting resources.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Product concept or feature idea
- Access to target users
- Basic analytics

### Steps
1. Formulate your core hypothesis: 'We believe [customer] will [behavior] because [reason]'
2. Identify the riskiest assumption that must be true for the product to succeed
3. Design a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) that tests only the riskiest assumption
4. Define the Build-Measure-Learn loop: what you build, what you measure, what you learn
5. Build the MVP in the shortest time possible (days, not months)
6. Measure: collect quantitative data (metrics) and qualitative data (interviews)
7. Learn: decide to persevere (scale), pivot (change direction), or kill (stop investment)

### Verification
- Core hypothesis documented
- MVP built and launched
- Build-Measure-Learn cycle completed with decision
