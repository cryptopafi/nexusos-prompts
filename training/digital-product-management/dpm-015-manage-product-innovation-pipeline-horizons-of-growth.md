# DPM-015 — Manage Product Innovation Pipeline (Horizons of Growth)

## Problema
Organizations focus only on incremental improvements, missing breakthrough and transformational opportunities.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Product portfolio overview
- Strategic goals
- Market trend data

### Steps
1. Define the three horizons: H1 (core business optimization), H2 (adjacent opportunities), H3 (transformational bets)
2. Audit current product portfolio and classify each initiative by horizon
3. Set resource allocation targets: e.g., 70% H1, 20% H2, 10% H3
4. For H1: focus on metrics like revenue growth and margin improvement
5. For H2: focus on metrics like new market penetration and pilot success
6. For H3: focus on metrics like experiment velocity and learning speed
7. Create a portfolio review cadence: monthly for H1, quarterly for H2, semi-annual for H3

### Verification
- Portfolio classified by horizon
- Resource allocation set
- Review cadence established
