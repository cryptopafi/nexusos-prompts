# DPM-023 — Create Actionable Personas

## Problema
Personas are created as demographic profiles that sit in a drawer instead of driving daily product decisions.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Customer research data (interviews, surveys, analytics)
- Team collaboration access

### Steps
1. Analyze customer data for behavioral patterns, not just demographics
2. Cluster users by behavior: usage patterns, goals, pain points, adoption speed
3. Create 2-4 behavioral personas with: name, key behavior, primary goal, main frustration, quote
4. For each persona, define: what they need from the product and what would make them leave
5. Add a 'day in the life' scenario showing when and why they use the product
6. Make personas visible: poster on wall, header in every PRD, reference in standups
7. Validate personas with quantitative data: do segments in analytics match persona behaviors?

### Verification
- 2-4 behavioral personas created
- Personas validated with data
- Personas integrated into team rituals
