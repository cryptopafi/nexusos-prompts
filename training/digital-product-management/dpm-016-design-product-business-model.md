# DPM-016 — Design Product Business Model

## Problema
Products are built without a viable business model, leading to traction without profitability.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Product concept or live product
- Business Model Canvas
- Market data

### Steps
1. Complete a Business Model Canvas: value proposition, customer segments, channels, revenue, costs, resources, activities, partners
2. Define the revenue model: subscription, transaction, freemium, marketplace, advertising, or hybrid
3. Calculate unit economics: CAC (customer acquisition cost), LTV (lifetime value), LTV:CAC ratio
4. Identify the key metric that drives the business model (e.g., retention for subscription, GMV for marketplace)
5. Stress-test the model: what happens if CAC doubles or retention drops 20%?
6. Compare your model to 3 competitors or analogous businesses
7. Present the business model to leadership with financial projections for 12 months

### Verification
- Business Model Canvas completed
- Unit economics calculated
- 12-month financial projection delivered
