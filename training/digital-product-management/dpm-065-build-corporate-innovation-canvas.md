# DPM-065 — Build Corporate Innovation Canvas

## Problema
Innovation efforts fail because they don't account for corporate constraints and existing assets.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Corporate strategy document
- Innovation team
- Market opportunity data

### Steps
1. Map corporate assets that can be leveraged: brand, distribution, data, customer relationships, IP
2. Map corporate constraints: regulation, legacy systems, organizational culture, budget cycles
3. Identify 5+ innovation opportunities at the intersection of assets and market needs
4. For each opportunity, assess: market size, fit with corporate assets, required investment, time to value
5. Score opportunities using a weighted matrix (market attractiveness x corporate fit)
6. Select top 2-3 opportunities for further exploration
7. Create an innovation brief for each: problem, opportunity, hypothesis, required resources, timeline

### Verification
- Corporate assets and constraints mapped
- Opportunities scored
- Innovation briefs created for top 3
