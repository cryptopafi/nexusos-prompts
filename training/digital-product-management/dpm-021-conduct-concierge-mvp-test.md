# DPM-021 — Conduct Concierge MVP Test

## Problema
Teams spend months building MVPs when they could manually deliver the service to test the concept faster.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Product concept
- Target customer access
- Ability to deliver service manually

### Steps
1. Define what your product would do if fully built
2. Identify the core value you can deliver manually (the 'concierge' service)
3. Recruit 5-10 customers willing to try the manual version
4. Deliver the service manually, mimicking what the product would do
5. Track: time per customer, customer satisfaction, willingness to pay, retention
6. After 2 weeks, interview participants: 'Would you pay for this? How much? What's missing?'
7. Decide: build the product (validated), pivot (partial validation), or kill (not validated)

### Verification
- Concierge service delivered to 5+ customers
- Metrics tracked
- Go/no-go decision made
