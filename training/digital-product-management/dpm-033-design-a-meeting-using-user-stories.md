# DPM-033 — Design a Meeting Using User Stories

## Problema
Product meetings are unfocused and waste time because they lack structure tied to deliverables.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Meeting purpose defined
- Attendee list
- Calendar tool

### Steps
1. Define the meeting's 'user story': 'As a [attendee role], I want to [meeting outcome], so that [decision/action]'
2. Set acceptance criteria: what must be true by the end of the meeting?
3. Design the agenda as steps to achieve the acceptance criteria (time-boxed)
4. Identify required inputs: what must attendees prepare or review before the meeting?
5. Send pre-read materials at least 24 hours before the meeting
6. During the meeting, track progress against acceptance criteria
7. Close with: decisions made, action items with owners and dates, next meeting purpose

### Verification
- Meeting user story defined
- Agenda with time boxes created
- Post-meeting actions documented
