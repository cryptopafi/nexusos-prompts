# EUCL-015 — CE Marking and Product Compliance

## Problema
Manufacturers and importers placing products on the EU market without proper CE marking and conformity assessment face market surveillance actions, fines, and product recalls.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- EUCL-009 (free movement of goods), understanding of product categories

### Steps
1. Determine if the product falls under a **CE marking directive/regulation**: toys (2009/48/EC), machinery (2006/42/EC), medical devices (Regulation 2017/745), electrical equipment (2014/35/EU — LVD), EMC (2014/30/EU), radio equipment (2014/53/EU), construction products (Regulation 305/2011), PPE (Regulation 2016/425), etc.
2. Apply the **New Legislative Framework (NLF)** — Regulation 765/2008 and Decision 768/2008/EC: standardized conformity assessment modules, market surveillance, accreditation of conformity assessment bodies (notified bodies)
3. **Identify economic operator obligations**: manufacturer (design, production, CE marking, DoC, technical documentation), importer (verify CE marking, ensure DoC and documentation available, label with name/address), distributor (verify CE marking present, storage/transport conditions)
4. **Conduct conformity assessment**: select appropriate module (A = internal production control, B+C/D/E/F = EU-type examination + production control, G = unit verification, H = full quality assurance). Higher-risk products require notified body involvement
5. **Prepare technical documentation**: design drawings, calculations, test reports, list of harmonized standards applied, risk assessment, instructions for use
6. **Draft EU Declaration of Conformity (DoC)**: identify product, state applicable directives/regulations, list harmonized standards, include notified body number if applicable, sign and date
7. **Affix CE marking**: visible, legible, indelible, minimum 5mm height, proportions per official design. If notified body involved, include its 4-digit identification number
8. Monitor **market surveillance** obligations: respond to authority requests, take corrective action if non-compliance discovered, report to RAPEX/Safety Gate for dangerous products

### Verification
- Can determine which directives/regulations apply to a specific product
- Can select the correct conformity assessment module
- Can prepare a compliant DoC and affix CE marking correctly
