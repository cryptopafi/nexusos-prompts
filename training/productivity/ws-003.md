# WS-003 — Create a Daily Task Prioritization System

## Problema
Starting each day without a clear prioritization system leads to reactive work patterns and missed deadlines.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- WS-001 completed

### Steps
1. At the end of each workday, identify tomorrow's top 3 priorities (MIT: Most Important Tasks)
2. Apply the 1-3-5 rule: plan 1 big task, 3 medium tasks, 5 small tasks maximum
3. Time-block the top priority into your first productive hours
4. Batch similar small tasks into dedicated time slots
5. Leave buffer time (20-30%) for unexpected requests and context switching
6. Review and adjust priorities at midday if circumstances change
7. At day's end, review completion and carry forward unfinished items with re-prioritization

### Verification
- Daily planning habit maintained for 10 consecutive workdays
- MITs completed before other tasks on at least 7 of 10 days
