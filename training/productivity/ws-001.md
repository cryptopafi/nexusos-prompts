# WS-001 — Prioritize Tasks Using the Eisenhower Matrix

## Problema
Without a systematic prioritization method, people default to urgency-driven work and neglect important strategic tasks.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- List of current tasks and responsibilities

### Steps
1. List all current tasks, commitments, and incoming requests
2. Classify each task on two dimensions: urgency (deadline-driven) and importance (value-driven)
3. Place tasks in four quadrants: Q1 (urgent+important: do now), Q2 (important+not urgent: schedule), Q3 (urgent+not important: delegate), Q4 (neither: eliminate)
4. Allocate the majority of proactive time to Q2 activities
5. Establish systems to reduce Q1 emergencies through better Q2 planning
6. Review and reclassify weekly

### Verification
- All current tasks classified into quadrants
- Q2 time blocks scheduled for the upcoming week
