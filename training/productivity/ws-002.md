# WS-002 — Track and Audit Your Time Usage

## Problema
People consistently misjudge how they spend their time, making improvement impossible without actual measurement.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Time tracking tool (spreadsheet, app, or paper)

### Steps
1. Select a tracking method: manual log (30-minute blocks), automatic tracker (RescueTime, Toggl), or hybrid
2. Track all activities for 5 consecutive workdays (minimum), including breaks and interruptions
3. Categorize tracked time into: deep work, shallow work, meetings, communication, breaks, personal, interruptions
4. Calculate percentage of time in each category
5. Compare actual allocation against ideal allocation for your role
6. Identify top 3 time drains that can be eliminated, reduced, or delegated
7. Set specific targets for time reallocation and track again in 2 weeks

### Verification
- Full 5-day time audit completed with no gaps
- At least 3 actionable time reallocation targets identified
