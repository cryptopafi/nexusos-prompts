# RE-012 — Select Energy Storage Technology

## Problema
Choosing the wrong storage technology for an application leads to poor performance, excessive cost, or safety issues.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- RE-002 completed
- Understanding of grid operations basics

### Steps
1. Define storage application requirements (duration, power, cycles, response time)
2. Map application to storage categories: short-duration (<4h), long-duration (>4h), seasonal
3. Evaluate candidate technologies: Li-ion, flow batteries, pumped hydro, compressed air, hydrogen
4. Compare round-trip efficiency for each candidate
5. Assess cycle life and degradation curves
6. Evaluate safety considerations and siting constraints
7. Rank candidates using weighted multi-criteria decision matrix

### Verification
- Decision matrix includes at least 6 criteria weighted by application priority
- Selected technology validated against at least 3 comparable deployed projects
