# RE-010 — Evaluate Hydroelectric Power Potential

## Problema
Hydroelectric assessments require understanding of hydrology, civil engineering, and environmental constraints unique to water-based generation.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- RE-001 and RE-002 completed

### Steps
1. Obtain streamflow data for candidate site (USGS gauge data or hydrological modeling)
2. Calculate gross head (elevation difference between intake and turbine)
3. Estimate net head accounting for friction and minor losses
4. Calculate theoretical power: P = rho x g x Q x H x efficiency
5. Select turbine type based on head and flow (Pelton, Francis, Kaplan)
6. Assess environmental flow requirements and regulatory constraints
7. Evaluate seasonal variation and firm capacity vs peaking capability

### Verification
- Power calculation validated against comparable existing hydro facilities
- Environmental flow requirements identified and incorporated
