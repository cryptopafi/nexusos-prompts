# RE-009 — Calculate Solar PV Economics (LCOE, ROI)

## Problema
Without rigorous economic analysis, solar projects may appear viable on paper but fail to deliver adequate returns.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- RE-007 completed
- RE-028 (time value of money) recommended

### Steps
1. Compile capital expenditure (CAPEX) breakdown: modules, inverters, BOS, EPC, soft costs
2. Estimate annual operating expenditure (OPEX): O&M, insurance, land lease, admin
3. Project annual energy generation with degradation rate (typically 0.5%/year)
4. Calculate LCOE: (lifetime costs NPV) / (lifetime energy NPV)
5. Model project cash flows including tax credits (ITC/PTC), depreciation (MACRS)
6. Calculate IRR, NPV, and payback period
7. Perform sensitivity analysis on key variables (module cost, electricity price, discount rate)

### Verification
- LCOE calculation matches within 5% of PVsyst or SAM economic output
- Sensitivity analysis covers at least 5 key variables
