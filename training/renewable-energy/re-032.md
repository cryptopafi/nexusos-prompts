# RE-032 — Structure Residential RE Project Financing

## Problema
Residential solar financing options differ significantly in ownership, tax benefit allocation, and customer economics.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- RE-009 completed
- RE-028 completed

### Steps
1. Evaluate customer-owned options: cash purchase, home equity loan, solar loan
2. Evaluate third-party-owned options: lease, PPA
3. Calculate customer economics for each option: monthly payment, savings, payback
4. Assess tax credit eligibility and utilization for each structure
5. Compare total cost of ownership over system lifetime
6. Evaluate financing impact on home value and transferability
7. Recommend optimal financing structure based on customer profile

### Verification
- Economics calculated for at least 3 financing options
- Tax credit assumptions match current IRS guidelines
