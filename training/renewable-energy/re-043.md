# RE-043 — Evaluate Renewable Energy Finance and Investment Structures

## Problema
Institutional investors require understanding of specialized RE finance structures that differ from traditional infrastructure investment.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- RE-029 completed
- RE-034 recommended

### Steps
1. Map the RE project finance ecosystem: developers, investors, lenders, tax equity, offtakers
2. Evaluate project finance vs corporate finance for RE investment
3. Analyze yieldco and infrastructure fund structures for RE assets
4. Assess green bond and sustainability-linked financing options
5. Evaluate risk allocation across project finance participants
6. Analyze credit enhancement mechanisms: reserves, guarantees, insurance wraps
7. Assess merchant risk vs contracted revenue and impact on financing terms
8. Review current market terms for RE project debt and tax equity

### Verification
- Risk allocation matrix covers all major project risks
- Financing terms benchmarked against current market data
