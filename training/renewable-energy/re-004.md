# RE-004 — Assess Offshore Wind Feasibility

## Problema
Offshore wind projects face unique technical and regulatory challenges that onshore assessments do not cover.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- RE-003 completed
- Understanding of marine construction basics

### Steps
1. Evaluate offshore wind resource using buoy data and satellite measurements
2. Assess water depth and seabed conditions for foundation type selection
3. Determine distance to shore and grid connection point
4. Evaluate environmental constraints (marine habitats, shipping lanes, military zones)
5. Estimate foundation costs based on depth (monopile <30m, jacket 30-60m, floating >60m)
6. Calculate transmission losses and subsea cable requirements
7. Assess regulatory framework and permitting timeline

### Verification
- Feasibility matrix covers all five dimensions: resource, technical, environmental, regulatory, financial
- Cost estimates benchmarked against comparable offshore projects
