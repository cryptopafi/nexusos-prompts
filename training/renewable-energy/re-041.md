# RE-041 — Assess Green Hydrogen Production Viability

## Problema
Green hydrogen is a critical enabler for hard-to-decarbonize sectors but faces significant cost and efficiency challenges.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- RE-002 completed
- RE-009 completed

### Steps
1. Evaluate electrolyzer technologies: alkaline, PEM, solid oxide (SOEC)
2. Calculate hydrogen production cost from renewable electricity ($/kg H2)
3. Assess electrolyzer efficiency and degradation over lifetime
4. Evaluate co-location with renewable generation vs grid-connected operation
5. Analyze hydrogen storage options: compressed gas, liquid, metal hydrides, salt caverns
6. Identify target end-use applications: industry, transport, power, heating
7. Compare green hydrogen cost against grey/blue hydrogen and fossil alternatives
8. Assess infrastructure requirements: pipelines, refueling stations, blending

### Verification
- Hydrogen production cost calculated with transparent assumptions
- End-use application economics compared against incumbent fuels
