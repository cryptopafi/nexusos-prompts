# RE-018 — Design Demand Response Program

## Problema
Demand response programs fail when they lack clear incentive structures, reliable communication, and measurable baselines.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- RE-014 completed

### Steps
1. Define program objectives: peak shaving, frequency response, emergency load reduction
2. Identify target customer segments and their load flexibility
3. Establish customer baseline load (CBL) calculation methodology
4. Design incentive structure: capacity payments, energy payments, or both
5. Specify communication and control infrastructure (AMI, SCADA, aggregator platforms)
6. Define event dispatch protocols and notification timelines
7. Establish measurement and verification (M&V) procedures
8. Design program evaluation metrics: participation rate, load reduction achieved, cost-effectiveness

### Verification
- CBL methodology statistically validated against actual consumption
- Pilot program achieves >70% of target load reduction
