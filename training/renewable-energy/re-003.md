# RE-003 — Evaluate Wind Site Characteristics for Energy Production

## Problema
Poor wind site assessment leads to underperforming turbines and failed project economics.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- RE-001 and RE-002 completed
- Access to wind resource data (e.g., NREL Wind Toolkit)

### Steps
1. Obtain wind speed data for the candidate site (minimum 1 year, ideally 3+ years)
2. Analyze wind speed distribution using Weibull or Rayleigh probability curves
3. Calculate wind power density (W/m2) at hub height
4. Assess wind shear and turbulence intensity
5. Evaluate terrain roughness and obstacle effects
6. Estimate annual energy production (AEP) using power curve and wind distribution
7. Compare site metrics against industry benchmarks for viability

### Verification
- Wind power density exceeds 200 W/m2 for Class 3+ rating
- AEP estimate within 10% of comparable reference sites
