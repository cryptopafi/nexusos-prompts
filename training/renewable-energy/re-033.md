# RE-033 — Structure Commercial/Industrial RE Project Financing

## Problema
C&I projects face unique challenges: split incentives, tenant/landlord structures, and corporate procurement complexity.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- RE-029 completed

### Steps
1. Evaluate on-site generation options: direct ownership, PPA, lease
2. Assess off-site procurement options: virtual PPA (VPPA), green tariff, REC purchase
3. Structure tax equity partnership if customer cannot utilize tax credits directly
4. Negotiate PPA terms: price, escalator, term, performance guarantees, buyout options
5. Address split incentive issues in leased building scenarios
6. Evaluate corporate renewable energy goals (RE100, SBTi) alignment
7. Model project economics from both developer and customer perspective

### Verification
- Financial model covers both developer and customer returns
- Contract structure addresses identified split incentive issues
