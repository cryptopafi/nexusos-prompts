# RE-006 — Evaluate Solar Radiation for PV Site Selection

## Problema
Incorrect solar resource assessment leads to oversized or undersized PV systems and inaccurate financial models.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- RE-001 and RE-002 completed
- Access to solar radiation databases

### Steps
1. Obtain Global Horizontal Irradiance (GHI) data for candidate site
2. Decompose GHI into Direct Normal Irradiance (DNI) and Diffuse Horizontal Irradiance (DHI)
3. Calculate Plane of Array (POA) irradiance for proposed tilt and azimuth
4. Assess inter-annual variability using P50/P75/P90 exceedance probabilities
5. Evaluate shading from terrain, structures, and vegetation
6. Estimate temperature effects on PV performance using site climate data
7. Compare site solar resource against regional benchmarks

### Verification
- GHI data sourced from at least two independent databases
- POA irradiance calculation validated against PVsyst or similar tool
