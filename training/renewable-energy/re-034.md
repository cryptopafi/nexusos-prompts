# RE-034 — Structure Utility-Scale RE Project Financing

## Problema
Utility-scale projects require sophisticated financing combining tax equity, project debt, and sponsor equity in specific structures.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- RE-030 completed

### Steps
1. Evaluate financing structures: partnership flip, inverted lease, sale-leaseback
2. Size project debt based on DSCR requirements and debt tenor
3. Structure tax equity investment with target returns (typically 7-9% after-tax)
4. Negotiate flip point and post-flip allocations in partnership structure
5. Arrange construction financing (construction loan or equity bridge)
6. Evaluate hedging strategies for merchant tail exposure
7. Coordinate closing conditions across lender, tax equity, and sponsor requirements
8. Model waterfall distributions with all partnership allocations

### Verification
- Partnership flip model validated by tax counsel
- DSCR covenants met in P99 downside scenario
