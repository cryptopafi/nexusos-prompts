# RE-023 — Evaluate Site Regulations and Permitting Requirements

## Problema
Permitting delays are a leading cause of renewable project cost overruns and missed commercial operation dates.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- RE-021 or RE-022 completed

### Steps
1. Identify all jurisdictions with authority over the project site (federal, state, county, municipal)
2. Map required permits by jurisdiction (land use, building, environmental, electrical, aviation)
3. Determine NEPA applicability and required level of review (CE, EA, EIS)
4. Assess state environmental review requirements (CEQA, SEPA, etc.)
5. Identify endangered species and cultural resource survey requirements
6. Estimate permitting timeline with critical path dependencies
7. Budget for permitting costs including consultant fees and agency fees

### Verification
- Complete permit matrix with all required approvals, agencies, timelines, and costs
- Critical path timeline validated against comparable project permitting durations
