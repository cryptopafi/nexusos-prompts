# RE-039 — Analyze Energy Transition Scenarios

## Problema
Strategic planning in the energy sector requires understanding multiple transition pathways and their implications.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- General understanding of energy systems

### Steps
1. Review major transition scenario frameworks (IEA NZE, IRENA 1.5C, BloombergNEF NEO, Shell Scenarios)
2. Compare scenario assumptions: demand growth, technology costs, policy stringency
3. Analyze projected energy mix evolution by decade (2030, 2040, 2050)
4. Evaluate implications for specific technologies (coal phase-out, gas bridge, nuclear role)
5. Assess investment requirements under each scenario
6. Identify key uncertainties and pivot points between scenarios
7. Apply scenario analysis to specific business or policy decision

### Verification
- At least 3 scenarios compared across consistent metrics
- Key pivot points identified with trigger indicators
