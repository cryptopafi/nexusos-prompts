# RE-020 — Evaluate ISO/Market Structure for Renewable Projects

## Problema
Each ISO/RTO has unique market products, rules, and settlement processes that directly impact renewable project revenue.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- RE-019 completed

### Steps
1. Identify the ISO/RTO governing the project location (CAISO, PJM, ERCOT, MISO, SPP, NYISO, ISO-NE)
2. Map market products: energy, capacity, ancillary services, RECs
3. Analyze Locational Marginal Pricing (LMP) at the project node
4. Evaluate capacity market rules and renewable resource qualification
5. Assess ancillary service participation requirements for renewables
6. Review interconnection study process and timeline for target ISO
7. Analyze historical curtailment data and congestion patterns at target node

### Verification
- LMP analysis covers at least 3 years of historical data
- Interconnection timeline estimate validated against ISO queue statistics
