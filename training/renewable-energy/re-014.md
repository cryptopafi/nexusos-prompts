# RE-014 — Analyze Grid Operations for Renewable Integration

## Problema
Understanding grid operations is essential before attempting to integrate variable renewable energy sources.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- RE-002 completed

### Steps
1. Map the grid hierarchy: generation, transmission, distribution, load
2. Understand dispatch order: baseload, intermediate, peaking units
3. Identify grid balancing mechanisms: frequency regulation, spinning reserves, load following
4. Analyze how variable renewables (solar, wind) affect net load curves
5. Understand the duck curve phenomenon and its operational implications
6. Evaluate curtailment causes and mitigation strategies
7. Assess grid flexibility resources: storage, demand response, interconnections

### Verification
- Can explain merit order dispatch with and without renewables
- Duck curve analysis includes quantified ramp rate requirements
