# RE-040 — Evaluate Electric Vehicle Integration with RE Grid

## Problema
EV adoption creates both challenges (load growth) and opportunities (mobile storage) for renewable-powered grids.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- RE-014 completed
- RE-012 completed

### Steps
1. Forecast EV adoption trajectory and charging load impact on grid
2. Analyze charging patterns: home (L2), workplace (L2), public fast (DCFC)
3. Evaluate managed charging (V1G) potential for load shifting to renewable generation peaks
4. Assess vehicle-to-grid (V2G) technical feasibility and economics
5. Calculate grid infrastructure upgrades needed for EV load growth
6. Design rate structures to incentivize RE-aligned charging behavior
7. Model combined RE + EV scenario for grid carbon intensity reduction

### Verification
- EV load forecast based on defensible adoption assumptions
- Managed charging potential quantified in MW of flexible load
