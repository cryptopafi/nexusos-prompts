# RE-028 — Calculate Time Value of Money for RE Projects

## Problema
Renewable energy projects span 20-35 years, making time value of money calculations critical for accurate financial assessment.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Basic finance and algebra knowledge

### Steps
1. Calculate present value (PV) of future cash flows using appropriate discount rate
2. Calculate future value (FV) of investments and costs
3. Determine Net Present Value (NPV) of a project cash flow stream
4. Calculate Internal Rate of Return (IRR) iteratively or using financial tools
5. Select appropriate discount rate: WACC, cost of equity, or risk-adjusted rate
6. Apply real vs nominal discount rates consistently with cash flow assumptions

### Verification
- NPV and IRR calculations match Excel/financial calculator results
- Discount rate selection justified with market data
