# RE-037 — Plan Ongoing RE Project Operations and Maintenance

## Problema
O&M planning directly affects long-term energy production, equipment life, and project financial returns.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- RE-007 or RE-005 completed

### Steps
1. Develop preventive maintenance schedule based on OEM recommendations
2. Establish performance monitoring system (SCADA, remote monitoring)
3. Define Key Performance Indicators: availability, performance ratio, specific yield
4. Plan spare parts inventory management
5. Develop corrective maintenance response protocols and SLAs
6. Establish vegetation management and site maintenance schedule
7. Plan periodic inverter and transformer maintenance outages
8. Budget annual O&M costs with major maintenance reserves

### Verification
- O&M plan covers full asset lifecycle
- KPI targets set against industry benchmarks
