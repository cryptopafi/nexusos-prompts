# RE-030 — Build Renewable Energy Project Financial Projections

## Problema
Financial projections must integrate technical performance, market assumptions, and financing terms into a coherent bankable model.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- RE-029 completed

### Steps
1. Build revenue model: energy production x price (PPA, merchant, or hybrid)
2. Model operating expenses with escalation rates
3. Structure capital stack: equity, tax equity, debt (construction and term)
4. Model depreciation schedule (MACRS 5-year for solar/wind)
5. Calculate federal and state tax credits (ITC or PTC) with adders
6. Build annual cash flow waterfall: revenue > OPEX > debt service > tax equity > sponsor equity
7. Model reserve accounts (debt service reserve, O&M reserve, major maintenance reserve)
8. Generate financial statements: income statement, balance sheet, cash flow statement

### Verification
- Model balances (assets = liabilities + equity) in every year
- Cash flow waterfall fully allocates all cash with no unexplained residuals
