# RE-013 — Calculate Energy Storage Economics

## Problema
Storage economics are complex due to multiple revenue streams, degradation, and rapidly changing technology costs.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- RE-012 completed
- RE-028 recommended

### Steps
1. Compile storage system CAPEX: battery/storage medium, power conversion, BOS, installation
2. Calculate Levelized Cost of Storage (LCOS) using lifetime throughput
3. Identify and quantify revenue streams: energy arbitrage, capacity payments, ancillary services
4. Model degradation impact on capacity and revenue over project life
5. Calculate augmentation or replacement costs to maintain contracted capacity
6. Perform NPV and IRR analysis with stacked revenue streams
7. Run sensitivity analysis on key variables (cycling frequency, electricity price spread, degradation rate)

### Verification
- LCOS calculation methodology consistent with Lazard or NREL framework
- Revenue stacking model validated against market price data
