# RE-005 — Design Wind Turbine System

## Problema
System-level wind turbine design requires integrating aerodynamic, structural, and electrical subsystems into a coherent architecture.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- RE-003 and RE-004 completed
- Engineering fundamentals in mechanics and electrical systems

### Steps
1. Define design wind class (IEC 61400 classification) based on site conditions
2. Select rotor diameter and hub height for target capacity and wind regime
3. Calculate tip speed ratio and optimal blade pitch for maximum Cp (Betz limit 59.3%)
4. Design drivetrain architecture (direct drive vs gearbox)
5. Specify generator type (DFIG, PMSG, or SCIG)
6. Design tower structure for load cases (extreme wind, fatigue, seismic)
7. Integrate control system for variable speed/pitch operation
8. Validate design against IEC standards and perform load simulation

### Verification
- Design meets IEC 61400 requirements for target wind class
- Simulated AEP within 5% of design target
