# RE-031 — Calculate Levelized Cost of Energy (LCOE)

## Problema
LCOE is the most widely used metric for comparing generation technologies, but its calculation requires careful assumption management.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- RE-028 completed
- RE-009 recommended

### Steps
1. Compile total installed cost (overnight cost) including EPC and soft costs
2. Estimate annual fixed O&M costs ($/kW-year) and variable O&M costs ($/MWh)
3. Project annual energy generation with degradation over project lifetime
4. Select discount rate (real WACC for real LCOE, nominal WACC for nominal LCOE)
5. Calculate LCOE = NPV(lifetime costs) / NPV(lifetime generation)
6. Adjust for tax credits and incentives if calculating after-incentive LCOE
7. Compare result against NREL ATB, Lazard, and BloombergNEF benchmarks
8. Document all assumptions for transparency and reproducibility

### Verification
- LCOE result within 15% of NREL ATB for same technology and year
- All assumptions documented with sources
