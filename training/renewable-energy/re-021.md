# RE-021 — Conduct Renewable Energy Project Feasibility Study

## Problema
Feasibility studies that skip critical dimensions lead to project failures during development, financing, or construction.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- RE-003 or RE-006 completed
- RE-009 completed
- RE-019 completed

### Steps
1. Define project scope: technology, nameplate capacity, target COD
2. Assess resource adequacy using site-specific data (wind, solar, or hydro)
3. Evaluate land/site suitability: access, topography, environmental constraints
4. Analyze grid interconnection feasibility and cost estimate
5. Assess regulatory and permitting pathway with timeline
6. Conduct preliminary economic analysis (LCOE, NPV, IRR)
7. Identify and rank project risks with mitigation strategies
8. Produce go/no-go recommendation with supporting evidence

### Verification
- Feasibility study covers all 7 dimensions above
- Risk register includes at least 10 identified risks with mitigations
