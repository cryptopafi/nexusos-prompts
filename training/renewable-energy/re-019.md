# RE-019 — Analyze Power Industry Structure for Renewable Deployment

## Problema
Renewable project success depends on understanding whether the market is regulated, deregulated, or hybrid, and how power is procured.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Basic understanding of electricity markets

### Steps
1. Classify target market structure: vertically integrated utility, wholesale market, or hybrid
2. Identify key regulatory bodies (FERC, state PUC, ISO/RTO) and their jurisdictions
3. Map power procurement mechanisms: rate base, PPA, wholesale market sales, net metering
4. Analyze retail rate structures and their impact on renewable project economics
5. Evaluate Renewable Portfolio Standards (RPS) and Clean Energy Standards in target jurisdiction
6. Assess interconnection policies and queue processes
7. Identify tax incentives and subsidies available for renewable projects

### Verification
- Market structure analysis covers all procurement pathways available to project
- Regulatory landscape summary validated against current state/federal policies
