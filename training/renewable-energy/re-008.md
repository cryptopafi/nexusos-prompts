# RE-008 — Plan Large-Scale Solar PV Installation

## Problema
Utility-scale PV projects require coordinating land, permitting, grid interconnection, and construction logistics beyond basic system design.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- RE-007 completed
- Project management fundamentals

### Steps
1. Conduct land assessment (topography, soil, access roads, flood risk)
2. Design plant layout with tracking system selection (fixed tilt vs single-axis vs dual-axis)
3. Specify medium voltage collection system and substation design
4. Plan grid interconnection: file application, conduct interconnection study
5. Obtain environmental permits (NEPA, state/local, endangered species review)
6. Develop construction schedule with critical path milestones
7. Plan commissioning and performance testing protocol
8. Establish long-term O&M plan with degradation assumptions

### Verification
- Interconnection study completed and approved
- Construction timeline realistic vs industry benchmarks (6-12 months for 100MW+)
