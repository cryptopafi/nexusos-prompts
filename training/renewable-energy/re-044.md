# RE-044 — Analyze Zero-Marginal-Cost Electricity Market Dynamics

## Problema
As renewables with zero marginal cost dominate generation, traditional electricity market designs face fundamental challenges.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- RE-019 completed
- RE-020 completed

### Steps
1. Explain the merit order effect and how zero-marginal-cost renewables suppress wholesale prices
2. Analyze the cannibalization effect as RE penetration increases
3. Evaluate energy-only market design limitations at high RE penetration
4. Assess capacity market and capacity payment mechanisms as alternatives
5. Evaluate scarcity pricing and ORDC (Operating Reserve Demand Curve) approaches
6. Analyze long-duration storage and flexible demand roles in price formation
7. Compare international market design approaches (Germany, Australia, Texas, UK)
8. Propose market design adaptations for high-RE future

### Verification
- Price suppression effect quantified with historical market data
- At least 3 market design approaches compared with pros/cons
