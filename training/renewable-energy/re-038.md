# RE-038 — Plan RE Project Decommissioning

## Problema
Decommissioning obligations must be planned and funded from project inception to avoid stranded liabilities.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- RE-021 completed

### Steps
1. Identify decommissioning obligations in land lease and permits
2. Estimate decommissioning costs: equipment removal, site restoration, waste disposal
3. Assess salvage value of equipment (steel, copper, silicon)
4. Calculate net decommissioning cost (gross cost minus salvage value)
5. Establish decommissioning financial assurance (surety bond, escrow, letter of credit)
6. Plan environmental remediation if required (soil, groundwater)
7. Develop decommissioning timeline and contractor procurement plan

### Verification
- Decommissioning cost estimate reviewed by independent engineer
- Financial assurance instrument in place before construction
