# RE-022 — Perform Renewable Energy Project Site Selection

## Problema
Site selection errors are the most expensive mistakes in renewable energy - they cannot be corrected after construction.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- RE-003 or RE-006 completed
- RE-021 recommended

### Steps
1. Define site selection criteria with weights: resource quality, land cost, grid proximity, permitting, community acceptance
2. Screen candidate sites using GIS analysis and exclusion layers
3. Conduct desktop resource assessment for shortlisted sites
4. Evaluate grid interconnection options and costs for each site
5. Assess environmental and social constraints (wetlands, endangered species, cultural resources)
6. Conduct preliminary geotechnical assessment
7. Score and rank candidate sites using weighted criteria matrix
8. Conduct site visit for top 2-3 candidates

### Verification
- At least 5 candidate sites evaluated before final selection
- Weighted scoring matrix documented and defensible
