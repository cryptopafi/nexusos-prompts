# RE-036 — Manage RE Project Construction and Commissioning

## Problema
Construction management failures lead to cost overruns, schedule delays, and performance shortfalls that persist for the project lifetime.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- RE-035 completed

### Steps
1. Monitor construction progress against baseline schedule (earned value management)
2. Conduct regular quality inspections: foundation, mechanical, electrical
3. Manage equipment delivery logistics and on-site inventory
4. Track construction budget with cost-to-complete forecasting
5. Coordinate utility interconnection construction and testing
6. Execute commissioning plan: mechanical completion, energization, performance test
7. Conduct capacity test per PPA and interconnection requirements
8. Achieve Commercial Operation Date (COD) and transition to O&M

### Verification
- Capacity test meets or exceeds guaranteed output
- Punch list items closed within 90 days of COD
