# RE-011 — Assess Biomass Energy Viability

## Problema
Biomass projects require careful feedstock analysis to avoid supply chain failures and emissions compliance issues.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- RE-001 completed
- Understanding of combustion chemistry basics

### Steps
1. Identify and characterize available biomass feedstocks (wood, agricultural residue, waste)
2. Analyze feedstock properties: moisture content, heating value, ash content
3. Estimate sustainable annual feedstock supply within economic transport radius
4. Select conversion technology (direct combustion, gasification, anaerobic digestion, pyrolysis)
5. Calculate energy output based on feedstock supply and conversion efficiency
6. Assess emissions profile and air quality permit requirements
7. Evaluate feedstock supply chain logistics and long-term contracts

### Verification
- Feedstock supply secured for minimum 10-year project life
- Emissions calculations meet local air quality standards
