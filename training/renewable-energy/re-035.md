# RE-035 — Prepare RE Project for Construction

## Problema
The transition from development to construction is where most project failures occur due to inadequate preparation.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- RE-021 completed
- Financing secured

### Steps
1. Finalize EPC contract with performance guarantees, liquidated damages, and warranty terms
2. Complete all permitting and obtain Notice to Proceed
3. Secure equipment procurement contracts with delivery schedules
4. Establish construction monitoring and quality assurance plan
5. Set up project accounting and cost tracking systems
6. Obtain construction insurance (builder's risk, general liability, environmental)
7. Conduct pre-construction meeting with all stakeholders
8. Establish change order management process

### Verification
- All permits obtained and NTP issued
- EPC contract executed with performance guarantees
