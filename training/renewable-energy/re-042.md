# RE-042 — Design Distributed Energy System

## Problema
Distributed energy systems require optimizing generation, storage, and load management at the local level.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- RE-007 completed
- RE-012 completed
- RE-018 completed

### Steps
1. Characterize site load profile (hourly, seasonal, peak vs base)
2. Evaluate distributed generation options: rooftop solar, small wind, CHP, fuel cells
3. Size energy storage for target self-consumption or resilience duration
4. Design microgrid architecture: AC-coupled vs DC-coupled, islanding capability
5. Implement energy management system logic: dispatch priority, mode transitions
6. Analyze grid interaction: net metering, feed-in tariff, demand charge reduction
7. Calculate system economics: payback, NPV, resilience value
8. Plan for future expandability and technology upgrades

### Verification
- System simulation shows target self-consumption rate achieved
- Economic analysis includes resilience value quantification
