# CL-016 — Determine Principal-Agent Liability

## Problema
Principals face liability for agent actions they may not have authorized, making liability analysis critical for risk management.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- CL-015 completed

### Steps
1. Classify the agent: employee vs independent contractor (control test)
2. Evaluate respondeat superior liability for employee torts (scope of employment)
3. Assess principal's liability for agent's contracts (actual vs apparent authority)
4. Determine liability for undisclosed or partially disclosed principal
5. Evaluate agent's personal liability to third parties
6. Assess frolic and detour doctrine for scope of employment limits
7. Analyze independent contractor exceptions (inherently dangerous activities, non-delegable duties)

### Verification
- Agent correctly classified as employee vs independent contractor
- Liability allocation determined for all three parties (principal, agent, third party)
