# CL-006 — Apply UCC Article 2 Rules to Sales Contracts

## Problema
Sales of goods follow UCC rules that differ significantly from common law contract principles, causing frequent errors.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- CL-001 completed

### Steps
1. Determine if the transaction involves goods (movable, tangible personal property)
2. Apply UCC rules for mixed transactions (predominant purpose test)
3. Identify merchant status of parties and additional merchant obligations
4. Apply UCC gap-filler provisions for missing terms (price, delivery, payment)
5. Evaluate the battle of the forms under UCC 2-207
6. Apply UCC modification rules (no consideration needed, good faith required)
7. Determine UCC statute of limitations (4 years from tender of delivery)

### Verification
- Goods vs services determination correctly made
- Merchant status of both parties assessed
