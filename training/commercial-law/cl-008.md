# CL-008 — Evaluate Warranties in Sale of Goods Transactions

## Problema
Warranty claims are a primary remedy for buyers, but understanding which warranties apply and how they can be disclaimed is essential.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- CL-006 completed

### Steps
1. Identify express warranties from seller statements, descriptions, samples, or models (UCC 2-313)
2. Determine if implied warranty of merchantability applies (UCC 2-314)
3. Determine if implied warranty of fitness for particular purpose applies (UCC 2-315)
4. Evaluate warranty disclaimers for legal sufficiency (conspicuous, specific language)
5. Assess limitation of remedies clauses and unconscionability limits
6. Analyze warranty privity requirements (horizontal and vertical)
7. Determine Magnuson-Moss Act applicability for consumer goods

### Verification
- All three warranty types evaluated for applicability
- Disclaimer language assessed against UCC conspicuousness requirement
