# CL-004 — Assess Contract Enforceability

## Problema
Even properly formed contracts may be unenforceable due to defenses that void or voidable the agreement.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- CL-001 through CL-003 completed

### Steps
1. Screen for lack of capacity (minor, mental incapacity, intoxication)
2. Evaluate duress claims (physical, economic, undue influence)
3. Assess misrepresentation or fraud (fraudulent, negligent, innocent)
4. Check for mutual or unilateral mistake of material fact
5. Evaluate unconscionability (procedural and substantive)
6. Assess illegality or violation of public policy
7. Determine if Statute of Frauds applies and is satisfied

### Verification
- All seven enforceability defenses systematically evaluated
- Each defense analysis includes factual basis and legal standard
