# CL-014 — Calculate Remedies for Breach of Sale of Goods (UCC)

## Problema
UCC remedies differ from common law and provide specific formulas that must be correctly applied for buyer and seller claims.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- CL-012 completed
- CL-006 completed

### Steps
1. Calculate buyer's remedies: cover damages (2-712), market price damages (2-713), or accepted goods damages (2-714)
2. Calculate seller's remedies: resale damages (2-706), market price damages (2-708), or lost profit (2-708(2))
3. Add incidental damages for both buyer (2-715) and seller (2-710)
4. Evaluate buyer's consequential damages (2-715(2)) with foreseeability and mitigation
5. Assess buyer's right to specific performance (2-716) for unique goods
6. Determine if seller qualifies as lost volume seller for lost profit remedy
7. Evaluate contractual limitation of remedies and its enforceability

### Verification
- Correct UCC remedy formula applied based on party and situation
- Lost volume seller analysis completed if applicable
