# CL-009 — Analyze Third-Party Beneficiary Rights in Contracts

## Problema
Third parties may have enforceable rights under a contract they did not sign, creating unexpected obligations for contracting parties.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- CL-001 completed

### Steps
1. Determine if the third party is an intended or incidental beneficiary
2. Distinguish between creditor beneficiaries and donee beneficiaries
3. Assess when the third party's rights vest (knowledge + reliance, or suit filed)
4. Evaluate the promisor's defenses against the third-party beneficiary
5. Determine if the original parties can modify or rescind after vesting
6. Analyze government contract third-party beneficiary issues

### Verification
- Beneficiary correctly classified as intended vs incidental
- Vesting analysis identifies the triggering event
