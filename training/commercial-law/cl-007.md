# CL-007 — Determine Title Transfer and Risk of Loss in Goods Sales

## Problema
Disputes over damaged or lost goods during transit are among the most common commercial law conflicts.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- CL-006 completed

### Steps
1. Identify if contract contains explicit delivery terms (FOB, FAS, CIF, etc.)
2. Determine if seller is a merchant (affects risk of loss rules)
3. Apply UCC 2-509 for risk of loss without breach (shipment vs destination contracts)
4. Apply UCC 2-510 for risk of loss with breach
5. Evaluate insurable interest timing for both buyer and seller
6. Assess rights of good faith purchasers and entrustment doctrine

### Verification
- Risk of loss correctly assigned based on contract terms and breach status
- Delivery term classification (shipment vs destination) supported by contract language
