# CL-002 — Evaluate Whether a Valid Offer Exists

## Problema
Confusing preliminary negotiations, advertisements, or invitations to deal with binding offers creates legal exposure.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- CL-001 completed or basic contract law knowledge

### Steps
1. Identify the communication claimed to be an offer
2. Verify the offeror has present intent to be bound upon acceptance
3. Check that terms are definite and certain (parties, subject matter, price, time)
4. Distinguish from invitation to deal, advertisement, or preliminary negotiation
5. Determine if the offer has been communicated to the offeree
6. Assess whether the offer is still open (not revoked, rejected, lapsed, or terminated by operation of law)

### Verification
- Analysis addresses all four requirements: intent, definiteness, communication, and viability
- Edge cases (ads, auctions, rewards) correctly classified
