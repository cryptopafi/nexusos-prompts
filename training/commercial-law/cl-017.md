# CL-017 — Terminate an Agency Relationship

## Problema
Improper termination can leave the principal bound by agent actions or expose the terminating party to liability.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- CL-015 completed

### Steps
1. Identify termination method: revocation by principal, renunciation by agent, mutual agreement
2. Evaluate if the agency is coupled with an interest (irrevocable)
3. Provide proper notice of termination to the agent
4. Notify third parties who dealt with the agent of termination
5. Assess lingering apparent authority risks and mitigation
6. Handle post-termination duties: return of property, final accounting

### Verification
- Termination method legally valid for the agency type
- Third-party notification completed to prevent apparent authority claims
