# CL-011 — Determine Contract Discharge Conditions

## Problema
Knowing when a contract obligation ends prevents unnecessary performance and missed claims for non-performance.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- CL-001 completed

### Steps
1. Evaluate discharge by complete performance
2. Assess discharge by mutual agreement (rescission, accord and satisfaction, novation)
3. Determine if impossibility/impracticability applies (destruction, death, illegality)
4. Evaluate frustration of purpose as discharge basis
5. Assess force majeure clause applicability and scope
6. Determine if a condition precedent, concurrent, or subsequent has failed
7. Evaluate discharge by operation of law (bankruptcy, statute of limitations, merger)

### Verification
- All applicable discharge methods evaluated
- Force majeure analysis addresses specific contract language
