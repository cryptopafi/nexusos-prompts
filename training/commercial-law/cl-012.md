# CL-012 — Assess Contract Performance Standards

## Problema
Applying the wrong performance standard (substantial vs perfect tender) leads to incorrect breach determinations.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- CL-001 completed
- CL-006 recommended

### Steps
1. Determine if common law or UCC governs the performance standard
2. Apply substantial performance doctrine for common law contracts (services, real estate)
3. Apply perfect tender rule for UCC sale of goods (UCC 2-601)
4. Evaluate buyer's right to reject, accept, or revoke acceptance under UCC
5. Assess cure rights of the seller under UCC 2-508
6. Determine material vs minor breach and consequences for each
7. Evaluate installment contract performance standards (UCC 2-612)

### Verification
- Correct performance standard applied based on governing law
- Breach classified as material or minor with supporting analysis
