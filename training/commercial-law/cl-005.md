# CL-005 — Determine When Written vs Oral Contract Is Required

## Problema
Failing to reduce an agreement to writing when legally required renders the contract unenforceable regardless of performance.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- CL-001 completed

### Steps
1. Identify if the contract falls within Statute of Frauds categories: land, >1 year, goods >$500 (UCC), suretyship, marriage, executor promises
2. Determine what constitutes a sufficient writing or memorandum
3. Evaluate electronic signatures and records under UETA and E-SIGN
4. Assess exceptions: part performance, judicial admission, specially manufactured goods (UCC)
5. Apply the one-year rule correctly (capable of performance within one year)
6. Document the writing requirement determination with supporting analysis

### Verification
- Statute of Frauds applicability correctly determined for the specific transaction
- All applicable exceptions evaluated
