# CL-018 — Evaluate Employee Rights and Protections

## Problema
Employers face significant legal exposure from employment law violations that proper understanding can prevent.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Basic understanding of employment relationships

### Steps
1. Classify the worker: employee vs independent contractor using applicable tests (ABC, economic reality, common law)
2. Identify applicable wage and hour laws (FLSA: minimum wage, overtime, exempt/non-exempt)
3. Evaluate anti-discrimination protections (Title VII, ADA, ADEA, state laws)
4. Assess workplace safety obligations (OSHA requirements)
5. Review at-will employment doctrine and its exceptions (public policy, implied contract, good faith)
6. Evaluate whistleblower and retaliation protections
7. Assess employee privacy rights in the workplace

### Verification
- Worker correctly classified under applicable test
- All major federal employment protections identified for the situation
