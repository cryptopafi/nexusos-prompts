# CL-001 — Draft a Legally Enforceable Contract (Elements Checklist)

## Problema
Many business contracts fail enforceability tests because they lack one or more essential elements of contract formation.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Basic understanding of legal terminology

### Steps
1. Identify all parties and verify their legal capacity to contract
2. Draft a clear and definite offer with all material terms
3. Document valid acceptance that mirrors the offer terms
4. Establish adequate consideration for all parties
5. Verify the contract purpose is legal and not against public policy
6. Include necessary formalities (writing if required by Statute of Frauds)
7. Add boilerplate clauses: governing law, dispute resolution, severability, entire agreement

### Verification
- All six elements of contract formation present and documented
- Contract reviewed against applicable Statute of Frauds requirements
