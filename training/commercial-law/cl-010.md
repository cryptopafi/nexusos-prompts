# CL-010 — Evaluate Contract Assignment and Delegation

## Problema
Improper assignment or delegation can breach the original contract or leave parties without expected performance.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- CL-001 completed

### Steps
1. Determine if the contract right is assignable (check for anti-assignment clauses)
2. Identify exceptions to assignability: personal services, material change in obligor's duty, prohibited by law
3. Evaluate requirements for valid assignment (present transfer, no formalities needed at common law)
4. Determine if the duty is delegable (personal skill/judgment exception)
5. Assess the effect of delegation on the delegator's liability (remains liable unless novation)
6. Distinguish assignment from novation and its legal consequences

### Verification
- Assignability and delegability correctly determined
- Remaining liability after delegation clearly identified
