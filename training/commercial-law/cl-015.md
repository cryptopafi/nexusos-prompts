# CL-015 — Establish an Agency Relationship

## Problema
Agency relationships create binding legal obligations and must be properly established to protect all parties.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Basic understanding of business relationships

### Steps
1. Identify the essential elements: consent, control, and fiduciary duty
2. Determine agency type: actual authority (express or implied), apparent authority, ratification
3. Evaluate if the agent has capacity to act (less stringent than contract capacity)
4. Assess whether the relationship requires a writing (equal dignities rule)
5. Document the scope of authority clearly
6. Establish fiduciary duties: loyalty, obedience, care, accounting, notification
7. Determine compensation and expense reimbursement terms

### Verification
- All three essential elements documented
- Scope of authority clearly defined and communicated
