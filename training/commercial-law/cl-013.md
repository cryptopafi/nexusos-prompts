# CL-013 — Calculate Remedies for Breach of Contract (Common Law)

## Problema
Incorrect remedy calculation leaves the non-breaching party either under-compensated or pursuing unavailable relief.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- CL-012 completed

### Steps
1. Calculate expectation damages (benefit of the bargain: lost value + incidental - costs avoided)
2. Assess reliance damages as alternative measure
3. Calculate restitution damages to prevent unjust enrichment
4. Apply the mitigation doctrine (duty to avoid avoidable consequences)
5. Evaluate foreseeability limitation (Hadley v Baxendale: general vs special damages)
6. Assess certainty requirement for lost profits
7. Determine if specific performance is available (unique goods, land, inadequate money damages)
8. Evaluate liquidated damages clause enforceability

### Verification
- Damages calculated under at least two alternative measures
- Mitigation duty and foreseeability limits applied
