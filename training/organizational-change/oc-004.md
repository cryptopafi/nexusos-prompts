# OC-004 — Develop a Change Management Team

## Problema
Change efforts led solely by project teams or executives lack the cross-functional sponsorship needed for organization-wide adoption.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- OC-003 completed or change strategy selected

### Steps
1. Define change team roles: executive sponsor, change lead, change agents, champions network
2. Select executive sponsor with sufficient authority and credibility
3. Recruit change agents from across affected departments (formal and informal influencers)
4. Build a champion network of respected peer-level advocates
5. Define responsibilities and time commitments for each role
6. Train change team on selected change management methodology
7. Establish regular change team cadence: meetings, reporting, escalation
8. Create recognition program for change team contributions

### Verification
- All four change team roles filled with named individuals
- Change team represents all major affected stakeholder groups
