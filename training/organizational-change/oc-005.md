# OC-005 — Establish Change Management KPIs and Metrics

## Problema
Without measurable indicators, change managers cannot identify resistance early or demonstrate progress to sponsors.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- OC-003 completed

### Steps
1. Define leading indicators: awareness, understanding, willingness to change (survey-based)
2. Define adoption indicators: training completion, tool usage, process compliance rates
3. Define lagging indicators: productivity, quality, customer satisfaction, employee engagement
4. Establish baseline measurements before change implementation begins
5. Set targets for each metric with realistic timelines
6. Design measurement instruments: pulse surveys, system analytics, observation checklists
7. Create reporting dashboard for change team and sponsors
8. Plan metric review cadence and decision triggers (when to intervene)

### Verification
- KPIs cover leading, adoption, and lagging categories
- Baseline measurements established before change implementation
