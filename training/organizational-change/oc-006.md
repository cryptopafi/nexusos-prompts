# OC-006 — Design an Employee Feedback Collection System for Change

## Problema
Organizations that treat feedback as a one-time survey miss the continuous signals needed to steer change initiatives successfully.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- OC-001 completed
- OC-005 completed

### Steps
1. Design multi-channel feedback system: surveys, focus groups, suggestion boxes, manager conversations
2. Create pulse survey cadence (weekly or biweekly during active change, monthly during sustain phase)
3. Develop focus group protocol for deeper qualitative insight
4. Train managers to collect and report informal feedback systematically
5. Establish anonymous feedback channel for sensitive concerns
6. Create feedback analysis and synthesis process
7. Design closed-loop response: acknowledge feedback, communicate actions taken
8. Integrate feedback insights into change team decision-making

### Verification
- At least 3 feedback channels operational
- Closed-loop process documented showing feedback-to-action pathway
