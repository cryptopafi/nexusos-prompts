# PP-010 — Build a Kindness and Social Connection Practice

## Problema
Prosocial behavior is one of the strongest predictors of happiness, yet people rarely practice it intentionally and systematically.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Willingness to engage in prosocial behavior

### Steps
1. Commit to 5 acts of kindness per week (can be concentrated in one day or spread out)
2. Vary the acts: do not repeat the same kindness to avoid habituation
3. Include both small acts (compliment, hold door) and meaningful acts (help with project, volunteer)
4. Direct some acts toward strangers and some toward people you know
5. Notice the emotional impact on yourself during and after each act
6. Track acts and emotional responses for 4 weeks
7. Build at least one act of kindness into your weekly routine permanently

### Verification
- 5 acts per week maintained for 4 weeks
- Variety in types of kindness documented
