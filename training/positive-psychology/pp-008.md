# PP-008 — Thwart Hedonic Adaptation (4 Strategies)

## Problema
Hedonic adaptation causes people to return to baseline happiness after positive changes, wasting the potential of good circumstances.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Understanding of hedonic adaptation concept

### Steps
1. Strategy 1 - Variety: introduce variation into positive experiences to prevent habituation
2. Strategy 2 - Appreciation: practice gratitude and savoring for existing positive circumstances
3. Strategy 3 - Surprise: create or seek unexpected positive experiences
4. Strategy 4 - Scarcity: temporarily abstain from pleasures to reset sensitivity
5. Apply one strategy per week for 4 weeks as an experiment
6. Track subjective wellbeing before and after each week's strategy
7. Identify which strategies are most effective for your personal adaptation patterns

### Verification
- All four strategies practiced for at least one week each
- Subjective wellbeing tracked with at least pre/post measurement per strategy
