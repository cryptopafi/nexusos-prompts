# PP-002 — Conduct a Positivity Self-Test (Fredrickson Scale)

## Problema
Without a validated baseline, efforts to increase positivity lack direction and measurable outcomes.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- PP-001 completed or basic understanding of positive emotions

### Steps
1. Obtain the modified Differential Emotions Scale (mDES) or Positivity Self-Test
2. Complete the assessment honestly based on the past 24 hours
3. Score positive emotions (10 items) and negative emotions (10 items) separately
4. Calculate the positivity ratio: positive score / negative score
5. Interpret the ratio: 3:1 or higher associated with flourishing (Fredrickson threshold)
6. Identify which specific positive and negative emotions are most prevalent
7. Repeat weekly to track trends over time

### Verification
- Initial baseline score calculated
- Weekly tracking established for at least 4 weeks
