# PP-006 — Calculate and Track Your Positivity Ratio

## Problema
The positivity ratio provides a single actionable metric for monitoring emotional wellbeing over time.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- PP-002 completed

### Steps
1. Record positive and negative emotional experiences at end of each day
2. Use the Day Reconstruction Method: walk through the day chronologically
3. Count distinct positive emotional episodes and distinct negative emotional episodes
4. Calculate daily ratio: positive count / negative count
5. Calculate rolling weekly average ratio
6. Identify patterns: which days, contexts, or activities yield highest and lowest ratios
7. Set a target to maintain ratio above 3:1 and identify specific strategies to improve

### Verification
- Ratio tracked for at least 21 consecutive days
- Weekly averages calculated and trends identified
