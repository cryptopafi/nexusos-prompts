# PP-004 — Design a Savoring Practice Routine

## Problema
People habitually rush through positive experiences without extracting their full emotional and psychological benefit.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Basic mindfulness awareness

### Steps
1. Identify 3 daily moments worth savoring (morning coffee, sunset, conversation, meal)
2. Practice anticipatory savoring: look forward to an upcoming positive event with full attention
3. Practice in-the-moment savoring: slow down during the experience, engage all senses
4. Practice reminiscent savoring: at day's end, replay a positive moment in detail
5. Use savoring amplifiers: share with others, take a photo, write it down
6. Avoid savoring killers: comparing, thinking about when it ends, multitasking
7. Track savored moments daily for 2 weeks

### Verification
- At least one savoring practice per day for 14 days
- All three temporal orientations (anticipatory, in-the-moment, reminiscent) practiced
