# PP-001 — Build a Positivity Portfolio (10 Positive Emotions Tracking)

## Problema
People underestimate the range and frequency of positive emotions they experience, limiting their ability to cultivate wellbeing.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Notebook or digital journaling tool

### Steps
1. Learn Fredrickson's 10 positive emotions: joy, gratitude, serenity, interest, hope, pride, amusement, inspiration, awe, love
2. Create a tracking template with all 10 emotions
3. At the end of each day, record which positive emotions you experienced and their context
4. Note the triggers and circumstances for each emotion
5. Track for a minimum of 14 consecutive days
6. Review patterns: which emotions are frequent, which are rare
7. Identify opportunities to intentionally cultivate underrepresented emotions

### Verification
- 14 days of tracking completed
- At least 7 of 10 emotions identified and logged with context
