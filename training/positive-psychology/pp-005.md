# PP-005 — Create a Gratitude Journaling Practice

## Problema
Gratitude journaling is one of the most validated positive psychology interventions but is often done incorrectly, reducing its impact.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Journal or digital note-taking tool

### Steps
1. Choose a consistent time for gratitude journaling (evening recommended)
2. Write 3-5 specific things you are grateful for each session
3. Include WHY you are grateful and WHO contributed to each item
4. Vary your entries: avoid repeating the same items
5. Focus on people and experiences rather than material things
6. Write with depth rather than breadth: one detailed entry beats five superficial ones
7. Practice 3 times per week (not daily, to avoid habituation)

### Verification
- Gratitude journal maintained for at least 4 weeks
- Entries show specificity and variety (not generic repetitions)
