# PP-015 — Implement a Daily Mindfulness/Meditation Routine

## Problema
Mindfulness meditation has robust evidence for reducing stress and increasing positive emotions but requires consistent daily practice to deliver benefits.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Quiet space and commitment to daily practice

### Steps
1. Start with 5-minute sessions (increase by 1 minute per week up to 15-20 minutes)
2. Choose a consistent time and place to anchor the habit
3. Begin with breath-focused meditation: attend to breath sensations, note wandering, return
4. Practice non-judgmental awareness: observe thoughts without engaging or evaluating
5. Add body scan meditation once per week for somatic awareness
6. Track daily: duration, quality of focus (1-10), mood before and after
7. After 4 weeks, explore additional techniques: open monitoring, compassion meditation
8. Maintain practice through the 'dip' (weeks 2-3 when novelty wears off and benefits are not yet felt)

### Verification
- Daily practice maintained for at least 30 consecutive days
- Tracking data shows trend in mood improvement pre vs post session
