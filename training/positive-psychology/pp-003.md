# PP-003 — Practice Loving-Kindness Meditation (Guided Protocol)

## Problema
Loving-kindness meditation is the most researched intervention for increasing daily positive emotions and social connection.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Quiet space and 15-20 minutes daily

### Steps
1. Find a comfortable seated position and close your eyes
2. Begin with self-directed phrases: 'May I be safe. May I be happy. May I be healthy. May I live with ease.'
3. Extend to a loved one: visualize them and repeat the phrases directed at them
4. Extend to a neutral person (acquaintance): repeat phrases
5. Extend to a difficult person: repeat phrases (start mildly difficult, progress over time)
6. Extend to all beings: 'May all beings be safe, happy, healthy, and live with ease'
7. Practice daily for a minimum of 6 weeks to build the habit and see effects

### Verification
- Daily practice maintained for at least 6 consecutive weeks
- Noticeable shift in feelings toward neutral and difficult persons
