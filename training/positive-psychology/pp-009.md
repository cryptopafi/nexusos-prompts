# PP-009 — Reset Reference Points for Happiness

## Problema
Social comparison and rising expectations silently erode happiness by shifting the benchmark against which we evaluate our lives.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- PP-008 completed or understanding of comparison processes

### Steps
1. Identify your current reference points for key life domains (income, relationships, health, achievement)
2. Assess whether reference points are based on social comparison, past experience, or expectations
3. Practice downward comparison consciously: acknowledge advantages relative to less fortunate circumstances
4. Limit exposure to upward comparison triggers (social media, lifestyle marketing)
5. Reset expectations by connecting with gratitude for current state
6. Practice 'mental subtraction': imagine your life without a positive element to restore appreciation
7. Revisit reference points monthly to prevent drift

### Verification
- Reference points identified for at least 3 life domains
- Mental subtraction exercise completed and reflected upon
