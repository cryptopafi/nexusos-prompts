# PP-007 — Apply Broaden-and-Build to Daily Decision-Making

## Problema
Fredrickson's Broaden-and-Build theory shows positive emotions expand thinking, but most people do not intentionally leverage this.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- PP-001 completed
- Understanding of Broaden-and-Build theory

### Steps
1. Identify a decision or challenge requiring creative or flexible thinking
2. Before engaging the problem, induce a positive emotional state (gratitude exercise, brief meditation, savoring)
3. Notice the broadened attention: consider more options and perspectives than usual
4. Explore novel approaches you would not consider in a neutral or negative state
5. Build durable resources: note new relationships, skills, or knowledge generated
6. Reflect on how the positive state affected the quality of your decision
7. Practice this broaden-before-decide approach for 2 weeks

### Verification
- Broaden-before-decide approach applied to at least 5 significant decisions
- Documented comparison of decision quality with and without positive state induction
