# PP-011 — Design a Time Affluence Schedule

## Problema
Feeling time-poor is a stronger predictor of unhappiness than being income-poor, making time affluence a critical wellbeing lever.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Time audit data or WS-002 completed

### Steps
1. Audit current time usage for 1 week (track in 30-minute blocks)
2. Identify time traps: activities consuming time disproportionate to their value or enjoyment
3. Calculate your time affluence score: hours per week of genuine discretionary time
4. Eliminate or delegate 3 low-value time commitments
5. Buy time where possible: outsource tasks that drain energy (cleaning, commuting, errands)
6. Protect time affluence: schedule discretionary time blocks as non-negotiable
7. Use recovered time for activities with highest wellbeing return (social connection, flow activities, nature)

### Verification
- Time audit completed for full week
- At least 3 hours per week of new discretionary time created
