# PP-013 — Build Situation Support for Habit Change

## Problema
Willpower is a depleting resource, making environmental design a more reliable strategy for sustaining behavior change.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- PP-012 completed or specific behavior change goal identified

### Steps
1. Identify the target behavior you want to increase or decrease
2. Audit your current environment for cues that trigger the old behavior
3. Design situation support: make desired behavior easy and visible
4. Design situation barriers: make undesired behavior hard and invisible
5. Apply commitment devices: pre-commit to the behavior with social or financial stakes
6. Stack the new behavior onto an existing habit (habit stacking)
7. Monitor environment effectiveness and adjust for 30 days

### Verification
- Environment modified with at least 3 specific changes
- Target behavior tracked daily for 30 days
