# PBR-046 — Feature Influencers to Boost Sharing

## Problema
Featuring influencers in your content gives them a reason to share it with their audience, giving you exposure to followers you could never reach organically.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Content creation capability
- Network of 10+ creators in your niche

### Steps
1. Create content formats designed for featuring: expert roundups, quote graphics, interview series, 'creators to follow' lists.
2. Choose 5 influencers per piece: mix of larger names (aspirational) and peers (likely to share).
3. Tag and notify every featured influencer when the content publishes.
4. Make sharing easy: send them a pre-written share message and the direct link.
5. Track which influencers share and which don't. Focus future features on those who reciprocate.
6. Build relationships before featuring: comment on their content, share their work, and engage genuinely first.
7. Repeat monthly: a consistent featuring strategy builds you a reputation as a connector in your niche.

### Verification
- First feature piece published with 5 influencers
- All featured influencers notified with share-ready materials
- Share rate tracked: how many of 5 featured influencers reshared
