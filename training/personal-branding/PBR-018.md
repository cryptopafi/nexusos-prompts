# PBR-018 — Make Viral Instagram Reels

## Problema
Reels are Instagram's highest-reach format, but most creators make Reels that look like repurposed TikToks without platform-native optimization.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Instagram account with Reels access
- Content niche defined (PBR-001)

### Steps
1. Study the Reels algorithm: watch time is the #1 signal. Your Reel must hook viewers in the first 1.5 seconds.
2. Create a hook library: 20 opening lines or visuals that stop the scroll (questions, shocking stats, pattern interrupts).
3. Keep Reels between 7-15 seconds for maximum completion rate (shorter = higher watch time ratio).
4. Use trending audio when it aligns with your niche (check Reels tab for trending sounds).
5. Add text overlays: 40% of users watch with sound off. Your message must work visually.
6. End with a CTA that encourages replay or sharing: 'Watch again to catch the detail' or 'Tag someone who needs this.'
7. Post 5-7 Reels per week and analyze: which hooks, topics, and lengths perform best?

### Verification
- Hook library of 20+ openers created
- 5-7 Reels posted per week for 2 weeks
- Top-performing hook and topic patterns identified
