# PBR-027 — Plan a YouTube Show for Personal Branding

## Problema
Random YouTube videos don't build a channel. A show format with consistent structure creates viewer habits and subscriber loyalty.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Niche defined (PBR-001)
- YouTube channel set up (PBR-037)

### Steps
1. Define your show concept: what is the recurring format? (weekly tutorial, interview series, challenge, review, commentary).
2. Name your show and create branded intro/outro templates (keep intro under 5 seconds).
3. Plan your content calendar: 12 episodes covering 12 sub-topics within your niche.
4. Create a standard episode structure: hook (0-15 sec), intro (5 sec), content (5-10 min), CTA (30 sec), end screen.
5. Batch-film content: record 4 episodes per filming session to maintain consistency with less effort.
6. Set a publishing schedule: same day and time every week (consistency trains the algorithm and your audience).
7. Create a show trailer (60-90 seconds) explaining what viewers will learn and why they should subscribe.

### Verification
- Show concept and name defined
- 12-episode content calendar created
- Publishing schedule set and maintained for 4 weeks
