# PBR-012 — Write a Sponsorship Pitch Email Template

## Problema
Cold sponsorship outreach fails when the pitch is generic or focuses on the creator instead of the brand's ROI.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Media kit or audience analytics
- Brand niche defined (PBR-001)

### Steps
1. Research the target brand: their marketing objectives, recent campaigns, target audience overlap with yours.
2. Write the subject line: specific and benefit-focused (e.g., 'Reaching 50K marketing professionals who buy your product category').
3. Paragraph 1: Acknowledge something specific about their brand (shows you did research).
4. Paragraph 2: Introduce yourself with one line on your audience size, demographics, and engagement rate.
5. Paragraph 3: Propose a specific collaboration idea (not 'let's work together' but 'I'd create a 3-part video series showing...').
6. Paragraph 4: Include social proof (past brand partnerships, testimonials, or case study metrics).
7. Close with a clear CTA: 'Would you be open to a 15-minute call this week to explore this?'
8. Attach or link your media kit with audience demographics, reach metrics, and rate card.

### Verification
- Template includes all 4 paragraphs plus CTA
- Subject line is specific and benefit-focused
- Media kit attached with audience data
