# PBR-017 — Create Instagram Stories Strategy

## Problema
Stories are the most intimate content format on Instagram, but without a strategy they become random updates that don't build brand affinity.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Active Instagram account
- Brand personality defined

### Steps
1. Define 5 Story content pillars: behind-the-scenes, tips/education, polls/questions, user-generated content, and personal moments.
2. Create a weekly Story calendar: assign 1-2 pillars per day for variety.
3. Use interactive stickers in every Story: polls, questions, quizzes, sliders (boosts engagement and algorithm ranking).
4. Design Story highlights covers that match your brand visual identity.
5. Organize highlights by topic: About Me, Tips, Reviews/Testimonials, Portfolio, FAQs.
6. Post 3-7 Story frames per day (the sweet spot for views without overwhelming).
7. Track Story metrics weekly: reach, exits, replies, and sticker interactions.

### Verification
- 5 content pillars defined
- Story highlights organized with branded covers
- 3-7 Stories posted daily for 2 weeks with metrics tracked
