# PBR-016 — Optimize for the Instagram Feed Algorithm

## Problema
Posting randomly without understanding the algorithm leads to poor reach and discouraging growth stalls.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Active Instagram account
- Content creation capability

### Steps
1. Understand the ranking signals: relationship (interactions with followers), interest (content relevance), timeliness (recency).
2. Post when your audience is active: check Instagram Insights for your specific audience peak times.
3. Create content in formats the algorithm favors: Reels (highest reach), Carousels (highest saves), Stories (highest engagement).
4. Write captions that drive engagement: ask questions, use CTAs (save this, share with a friend), and tell stories.
5. Respond to every comment within 1 hour of posting to boost the engagement signal.
6. Use 5-10 relevant hashtags (not 30): mix niche-specific, medium-reach, and broad hashtags.
7. Maintain a consistent posting schedule: minimum 4 feed posts per week.

### Verification
- Posting times aligned with audience peak activity
- Content mix includes Reels, Carousels, and Stories
- 4+ feed posts per week maintained for 4 weeks
