# PBR-037 — Set Up YouTube Channel Branding

## Problema
A YouTube channel without consistent branding looks amateur and fails to convert visitors into subscribers.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Brand name and visual identity (PBR-005, PBR-007)

### Steps
1. Create a channel banner at 2560x1440 pixels: include your name, tagline, and upload schedule.
2. Upload your professional brand photo as the channel profile picture.
3. Write the channel description: who you are, what the channel covers, why someone should subscribe, and upload schedule.
4. Add channel keywords in YouTube Studio settings (your niche topics).
5. Create a channel trailer for non-subscribers (60-90 seconds): introduce yourself, show highlights, and CTA to subscribe.
6. Organize videos into playlists by topic or series.
7. Add channel links: website, social media, and email in the banner and About section.

### Verification
- Banner uploaded at correct dimensions
- Channel trailer published
- Videos organized into playlists
