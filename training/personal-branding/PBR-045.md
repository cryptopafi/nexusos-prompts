# PBR-045 — Run a Social Media Contest for Growth

## Problema
Contests accelerate follower growth by incentivizing sharing and engagement, but poorly structured contests attract freebie-seekers instead of real fans.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Active social media account with 500+ followers
- Prize budget or sponsor

### Steps
1. Define the contest goal: followers, email subscribers, UGC content, or engagement. Pick one primary metric.
2. Choose a contest type: giveaway (follow + tag), photo/video challenge (UGC), quiz, or creative submission.
3. Select a prize that attracts your target audience specifically (not just anyone): niche-relevant products, experiences, or access.
4. Create clear rules: entry method, deadline, winner selection criteria, legal disclaimers.
5. Promote the contest: organic posts, Stories, email, and optional paid boost ($50-100 amplifies reach significantly).
6. Maximize viral mechanics: bonus entries for tagging friends, sharing, or creating content.
7. Announce the winner publicly, then analyze: new followers gained, cost per follower, and retention rate 30 days later.

### Verification
- Contest goal and type defined
- Contest launched with clear rules and promotion
- Post-contest analysis includes retention rate at 30 days
