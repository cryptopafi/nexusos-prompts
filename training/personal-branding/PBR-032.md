# PBR-032 — Write Attention-Grabbing YouTube Video Titles

## Problema
A great video with a boring title gets no clicks. Your title competes with hundreds of others on the search results page.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Video topic and target keyword defined

### Steps
1. Start with the keyword: search intent should be immediately clear from the title.
2. Write 10 title variations for every video before choosing one.
3. Apply proven title formulas: How to [Result] in [Timeframe], [Number] [Topic] Mistakes (and How to Fix Them), Why [Common Belief] is Wrong.
4. Add emotional triggers: curiosity gap (don't reveal everything), urgency (before it's too late), social proof (used by 10K+ marketers).
5. Keep under 60 characters to avoid truncation in search results.
6. Avoid clickbait that doesn't deliver: misleading titles kill watch time and destroy channel reputation.
7. A/B test titles: change the title of underperforming videos after 48 hours and track CTR changes.

### Verification
- 10 title variations written per video
- Final title under 60 characters with keyword
- CTR tracked and titles A/B tested on underperformers
