# PBR-034 — Optimize for YouTube Recommendation Algorithm

## Problema
YouTube search drives discovery, but recommendations drive 70%+ of watch time. Understanding browse and suggested signals is essential for growth.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Active YouTube channel with 10+ videos

### Steps
1. Understand the three recommendation surfaces: Home (browse), Suggested (sidebar), and Shorts shelf.
2. Optimize for watch time: the algorithm promotes videos that keep people watching. Cut slow intros and optimize pacing.
3. Increase session duration: use playlists, series, and end screens to keep viewers on YOUR channel (not just YouTube).
4. Improve CTR: test thumbnails and titles. Aim for 5%+ CTR on impressions.
5. Track audience retention graphs: identify exactly where viewers drop off and fix those moments in future videos.
6. Create content clusters: 5-10 videos on closely related topics. The algorithm recommends topically related content.
7. Study your 'Impressions and how they led to watch time' funnel in YouTube Studio.

### Verification
- Audience retention analyzed for top 10 videos
- Content clusters of 5+ related videos created
- CTR above 5% on recent uploads
