# PBR-038 — Collaborate with Other YouTubers (Outreach Template)

## Problema
Collaborations expose you to established audiences, but most outreach emails are so generic that creators delete them instantly.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Active YouTube channel with content
- Niche defined (PBR-001)

### Steps
1. Identify 20 potential collaborators: channels in your niche with 2x-10x your subscriber count (not too small, not too large).
2. Engage genuinely first: watch their content, leave thoughtful comments, share their videos for 2-4 weeks before pitching.
3. Write your outreach email using this structure: genuine compliment on specific video, shared audience connection, specific collab idea with clear benefit to them, make it easy (suggest format, you handle editing).
4. Send outreach to 5 creators per week. Expect a 10-20% positive response rate.
5. For accepted collabs: plan content that serves both audiences, cross-promote on both channels, and create a CTA driving viewers to the other channel.
6. After the collab: thank them publicly, share results, and maintain the relationship for future partnerships.

### Verification
- 20 potential collaborators identified and categorized
- Pre-outreach engagement done for 2+ weeks
- 5 outreach emails sent per week with tracking
