# PBR-013 — Find and Connect with Potential Brand Sponsors

## Problema
Waiting for brands to find you limits sponsorship revenue. Proactive outreach to the right brands at the right time accelerates monetization.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Sponsorship pitch template (PBR-012)
- Audience demographics data

### Steps
1. List 50 brands that advertise to your audience: check competitors' sponsorships, podcast ads, and social media ads in your niche.
2. Research each brand's marketing contact: use LinkedIn, Hunter.io, or the brand's agency roster.
3. Prioritize brands by fit: audience overlap (high), budget (medium-large), and campaign timing (launching new products).
4. Segment your list into tiers: Tier 1 (dream brands, 10), Tier 2 (strong fit, 20), Tier 3 (good fit, 20).
5. Send personalized outreach to 5 brands per week using your pitch template (PBR-012).
6. Follow up once after 5 business days if no response. Do not follow up more than twice.
7. Track all outreach in a CRM or spreadsheet: brand, contact, date sent, response, status.

### Verification
- 50 target brands identified and categorized
- 5 outreach emails sent per week
- Tracking system active with response rates
