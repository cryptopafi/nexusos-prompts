# PBR-009 — Set Up SEO for Personal Reputation Management

## Problema
When someone Googles your name, you want to control what they find. SEO for personal branding ensures the top results tell your story.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Personal brand name secured (PBR-005)
- LinkedIn profile optimized (PBR-008)

### Steps
1. Google your name and document the current top 10 results. Classify each as positive, neutral, or negative.
2. Claim and optimize profiles on high-authority platforms: LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Medium, YouTube, Crunchbase, About.me.
3. Create a personal website with your name as the domain. Include: about page, blog, portfolio, and contact.
4. Publish 3-5 guest articles on high-DA sites in your niche with author bios linking to your site.
5. Set up Google Alerts for your name to monitor new mentions.
6. Build backlinks to your profiles: cross-link all platforms, contribute to interviews, and appear on podcasts.
7. Review and update quarterly: push positive results up by creating more content on high-authority platforms.

### Verification
- Top 10 Google results documented and classified
- Personal website live with all key pages
- Google Alerts active for your name
