# PBR-008 — Create a LinkedIn Profile for Reputation Management

## Problema
LinkedIn profiles that read like resumes fail to attract opportunities. A brand-optimized LinkedIn profile is a landing page for your professional reputation.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Brand pitch (PBR-006)
- Professional photo (PBR-007)

### Steps
1. Write a headline using your byline formula (not your job title): 'I help X achieve Y through Z.'
2. Write an About section: start with a hook, state the problem you solve, share your credibility, and end with a CTA.
3. Optimize the Featured section: pin your best content, lead magnet, or portfolio piece.
4. Rewrite Experience entries as impact stories: 'Achieved X by doing Y, resulting in Z.'
5. Request 5 strategic recommendations from people who can speak to your brand promise.
6. Add relevant skills (max 50) and get endorsements for the top 3 skills that align with your niche.
7. Set a LinkedIn content schedule: 3 posts per week minimum (text posts perform best for reach).

### Verification
- Headline uses byline formula
- About section has hook, problem, credibility, and CTA
- Content schedule active with 3 posts/week
