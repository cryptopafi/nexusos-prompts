# PBR-007 — Design a Professional Brand Photo

## Problema
A low-quality or inconsistent profile photo undermines credibility before anyone reads a single word of your content.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Brand personality and niche defined (PBR-001)

### Steps
1. Define the impression you want your photo to create: approachable, authoritative, creative, professional.
2. Choose a background that reinforces your brand: solid color (clean/minimal), workspace (expert), outdoors (adventurous).
3. Dress for your audience: what would your ideal follower expect from someone they trust in your niche?
4. Take photos in natural light or professional lighting. Avoid harsh shadows and cluttered backgrounds.
5. Shoot at least 30 photos with slight variations in expression, angle, and pose.
6. Select the top 3 and get feedback from 5 people: which photo makes you most trustworthy/compelling?
7. Edit the winner for consistency: same color grading across all platforms. Use the same photo everywhere.

### Verification
- 30+ photos taken
- Top photo selected with audience feedback
- Same photo deployed across all platforms
