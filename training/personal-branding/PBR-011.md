# PBR-011 — Control Google Image Results for Your Name

## Problema
Google Image results for your name can show random or unflattering photos. Controlling this visual first impression is part of reputation management.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Professional brand photo (PBR-007)
- SEO foundation (PBR-009)

### Steps
1. Google Image search your name and document the current results.
2. Upload your professional brand photo to all owned profiles with your name in the filename and alt text.
3. Publish your photo on high-authority platforms: LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Medium, YouTube, Gravatar.
4. Add structured data (schema markup) to your personal website with your photo for rich results.
5. Create image-rich content (infographics, presentations) attributed to your name on SlideShare, Pinterest, and your blog.
6. Ensure consistent naming: all image files should include your full name (e.g., firstname-lastname-speaker.jpg).

### Verification
- Professional photo uploaded to all major platforms
- Image filenames include your name
- Google Image results show your controlled photos within 30 days
