# PBR-047 — Create a Book/eBook with AI

## Problema
Writing a book positions you as an authority in your niche, and AI tools reduce the writing time from months to weeks while maintaining quality.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Niche expertise defined (PBR-001)
- AI writing tool access (ChatGPT, Claude)

### Steps
1. Define your book concept: what specific problem does it solve for your target reader?
2. Create a detailed outline: 8-12 chapters, each with 3-5 sections. The outline is the most important step.
3. Use AI to draft each section: provide the outline, key points, your voice guidelines, and examples. Review and rewrite every output.
4. Edit ruthlessly: remove AI-generic phrases, add personal stories and unique insights, and ensure your voice is consistent.
5. Hire a professional editor or use a beta reader group (5 people from your target audience).
6. Design the cover: use Canva for eBooks, or hire a designer on Fiverr for print ($50-150).
7. Format for publishing: EPUB for eBooks, PDF for lead magnets, KDP for Amazon (PBR-048).

### Verification
- Book outline completed with 8-12 chapters
- Draft completed and edited with personal voice
- Cover designed and book formatted for target platform
