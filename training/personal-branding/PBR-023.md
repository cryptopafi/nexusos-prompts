# PBR-023 — Create Retargeting Ads for E-Commerce

## Problema
97% of first-time visitors don't buy. Retargeting ads bring them back at a fraction of the cost of acquiring new visitors.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Pixel and audiences set up (PBR-022)
- E-commerce product catalog

### Steps
1. Create a retargeting audience: website visitors who viewed products but didn't purchase (7, 14, and 30 day windows).
2. Create a cart abandonment audience: visitors who added to cart but didn't complete checkout.
3. Design retargeting ad creative: show the exact product they viewed (Dynamic Product Ads) or category-level reminder ads.
4. Write retargeting copy that addresses common objections: free shipping, money-back guarantee, limited stock, discount code.
5. Set frequency caps: max 3 impressions per person per day to avoid ad fatigue.
6. Create a post-purchase audience and exclude them from retargeting campaigns.
7. A/B test: product image ads vs. testimonial/review ads for retargeting.

### Verification
- Retargeting audiences created for 3 time windows
- Dynamic Product Ads or category ads running
- Frequency cap set and post-purchase exclusion active
