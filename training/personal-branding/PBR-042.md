# PBR-042 — Improve On-Camera Presence and Body Language

## Problema
Stiff, nervous, or monotone on-camera delivery kills audience retention regardless of how good the content is.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Recording setup ready (PBR-041)

### Steps
1. Record yourself speaking for 2 minutes on any topic. Watch it back and note: energy level, eye contact, hand gestures, filler words.
2. Practice the 'talking to a friend' technique: imagine one specific person you are helping and speak directly to them.
3. Improve eye contact: look at the camera lens, not the screen. Tape an arrow next to your lens as a reminder.
4. Use hand gestures deliberately: open palms (trust), counting on fingers (structure), expansive gestures (emphasis).
5. Vary your vocal delivery: speed up for excitement, slow down for emphasis, pause before key points.
6. Eliminate filler words (um, uh, like): practice short takes and re-record until clean.
7. Record and review yourself weekly: compare week 1 to week 4 for visible improvement.

### Verification
- Initial 2-minute recording analyzed
- Weekly recording practice established
- Visible improvement documented over 4 weeks
