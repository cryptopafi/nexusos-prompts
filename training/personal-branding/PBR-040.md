# PBR-040 — Go Live on YouTube

## Problema
Live streaming creates real-time audience connection that pre-recorded content cannot match, driving deeper loyalty and engagement.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- YouTube channel active
- Basic streaming setup (webcam, mic)

### Steps
1. Check eligibility: you need 50+ subscribers to go live from mobile, no requirements for desktop.
2. Set up streaming software: OBS Studio (free) or Streamlabs for professional-looking streams.
3. Plan your stream: topic, outline (not a script), duration (30-60 minutes for starting), and CTA.
4. Promote the stream 24-48 hours in advance: community post, Story, email to subscribers.
5. Go live: start with a 2-minute warm-up (greet viewers, state the topic), deliver content, and engage with chat throughout.
6. Enable Super Chat and Super Stickers for live monetization.
7. After the stream: edit the replay into a polished video or extract clips for Shorts.

### Verification
- OBS or streaming software configured and tested
- First live stream completed
- Replay edited or clipped for additional content
