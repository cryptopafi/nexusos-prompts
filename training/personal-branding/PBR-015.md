# PBR-015 — Write an Instagram Bio for Personal Branding

## Problema
Your Instagram bio is a 150-character landing page. A weak bio means lost followers from every profile visit.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Brand pitch (PBR-006)
- Niche defined (PBR-001)

### Steps
1. Line 1: State what you do and for whom (use your byline from PBR-006).
2. Line 2: Add a credibility signal (featured in..., helped X people..., built Y...).
3. Line 3: Include a clear CTA (Download my free guide / New video every Tuesday / DM me for...).
4. Add relevant emojis sparingly to improve scannability (1-2 per line maximum).
5. Set your link in bio: use a link tool (Linktree, Stan Store, or custom landing page) to maximize the single link.
6. Choose a @username that matches your brand name across all platforms.

### Verification
- Bio includes what you do, credibility, and CTA
- Link in bio set up with tracking
- Username consistent across platforms
