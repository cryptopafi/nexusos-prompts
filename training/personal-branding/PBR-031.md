# PBR-031 — Optimize YouTube Video On-Page SEO

## Problema
Great content with poor on-page SEO gets buried. Title, description, and tags must be optimized for both the algorithm and human click-through.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Keyword research completed (PBR-029)
- Video ready to publish

### Steps
1. Title: Include your primary keyword in the first 5 words. Keep under 60 characters. Add a power word (Ultimate, Proven, Simple).
2. Description: Write 200+ words. Include primary keyword in the first 2 sentences. Add timestamps, links, and related keywords naturally.
3. Tags: Add 10-15 tags. Start with exact-match keyword, then variations, then broad topic tags.
4. Thumbnail: Design a custom thumbnail with text overlay, face (if applicable), and contrasting colors (PBR-033).
5. Add cards at key moments linking to related videos (keeps viewers in your ecosystem).
6. Create an end screen: subscribe button + next video + playlist.
7. Add to a relevant playlist: playlists boost watch time and session duration signals.
8. Publish and share immediately on 2-3 platforms to generate initial velocity.

### Verification
- Title includes keyword in first 5 words
- Description is 200+ words with keyword in first 2 sentences
- Custom thumbnail, cards, and end screen added
