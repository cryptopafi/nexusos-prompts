# PBR-035 — Create and Optimize YouTube Shorts

## Problema
YouTube Shorts reach new audiences that long-form never touches. They act as a top-of-funnel for channel subscriber growth.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- YouTube channel active
- Short-form video creation capability

### Steps
1. Create Shorts at 9:16 aspect ratio, under 60 seconds (15-30 seconds performs best).
2. Hook in the first 2 seconds: start with motion, a bold statement, or a visual surprise.
3. Deliver one clear idea per Short: tip, fact, reaction, or micro-tutorial.
4. Add text overlays for accessibility and engagement (many watch without sound).
5. Use trending sounds from the Shorts creation tool when relevant to your niche.
6. End with a hook that drives to your long-form: 'Full tutorial on my channel' or 'Part 2 is a full video.'
7. Post 3-5 Shorts per week and track: which topics drive the most subscriber conversions (not just views)?

### Verification
- Shorts filmed at correct aspect ratio under 60 seconds
- 3-5 Shorts posted per week for 4 weeks
- Subscriber conversion tracked per Short
