# PBR-049 — Build a Book Business Plan and Marketing Strategy

## Problema
Publishing a book without a marketing plan results in zero sales. A book needs a launch strategy just like any other product.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Book completed or near completion (PBR-047)
- Personal brand audience

### Steps
1. Define your book launch goal: number of copies sold, Amazon bestseller rank target, or email list growth.
2. Build a launch team: 20-50 people who commit to buying and reviewing the book in the first week.
3. Create a pre-launch sequence (30 days out): tease the book on social media, share behind-the-scenes, and build anticipation.
4. Set up a launch day promotion: discount pricing, email blast to your list, social media push, and launch team reviews go live.
5. Pitch podcast appearances and guest posts timed to launch week for maximum visibility.
6. Run Amazon Ads: Sponsored Product ads targeting competitor books and niche keywords ($5-10/day starting budget).
7. Post-launch: maintain momentum with weekly content related to the book's topic, speaking engagements, and ongoing Amazon Ads.

### Verification
- Launch team of 20+ recruited
- 30-day pre-launch sequence executed
- Launch week includes reviews, email blast, social push, and podcast appearances
