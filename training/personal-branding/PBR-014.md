# PBR-014 — Negotiate and Maximize Revenue from Brand Deals

## Problema
Creators undervalue their work because they lack negotiation frameworks and market rate data, leaving money on the table.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Active sponsorship conversation
- Audience metrics and media kit

### Steps
1. Research market rates: use industry benchmarks (e.g., $100 CPM for YouTube, $10-20 CPM for Instagram) adjusted for your niche and engagement.
2. Never share your rate first. Ask the brand for their budget range.
3. Calculate your floor rate: the minimum you'll accept based on time, production cost, and audience value.
4. Structure the deal as a package, not a single post: bundle content types (video + stories + post) for a higher total.
5. Add value without lowering price: offer usage rights extensions, extra platform coverage, or performance bonuses.
6. Request a performance bonus clause: if engagement exceeds benchmarks, you earn a bonus (aligns your incentives with the brand).
7. Get everything in writing: deliverables, timeline, usage rights, payment terms (net-30 maximum), and kill fee.

### Verification
- Market rate research documented
- Floor rate calculated
- Contract includes all key terms in writing
