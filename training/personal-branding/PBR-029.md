# PBR-029 — Conduct YouTube SEO Keyword Research

## Problema
Creating YouTube videos without keyword research means relying on luck for discovery. SEO-driven topic selection ensures your videos get found.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- YouTube channel active
- Niche defined (PBR-001)

### Steps
1. Start with YouTube autocomplete: type your main topic and note all suggested completions.
2. Use YouTube's search filter to check competition: search your keyword and count videos with under 10K views in the top 10 (low competition signal).
3. Use free tools: Google Trends (YouTube filter), Keywords Everywhere, or TubeBuddy free tier.
4. Build a keyword spreadsheet: keyword, monthly search volume estimate, competition level, content angle.
5. Identify 20 keywords with medium search volume and low-medium competition (your sweet spot as a growing channel).
6. Group keywords into content clusters: 3-5 related keywords per cluster to build topical authority.
7. Prioritize: create videos for the highest-opportunity keywords first.

### Verification
- 20+ keywords researched with volume and competition data
- Keywords grouped into content clusters
- Priority list created for next 10 videos
