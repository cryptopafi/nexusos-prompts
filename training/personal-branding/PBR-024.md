# PBR-024 — Set Up a TikTok Account for Brand Growth

## Problema
TikTok's discovery algorithm gives new accounts massive reach potential, but only if the account is optimized for the algorithm from day one.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Niche defined (PBR-001)
- Brand photo and byline ready (PBR-006, PBR-007)

### Steps
1. Download TikTok and create a Business or Creator account (Creator for personal branding).
2. Set your username to match your brand name across all platforms.
3. Write your bio: who you help + what they get + CTA (link in bio).
4. Set up a TikTok link in bio using Linktree or Stan Store.
5. Study your niche: watch 50 TikToks in your topic, save the top performers, and note patterns (hooks, formats, lengths).
6. Create and post your first 10 TikToks within the first 3 days (the algorithm tests new accounts aggressively).
7. Post 1-3 TikToks per day for the first 30 days to establish your account in the algorithm.

### Verification
- Account set up with optimized bio and link
- 50 niche TikToks studied with patterns noted
- 10 TikToks posted within first 3 days
