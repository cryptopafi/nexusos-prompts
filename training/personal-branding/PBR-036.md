# PBR-036 — Boost YouTube Rankings with Social Media Automation

## Problema
YouTube videos need initial velocity (views in the first 24-48 hours) to trigger algorithmic promotion. Social media automation drives that early momentum.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- YouTube channel active
- Social media accounts on 2+ platforms

### Steps
1. Set up auto-sharing: every new YouTube upload triggers a share on Twitter/X, Facebook, LinkedIn, and Reddit (use Zapier or IFTTT).
2. Create platform-specific teasers: a 15-second clip for Instagram Reels, a text thread for Twitter, a carousel for LinkedIn.
3. Build an email notification: every new video triggers an email to your subscriber list within 1 hour of publishing.
4. Join and contribute to 5 niche communities (Reddit, Discord, Facebook Groups) where sharing your content is welcomed (not spammed).
5. Schedule promotion across 48 hours: immediate share (hour 0), teaser clip (hour 6), community post (hour 12), reminder (hour 24).
6. Track initial velocity: views in the first 48 hours correlated with social media promotion effort.

### Verification
- Auto-sharing set up for 3+ platforms
- Email notification trigger active
- 48-hour promotion schedule documented and executed for 4 videos
