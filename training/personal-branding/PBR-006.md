# PBR-006 — Write a One-Sentence Brand Pitch (Byline)

## Problema
Without a clear byline, visitors to your profile don't instantly understand who you are, what you do, and why they should follow you.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Niche defined (PBR-001)
- Target audience identified

### Steps
1. Use the formula: I help [specific audience] [achieve specific outcome] through [your unique method/content].
2. Write 10 variations of your byline.
3. Test each against the 3-second rule: does a stranger immediately understand your value in 3 seconds?
4. Eliminate jargon, buzzwords, and vague claims (no 'passionate about helping people succeed').
5. Select the strongest byline and test it with 5 people from your target audience.
6. Deploy the byline consistently across all platforms: social bios, website header, email signature.

### Verification
- 10 variations written
- Final byline passes 3-second test
- Deployed across all platforms consistently
