# PBR-025 — Research and Use TikTok Hashtags

## Problema
Using wrong hashtags on TikTok either buries your content in competition or sends it to the wrong audience.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Active TikTok account (PBR-024)

### Steps
1. Search your main topic keyword on TikTok's Discover page and note the suggested hashtags.
2. Categorize hashtags into 3 tiers: broad (100M+ views), medium (1M-100M views), and niche (under 1M views).
3. Create a hashtag library of 30 hashtags: 10 broad, 10 medium, 10 niche.
4. Use 3-5 hashtags per TikTok: 1 broad (for discoverability), 2 medium (for relevance), 1-2 niche (for targeting).
5. Track which hashtag combinations correlate with higher views over 2 weeks.
6. Update your hashtag library monthly as trends shift.

### Verification
- 30-hashtag library created with tier classification
- 3-5 hashtags used per post consistently
- Performance tracked by hashtag combination
