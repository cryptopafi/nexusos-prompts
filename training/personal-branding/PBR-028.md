# PBR-028 — Repurpose Content Across Platforms

## Problema
Creating unique content for every platform is unsustainable. Strategic repurposing maximizes reach while minimizing production effort.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Active presence on 2+ platforms
- Content creation workflow

### Steps
1. Designate a pillar platform: where you create long-form hero content (YouTube, blog, or podcast).
2. Map the repurposing chain: one YouTube video becomes 1 blog post, 3 Reels/TikToks, 5 Twitter threads, 2 LinkedIn posts, and 7 Stories.
3. Create a repurposing SOP: after each pillar piece, follow a checklist to extract all derivative content.
4. Adapt each piece for the platform: don't just cross-post. Adjust aspect ratio, caption style, hook format, and length.
5. Schedule repurposed content to publish throughout the week (1 pillar piece fuels a full week of content).
6. Track which repurposed formats drive the most engagement and traffic back to the pillar.
7. Optimize the chain: eliminate low-performing formats, double down on high-performing ones.

### Verification
- Repurposing chain documented (1 pillar = X derivative pieces)
- SOP checklist created and used for 4 weeks
- Performance tracked by format and platform
