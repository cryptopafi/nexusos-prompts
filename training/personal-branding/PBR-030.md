# PBR-030 — Use VidIQ and TubeBuddy for Keyword Research

## Problema
Manual keyword research is slow and imprecise. VidIQ and TubeBuddy provide data-driven insights that accelerate YouTube SEO.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- YouTube channel
- VidIQ and/or TubeBuddy installed

### Steps
1. Install VidIQ and TubeBuddy Chrome extensions (free tiers are sufficient to start).
2. Use VidIQ's Keyword Inspector: search your topic and check search volume, competition score, and overall keyword score.
3. Use TubeBuddy's Keyword Explorer: compare your keyword against related alternatives to find the best opportunity.
4. Check competitor videos: use VidIQ's video scorecard to see which keywords competitors rank for.
5. Identify keyword gaps: topics with high search volume but low competition where you can rank.
6. Export your top 20 keyword opportunities to your content calendar.
7. Track rankings: use VidIQ or TubeBuddy to monitor your position for target keywords weekly.

### Verification
- Both tools installed and tested
- 20 keyword opportunities identified with score data
- Weekly ranking tracking set up
