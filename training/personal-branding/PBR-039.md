# PBR-039 — Monetize a YouTube Channel (Multiple Revenue Streams)

## Problema
Relying solely on AdSense means you need millions of views to earn meaningfully. Diversified monetization multiplies revenue per viewer.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- YouTube channel with growing audience
- Monetization eligibility or path to it

### Steps
1. Revenue Stream 1 - AdSense: apply for the YouTube Partner Program (1,000 subscribers + 4,000 watch hours or 10M Shorts views).
2. Revenue Stream 2 - Sponsorships: use your media kit (PBR-012) to pitch brands when you reach 5K+ subscribers.
3. Revenue Stream 3 - Affiliate Marketing: add affiliate links for tools and products you mention in videos. Disclose clearly.
4. Revenue Stream 4 - Digital Products: create and sell a course, template, or guide related to your content.
5. Revenue Stream 5 - Channel Memberships: offer exclusive content, early access, or community perks.
6. Revenue Stream 6 - Super Chats and Super Thanks: enable for live streams and regular videos.
7. Track all revenue streams monthly: which generates the most per 1,000 views? Optimize for that metric.

### Verification
- At least 3 revenue streams active
- Revenue tracked monthly by stream
- Revenue per 1,000 views calculated and optimized
