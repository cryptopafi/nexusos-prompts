# PBR-020 — Set Up Facebook Business Manager for Instagram Ads

## Problema
Running Instagram ads without a properly configured Business Manager leads to tracking issues, wasted spend, and inability to scale.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Facebook account
- Instagram Business/Creator account

### Steps
1. Go to business.facebook.com and create a Business Manager account.
2. Add your Facebook Page and Instagram account to Business Manager.
3. Create an Ad Account within Business Manager (or claim an existing one).
4. Set up payment method: credit card or PayPal linked to the ad account.
5. Install the Facebook Pixel on your website for conversion tracking (PBR-022).
6. Create your first Custom Audience: website visitors, email list, or Instagram engagers.
7. Set up a Lookalike Audience based on your best Custom Audience (1% for precision, 5% for reach).
8. Assign roles: admin yourself, add any team members as advertisers.

### Verification
- Business Manager account active with Page and Instagram connected
- Pixel installed and firing
- First Custom and Lookalike Audiences created
