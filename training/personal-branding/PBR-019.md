# PBR-019 — Create Viral Carousel Posts

## Problema
Carousels generate the highest save rates on Instagram, making them the best format for building a loyal audience that returns to your content.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Instagram account
- Content niche defined (PBR-001)

### Steps
1. Choose a carousel type: educational (how-to), listicle (top 5), storytelling (before/after), or myth-busting.
2. Design slide 1 as a scroll-stopper: bold headline, contrasting colors, clear topic promise.
3. Structure slides 2-9 with one idea per slide: short text, large font, consistent visual template.
4. Slide 10 (final): strong CTA — save this for later, share with someone, follow for more.
5. Use Canva templates for consistent branding: same fonts, colors, and layout across all carousels.
6. Write a caption that adds context the slides don't cover. End with a question to drive comments.
7. Post 2-3 carousels per week and track save rate as the primary metric.

### Verification
- Carousel template created in Canva
- 2-3 carousels posted per week
- Save rate tracked and improving over 4 weeks
