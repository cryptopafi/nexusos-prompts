# EM-008 — Design Event Customer Experience

## Problema
Events that focus only on logistics miss the opportunity to design memorable experiences that build brand loyalty and generate word-of-mouth.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Event plan (EM-001)
- Brand guidelines if applicable

### Steps
1. Map the attendee journey: pre-event communication, arrival, registration, main experience, breaks/networking, departure, post-event follow-up.
2. For each journey stage, define: what the attendee does, what they see/hear/feel, and what brand impression they form.
3. Identify 2-3 'wow moments': unexpected delights that create memorable experiences (e.g., personalized welcome, surprise entertainment, premium swag).
4. Design the networking experience: structured introductions, conversation starters, designated networking zones.
5. Plan the digital experience: event app, live polling, social media wall, hashtag.
6. Create a feedback mechanism: live pulse surveys during the event and a comprehensive post-event survey.
7. Design the post-event experience: thank-you email within 24 hours, content recap, community invitation.

### Verification
- Full attendee journey mapped
- 2-3 wow moments designed
- Post-event follow-up sequence planned
