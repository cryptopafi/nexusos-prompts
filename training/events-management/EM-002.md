# EM-002 — Select and Evaluate an Event Venue

## Problema
Choosing a venue based on price alone leads to logistical nightmares, poor attendee experience, and brand misalignment.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Event requirements: audience size, format, date range
- Budget range

### Steps
1. Define non-negotiable venue requirements: capacity, location, accessibility, AV capability, catering options.
2. Research and shortlist 5 venues that meet the requirements.
3. Create a venue scoring matrix: location (weight 20%), capacity (15%), cost (20%), AV/tech (15%), ambiance (15%), flexibility (15%).
4. Visit or virtually tour the top 3 venues.
5. Check availability for your date range and get written quotes.
6. Evaluate cancellation and force majeure policies.
7. Select the venue and negotiate contract terms.

### Verification
- Scoring matrix completed for at least 3 venues
- Top venue visited or toured
- Contract terms reviewed before signing
