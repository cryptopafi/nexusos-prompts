# EM-007 — Manage Event Vendors and Hospitality

## Problema
Poor vendor management leads to late deliveries, wrong setups, and hospitality failures that directly impact attendee experience.

## Procedura
### Prerequisites
- Vendor list and contracts
- Event logistics checklist (EM-003)

### Steps
1. Create a vendor registry: company, contact person, phone, email, service provided, contract value, payment terms.
2. Confirm all vendor deliverables in writing at least 2 weeks before the event.
3. Schedule vendor load-in times with 30-minute buffers between vendors to avoid conflicts.
4. Create a hospitality plan: VIP handling, speaker green room, dietary requirements, special needs accommodations.
5. Prepare welcome packets for VIPs and speakers: schedule, WiFi info, contact numbers, venue map.
6. Conduct a vendor walkthrough at the venue 48 hours before the event.
7. Set up a day-of vendor check-in process with a designated team member as the vendor liaison.

### Verification
- Vendor registry complete with all contact details
- All deliverables confirmed in writing
- Hospitality plan covers VIPs, speakers, and special needs
